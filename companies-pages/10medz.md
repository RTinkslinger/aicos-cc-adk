### Business Evolution

### Initial Experiments (2022)

- Tried own packer and delivery - disturbed pharmacy workflow
- Launched dark store in April 2022

- Tested in 7-8 cities in Bundelkhand belt
  - Delivered 45 crore worth of medicines in 8 months
