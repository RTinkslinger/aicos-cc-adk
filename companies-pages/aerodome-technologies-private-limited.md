## Deck insights from Notion AI on 2025-05-07

Based on the attached presentation deck, AeroDome Technologies is developing India's first Low Earth Orbit (LEO) Position Navigation and Timing (PNT) satellite constellation.

**Founders:**
- Three IIT Madras graduates: Vibhor Jain, Lokesh Kabdal, and Anurag Patil - former leaders of Avishkar Hyperloop project

**Technology & Product:**
- Building LEO-based satellite constellation for precise positioning and secure navigation
- Developed RTK technology achieving 2-3cm accuracy vs standard GPS's 3-5m
- Created proprietary SDP (Standard Positioning Service Data Processing) algorithm reducing required satellites from 250 to 125

**Market Opportunity:**
- Global PNT market potential of $580 billion with 9 billion GNSS devices by 2033
- Key applications: Defense, maritime, automotive, timing services, agriculture, and construction

**Development Plan:**
- Phase 0 (18 months): Space-borne payload demonstration - Rs 14.2 Cr
- Phase 1 (12 months): City coverage with 4-6 satellites - $12M
- Phase 2 (15 months): Asia Pacific coverage with 50 satellites - $40-45M
- Phase 3 (15 months): Global coverage with 125 satellites - $70-80M

**Current Round:**
- Raising Rs 14.2 Cr for space-borne demonstration
