### 2. Business Overview

AskMyGuru is building an AI-powered spiritual tech platform focused on Vedic knowledge and practices. The product features:

- AI-powered guru chatbot available 24x7 for free conversations

- Trained on Vedic data, Sanskrit, and guru voice patterns

- Multi-lingual support with both chat and audio capabilities

- Personalized matchmaking between seekers and resolutions

### 3. Current State

The product appears to be under development with:

- Phase 1 launch planned focusing on Vedic Astrology

- Technology architecture defined including LLM and chat interface

- Initial guru partnerships being established (mentioned names like Siddharth Banerjee, Arun Pandit)

No clear indication of current revenue or active users in the deck.

### 4. Market Opportunity

- Target market: 1.1 billion Hindus in India, with 73% believing in spirituality

- Astrology alone is cited as a $58.56 billion industry with 10.4% CAGR

- Current gap: Only ~5% of market addressed by existing players

- Competitive advantage: First platform attempting to aggregate multiple Vedic offerings versus vertical-only players
