### Company Snapshot

Problem & solution
Current tax filing journey is friction-prone, manual, and error‑prone with late submissions and notices. AXO proposes proactive tax planning, integrated filing, and year‑round wealth optimisation, using automated data ingestion from AIS/Accounts Aggregator (AA)/eRI, continuous monitoring, and AI‑driven planning to turn tax compliance into a wealth management asset.

Ideal customer profile and pricing
Target segments and personas
Early professional: simple filing; basic 80C deductions; MF/NPS suggestions; first‑time tax education.
Mid‑career professional: regime selection; HRA/LTA, 80C/D; MF SIPs, NPS for tax saving; proactive planning.
Senior professional: capital gains, ESOP/RSU, property income; quarterly advance tax; active portfolio management.

### (2) Product and Technology

Product core and tech stack
End‑to‑end tax lifecycle: data ingestion from AIS/AA/eRI, automatic data reconciliation, planning, filing, and tax‑optimisation with ongoing monitoring; embedded AI chatbot for tax/finance questions; 1‑click data fetch from brokers; built‑in wealth context.
Tech stack highlights
Vertical AI stack with a fine‑tuned reasoning layer; multi‑agent orchestration; deterministic tax calculations to avoid probabilistic errors; data inputs from AIS, AA, eRI.

Unique IP and differentiators
[Inference] No explicit patents or regulatory certifications stated.
Differentiators include: continuous year‑round planning and optimisation, 100% data completeness before filing, integrated wealth actions (NPS, ELSS, tax‑loss harvesting), and ecosystem integrations (brokers, HRMS, CA networks).

Implementation complexity / switching costs
High due to deep integrations (AIS/AA/eRI), automated reconciliation, and year‑round engagement; switching requires migrating data and trust in AI‑driven advice.
