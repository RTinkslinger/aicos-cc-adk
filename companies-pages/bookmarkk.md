## Analysis by dev - 2026-03-10

### Company Snapshot

Problem & solution
Reading lacks daily feedback; progress is invisible, hurting motivation. Existing tools track books or time, not the habit. Bookmarkk positions as the “Strava for books” with AI-driven quizzes, social features, and an identity/insights layer to turn reading into a measurable habit.

Ideal customer profile and pricing
Pricing tested: $5 per month (24 paid users in initial test). Target segments implied: high‑intent readers; book clubs; colleges; online communities; creator‑led ecosystems.

Status of product & traction highlight
Live pilot with paid users: 24 at $5/mo; 21/24 renewed for a second month; ~30% of users read 5+ days/week.
