- Connor (founder we are underwriting) is Spiky = 1 - rapid career trajectory from intern to Eng to PM to Growth; directly working with CEO! Connor has given founding a shot before so carries scar tissue & learnings.

- Founders are AI native - built Loom’s AI feature which has created a 10x experience (for me, as a customer)

- Signal: Loom CoFo writing his largest Angel cheque to ex-Loom team & actively spending time every week with them; doesn’t need to - has made FU money, they are clearly top performers.

Why NOT $300K?

- Too many assumptions: Underestimating the amount of configuration & customization needed to make even dictation work - they seem to want to offload dictation to APIs, this CANNOT be the case - since dictation accuracy is determined by memory, context & environment (which app/surface)

- Market/GTM risk:  Worried that their landing wedge is dictation - didn’t hear anything about actions - therefore, Wispr Flow is the competition. Even Joe alluded to this in his BRC - said they will need to find some kernel of insight to stand out.

- GTM execution? They needed to have the Superhuman like engine firing for them almost 45 days back - where is the Community? Webinars? Waitlist virality?

- Main CoFo we are underwriting is not Desi - therefore, need to exercise caution on over-reading (same with the critical BRC we got from Joe at Loom)

- Shipping velocity? Am I missing something - they are supposed to be high velocity & pace; cannot find comfort in this given most YC batch co’s launch well before Demo Day.

- Fear that founder dynamics will break somewhere - Joe rightly pointed out that Connor is Spiky >1 whereas Rajiv has a lot of the “Design leader” traits which don’t always help in 0-1 

- Even if we were at the start of Fund II, I would constraint cheque size to $150K v/s $300K given all the above considerations

Why NOT $0K?

- Thesis resonates: Contrary to Joe & Cash’s POV - I think this is NOT a “dictations to actions” product - this is the Limitless 1.0 agent running in the background learning everything about you AND the use of voice to be used for editing the “first draft” created by Caddy 

- Do we need to do ≥ 1 cheque per YC batch? I think yes - IFF we can find a good team and NOT FOMO into the investment. 

- Amongst YC F25 co’s interacted with - BEST pedigree team (comp. v/s AnswerThis, Mantle, Questom, Kestrel etc)

- There is no FOMO involved in this investment process - we’ve taken our time & run the full playbook i.e. 1PE, 2PE and deep BRC

- Therefore, have comfort in being able to underwrite these founders v/s other ones met in this batch

What would make me change my mind on $150K? (everything below needs time):
