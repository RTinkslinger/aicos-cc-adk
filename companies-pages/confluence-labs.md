Confluence Labs (YC W26) | confluence.sh

What they do
AI research lab building foundation models optimised for learning efficiency in data-sparse domains — drug design, materials science, hardware engineering, physics discovery. Their thesis: modern ML only excels where you can collect massive training data. In domains where data is expensive or slow to generate (physical experiments), current AI fails. Confluence's approach: combine LLMs with discrete program synthesis to maximise what you can learn from a small number of experiments.

Two-track strategy:

1. Hypothesis generation — AI that designs maximally informative experiments so each one teaches you the most

1. Data-efficient modelling — program synthesis + LLMs to make predictions with far fewer data points

Technical milestone


Came out of stealth with 97.9% on ARC-AGI-2 at $11.77/task — SOTA at launch (Feb 2026). ARC-AGI-2 is considered one of the hardest AI benchmarks in the world (tests novel reasoning from just a few examples). Approaching 100% on what was previously considered unsolvable. Open-sourced the solver.

Backed by YC and Paul Graham personally.
