## Intro conversation with Founder on 13 Dec 2024

### Attendees from DeVC: @Anonymous 

### Notes:

Cookr
Wed, 13 Nov 24
About the founders
• Prabhu is the cofounder and CEO
• Has a background in technology, worked at Microsoft for many years
• Built Xbox consumer business and Xbox Live platform
• This is his second startup, first one was in healthcare but didn’t succeed due to COVID
• Other cofounders have backgrounds in food, technology, and packaging
What is the business
• Category marketplace for clean food and food products
• Connects consumers with home kitchens for food delivery
• Focuses on health, organic, and gourmet food products
• Aims to provide home-cooked style meals through technology
Scale of the business
• Year 1 (FY21) - 96L GMV, spent around 10 cr
• Year 2 (FY22) - 9.4cr GMV, total burn 16-17cr
• Year 3 (FY23 target) - 20cr+ GMV, 30% take rate so Net Revenue 46-47%, -45% Net EBITDA, Burn 68L/month
• Current monthly sales around 1.3 crores
Operations
• Initially had their own fleet
• Now moved to 3rd party fleets (Porter, Dunzo, Shadowfax)
• Facing challenges with Dunzo exiting the market and Zomato Blinkit pulling out
• Building their own 3PL now
• 52,000 chefs registered and waiting to be onboarded
• 1300-1400 active chefs currently
Consumer Insights
• Core consumers: 28-45 age group working couples, 65+ senior citizens
• Offers subscription meal services (30-day lunch/dinner plans)
• Provides party meals for small gatherings (15-20 guests)
• Menu focuses on home-style food not typically available in restaurants
• Allows customization of meals
What worked through the years
• Claims to have product-market fit:
    ◦ 73% orders from repeat customers
    ◦ 27% from new customers daily
    ◦ 6-month cohort retention at 35-37%
• Contribution margin positive for the last 4-5 months
• Average Order Value (AOV) around 339 rupees in September
Fund Raise
• 2.6mn raised so far
    ◦ First round: Family and friends - employees of Amazon and Microsoft
    ◦ Second round: Family offices
• Currently raising 2mn
    ◦ 500k lead at 18mn pre-money valuation - Soonicorn
    ◦ Another at 500k - Four Pursuits
    ◦ And at 250k - unnamed investor
• Brand Capital (VC arm of Times of India) has invested for digital and print media promotion
Challenges and Future Plans
• Main challenge: delivering food to end customers on time
• Plans to expand both vertically and horizontally
• Aiming to be EBITDA positive in the next 12 months
• Needs 5x current volume (6-7 crores monthly sales) to reach EBITDA positive
• Expanding to new cities like Hyderabad and Pune
• Focusing on subscription meals and party catering as growth areas
Technology and Differentiation
• Developed own CRM to manage supply flow
• Uses semantic search and large language models for personalized recommendations
• Guarantees fresh food and allows customization
• Playbook for launching in new cities, takes about 6 months to cover a city
