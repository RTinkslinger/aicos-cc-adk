## Note from founder dated Oct ‘25

I’m Himanshu, Co-Founder at Credeau, an AI-powered digital lending infrastructure company based in Noida. We’re currently bootstrapped, backed by a 18-member team across product, data science, technology, and growth.

At Credeau, we’re building a next-gen Digital Lending solutions, powered by an AI-agent Business Rule Engine (BRE) which is backed by our propertieary intelligent APIs.

Our goal is to digitise every lending decision through a cloud-agnostic, on-premise, self-serve decisioning platform with intelligent data APIs (bureau, mobile, and bank).

What we’ve achieved so far:

- Digitised multiple small NBFCs, scaling them from ₹20 Cr/month to ₹500 Cr/month disbursals within 3–6 months.

- Built an on-premise, self-serve BRE that enables lenders to integrate and start using our APIs within hours.

- Currently integrating our decisioning system with one of the largest banks in India.

Here's a summary of the Credeau deck:

Company Overview

Credeau is a credit risk management solution provider helping digital lenders scale operations from day one with AI-driven decisioning infrastructure.

The Problem

New NBFCs in India take 12-18 months to stabilize credit losses due to evolving fraud, static scorecards, and costly experimentation—losing significant time and capital.

Solution

ML/AI-driven Business Rule Engine (BRE) with three core modules:

- Underwriting Models: Bureau-based risk segmentation, SMS/bank intelligence, alternate data

- Anti-Fraud Engine: Customer deduplication, SDK-based fraud detection, network triangulation

- Collections & CRM: Prioritization scores, early warning triggers, behavioral scorecards

Market & Traction

- TAM: ₹560 Cr in India targeting ~2,000 NBFCs

- Current: 20 live clients, ₹6.6 Cr ARR, ₹55L monthly revenue

- Growth: Since incorporation (May 2024), scaled from 2 to 20 clients and 2 to 20 employees

Financial Model

- Pricing: Fixed (₹50K-₹2L/month) + variable API costs (₹1-10/call)

- Margins: 80% gross, 55% contribution, 45% net

- ARPU: ~₹2.7L/month per client

24-Month Outlook

- 12 months: 50 clients, ₹20 Cr ARR

- 24 months: 100 clients, ₹50 Cr ARR

Key Differentiators

- Tested go-live strategies built-in (no experimentation needed)

- Proprietary fraud intelligence database

- On-premise deployment option

- Competitive pricing for early-stage lenders

- Expanding to banks and SEA markets (Vietnam, Philippines, Mexico)

Team

Founders with 14+ years (Rajat Jain - data science, ex-Home Credit, FinBox) and 8.5+ years (Himanshu Chauhan - fintech growth, IIM Calcutta) in credit risk and fintech.
