## Monthly Progress: July

Hello, early believers, welcome to our latest update! 👋

We rolled out many improvements to the product following beta users’ feedback + exciting updates on the launch content.

More details below ⬇️

### 🛠️ Product

Sleep Sensing

We’ve made great progress on our sleep sensor development. Our sensor’s heart rate accuracy now closely correlates with the gold standard data [ECG] (0.86 on the correlation scale; 1.0 = perfect).

Orange Graph - Dreamspan
Blue Graph - Polar ECG

App Updates

Following beta users’ feedback, we rolled out multiple app features.

Wearable Integration + Biomarker Improvements: Users can now connect their existing wearables to see what biomarkers have improved since they started using our product.

Edit Sleep Schedule: Users can now easily edit their sleep schedule without the need to delete and re-create it.

Dynamic Temperature UI: Users can now see the dynamic temperature throughout the night directly on the Schedule tab for a better understanding of how the temperature will change throughout the night.
