## Analysis by dev - 2026-03-14

### Company Snapshot

Problem & solution: After-school learning in India is large but inefficient due to low personalization and costly 1:1 tutoring; Eduro offers an AI-powered tutor with NCERT-aligned scaffolding and feedback to provide context-relevant, time-efficient revision.

Ideal customer profile and pricing: Target CBSE students, Class 9+; pricing band ₹200-₹600 per month; CAC per activation ₹7; 30%+ of students already paying for offline tutors (average ~₹25k/year), signaling a credible path from complement to substitute at lower cost.

Status of product & traction highlight: 15K+ signups via micro-influencers; 300+ daily activated users (200 new, 100 repeat); 7 CAC per activation; retention: D1-2 25%, W2 15%, long-term ~10% after W4; repeat user frequency ~3 sessions/week; average active session ~13 minutes; 200+ qualitative delight signals; 15+ organic paid upgrades indicating willingness to pay.
