Insurance: Bigg Boss FinTech version:

Sounds like a clickbait? No. There has been an ongoing tussle between the IRDAI (Governing body of Insurance) and the Insurers in India.

Insurer's perspective: https://www.newindianexpress.com/business/2024/Sep/07/council-may-cut-gst-on-health-insurance-to-5-per-cent

IRDAI's perspective: https://www.cnbctv18.com/personal-finance/irdai-calls-affordable-health-insurance-premiums-price-rise-life-19465623.htm/amp

Since the last few weeks, the demand to lower down the GST for health and life insurance has been a epicenter for the Insurers, accusing with the anecdote that due to the increased GST, the prices go up, decreasing the conversion rate of the users for the product.

Meanwhile IRDAI has also pushed back, asking the insurers to lower down their prices irrespective of the GST. (Something that only FinLead can do)

Conclusion: The GST council looks keen on decreasing the GST from 18% to 5% for Health Insurance and Life Insurance. This can be one of the biggest boosts in the Insurance industry due to the price consciousness of the users. The Eureka moment for Insurance to have more space in the premium margins and drive better penetration in India.
