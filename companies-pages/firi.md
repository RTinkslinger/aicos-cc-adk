Here's a summary of the Firi deck:

Firi — High-quality BPC (beauty & personal care) products, personalized for you, delivered in minutes.

Founders

- Vivek — Ex-Uber (launched 5 cities, built Rentals globally), Ex-Flipkart (set up FK Labs, led Web3 charter onboarding 3.5M users). Built an AI search tool for BPC from Jul '24–Nov '25 before starting Firi.

- Karishma — Ex-Uber, Ex-Swvl (global growth, built CX function), co-founded Salad (2022–23). Also worked on the AI BPC search tool before Firi.

Problem

- Beauty product pages get 1B+ eyeballs/month, yet 97% of users rely on reviews, 65% regret purchases, and 71% YoY growth in beauty conversations on Reddit/YouTube.

- Brands make inflated claims, platforms reinforce them and don't allow returns, regulations lag behind, and social media doesn't optimize for information integrity. The onus of finding the right product falls entirely on the consumer.

Target User

- Heavy BPC buyers (top 1% spenders in India) for whom skincare = self-care and identity. ACV grows 4.5x by year 5 and 8.5x by year 10. 33% of their shelves contain products not yet sold in India; 38% contain Rx/OTC pharma-grade products.

- SAM: $2.2–2.4B today, projected $5.4B by 2030. ~5.6M users, LTV ~$4,175.

Solution

1. AI-powered product graph — 22 LLM agents process ~48M reviews from Reddit & YouTube, analyze ingredients, skin/hair types, and detect fake reviews. 4,295 products mapped across 98 attributes and 13.4M use-case × user-type permutations.

1. Review-curated assortment — Only stock the highest-quality products backed by real review data and ingredient science.

1. Quick delivery — Avg 18 mins, ~3 km catchment per dark store, 36 stores planned for Delhi NCR.

Moats / Vision

- Unbeatable assortment: Current, high-quality, across price points + exclusive supply (e.g. La Roche-Posay only on Firi in quick commerce, 33% baskets had LRP). Rx & OTC on one platform. Authentic supply direct from brands (vs. Blinkit/Zepto sourcing from random distributors).

- Delivery speed: CM1 breakeven at ~99 OPD; 12K SKUs incl. long-tail.

- Personalization: Netflix-style "For You" home feed — a first for BPC e-commerce.

Pilot Metrics (Jan '26, 308 SKUs)

- ₹3,184 L30 AOV (6–10x typical q-commerce)

- ~50% weekly session retention

- 32% L30 repeat users, 23% CWGR

- 18% sessions → orders

- 65% of SKUs sold at least once

- 8.83/10 avg rating on products bought

- 24% men buyers

- ₹1,462 8-week CAC (incl ₹30K brand spend)

- 25% pilot take rate (target 35%)

Ask: Raising $3M to expand inventory, grow the team, and reach CM1 breakeven in ~7 months.
