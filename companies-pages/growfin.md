# Growfin

## Company Overview

Growfin is an AR (Accounts Receivable) automation platform focused on order-to-cash workflows — specifically cash application/reconciliation, collections management, and invoice presentment. Sits in the finance operations layer of the CFO stack, not core accounting/GL. Key integrations with NetSuite and Zuora (Zora), through which it also has a strategic investment.

## Founder

**Arvind Gopalan** — CEO & Co-founder. Also spoken with Pranay (likely co-founder or early team). Building a partner-led GTM through ERP/billing platform resellers.
