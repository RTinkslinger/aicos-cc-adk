**Problem**

- Real estate rental market stuck in “classifieds 1.0”

- High friction rental process for both owners & tenants

- Large security deposits (4-6 months) blocking access

- No standardized rental experience

**Product**

- Full-stack rental marketplace for premium apartments (40K+ rent)

- For owners:

  - “Helium Instant”: Guaranteed rent starts in 10 days
  - Takes properties exclusively
  - Charges 5% fee from owners
  - Maintains full security deposit structure

- For tenants:

  - Only 1-month security deposit required
  - Rest covered through bank overdraft facility
  - Digital lockbox for instant viewings
  - Standardized move-in/move-out process

- **Property Management Layer**: A marketplace with an added property management layer to ensure quality control over supply.

- **Tech-First Approach**: Digital lockboxes for self-viewing, on-ground team for property verification.

**Process**:

- **Owner’s Process**: Owners input property details; Helium Homes provides a price range and schedules an inspection. After inspection, the final price is quoted, and owners receive a minimum deposit upon rent commitment.

- **Tenant’s Process**: Tenants use an app to view listings (photos, video walk-throughs), schedule visits, and can visit properties independently or with a team member

**Market Strategy**:

- **Demand and Supply Hooks**:

  - **Supply side**: Instant rent payment guarantees.
  - **Demand side**: One-month security deposit.

- Targeting tenants with a **higher household income (INR 40L+)** and properties with rents starting at **INR 40K/month**.

**Go-to-market**

- Focus on gated communities & premium properties first

- Target conversion: 8% of approached owners

- Initial focus on properties that typically rent within 15-20 days

- Planning city-by-city expansion starting with micro-markets

**Monetization & Flexibility**:

- **Revenue Streams**: The business will earn through owner fees (with a commission), tenant brokerage, and exit fees. The model's sensitivity lies in the owner fee, which can vary based on property demand.

- **Challenges with Pricing**: There is concern over landlords being unwilling to accept reduced fees or guarantees. The critics suggest the model could scale faster if it charged landlords zero fees upfront and instead focused on controlling supply.

**Traction**

- Running pilot

- Early validation on owner acceptance of delayed deposit payments

- Limited details shared on current scale

**Competitors**:

- **Key competitors**: Howzeezy, IvyHomes, and Truva (focused on buy/sell, not rental).

- Unlike these competitors, Helium Homes guarantees instant rentals and a higher degree of quality control.

**Critique & Concerns**:

- **Linear Business Model**: The critics highlight that starting with high-demand properties and controlling supply seems like a linear approach, raising concerns about scalability.

- **Demand & Supply**: The business relies on offering a one-month security deposit to attract tenants, which could potentially drive the flywheel of supply-demand growth. However, questions arise around the effectiveness of this model, especially with landlords reluctant to accept lower fees or guarantees.
