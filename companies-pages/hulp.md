## Company Overview

HULP is building an AI + Human-led Concierge - personal assistance for modern families. Main founder is Tarun Mathur (CBO and Cofo elevated at PolicyBazaar - one of the core early architects of PB). Yashish is likely doing an outsized check as part of F&F round.

Target: Parents with two kids not living with their own parents (dual-income households with Rs 70L+ income). Subscription model at Rs 3,000-5,000/month.

Architecture: One PA managing 12 families initially, scaling to 25-27 families by year three. PA as front-end with specialized desks (travel, maid coordination, government forms) in backend.

## Meeting Notes (17 Feb - Gurgaon Office) - 2026-02-26

TARUN'S BACKGROUND & POLICY BAZAAR JOURNEY:
- 13 years in ecosystem, started with comparison websites and SEO/SEM
- Built revenue models through Google AdSense, then booking engines
- Set up Affle's partnership platform in India - worked with 500+ brands creating performance marketing and partnership models

POLICY BAZAAR ACHIEVEMENTS:
- Term insurance: Increased commissions from Rs 700 to Rs 3,400 per transaction in 1.5 years
- Investment products: Built high-IRR products (15-18%) competing with mutual funds
- Motor insurance: Created CURE model, became contribution positive
- Health insurance: Developed "one crore health person" product with 15% additional premium
- Key insight: One RM per customer dramatically improves NPS/CSAT and purchase control
- Health persistency increased from 70-72% to current levels through dedicated RM
- Cross-sell ratios improved from 1.4-1.5x to 2.6x transactions, 4x revenue

HULP'S CORE CONCEPT & TARGET MARKET:
- Personal assistance for modern families - "Can we take away all the pain in your life?"
- Target: Parents with two kids not living with their own parents (dual-income households with Rs 70L+ income)
- Subscription model at Rs 3,000-5,000/month
- One PA managing 12 families initially, scaling to 25-27 families by year three
- Architecture: PA as front-end with specialized desks (travel, maid coordination, government forms) in backend

THREE CORE PRODUCT PILLARS FOR DAILY USAGE:
1. Meal management: Weekly menu planning with nutritional analysis, ingredient checking and procurement coordination, cook coordination and instruction delivery
2. Service coordination: Home maintenance and repairs (60% of calls happen same morning), asset management across multiple properties
3. Daily engagement: Morning check-ins "What can I do for you today?", must achieve 7+ monthly touchpoints for retention

DESIGN CHOICES & OPERATIONAL MODEL:
- Human-in-the-loop approach over pure AI: "Complete AI agent doesn't give me comfort it will get done"
- AI enables PAs but human layer provides empathy and complex task handling
- Dedicated phone number per customer for OTP handling and call screening
- Specialized desk structure inspired by Policy Bazaar's operational model
- 18-month timeline to transition more tasks to AI automation

FUNDING & BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT:
- Raising $2M from F&F
- Seeking portfolio company partnerships for service provider ecosystem
- 6-8 month timeline to MVP, planning larger round by December/January
- Focus on NCR initially with 5 PAs serving 200 customers in first phase

COMPETITIVE POSITIONING & MARKET VALIDATION:
- Differentiation from Pinch, Crew, other concierge services:
  - Lower price point (Rs 5K vs Rs 15-18K) to avoid luxury buying segment
  - Household memory and context building over time
  - Proactive daily engagement vs reactive task completion
- Consumer research: 340 people interviewed, form-filling identified as universal pain point
- Retention challenge: PA turnover could disrupt customer relationships

KEY STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS FROM MEETING:
- Acquisition PMF priority: Start with call handling as lowest common denominator
- Universal pain point with immediate time-to-value
- Can demonstrate capability within 24 hours
- Task replacement focus over complex orchestration initially
- Build household memory and context layer through human interactions first
- Avoid "dreaming of AI use cases" - solve real consumer problems with current technology
- Architecture will naturally evolve toward verticalized desk setup with RM efficiency as key metric
