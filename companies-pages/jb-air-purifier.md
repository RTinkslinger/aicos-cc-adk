### Company Background & Previous Venture

Shiv previously ran an offline affiliate program for retail stores, restaurants, and hotels from 2021-2024. The business worked with 1,500+ outlets and created a QR code-based WhatsApp bot system that tracked customer referrals without requiring an app.    The company was backed by founders of Swiggy, Razorpay, and Mama Earth, and received an acqui-hire offer from Razorpay that was declined.  The business was ultimately wound down through a trust structure where investors received equity in the new company.  

**Key learnings:** The previous venture validated that word-of-mouth drives businesses and that everyday influencers (not just celebrities) are highly effective for product adoption. This insight is being applied to the air purifier GTM strategy.    

### Product Overview & Differentiation

JB manufactures air purifiers with significantly better specifications than competitors while maintaining 50% lower pricing on MSRP.  Key differentiators include:

- Larger motors: 42W for 400 sq ft rooms vs industry standard 18-25W; 55W for 800 sq ft rooms vs standard 40-42W 

- Larger filters than all competitors in each size category 

- Only 20-22 total parts vs Xiaomi's 190 parts, using monocoque chassis and clamshell design  

- Single PCB running on ESP32 vs competitors' 4 separate PCBs 

- 52 decibels at turbo max vs Dyson's 57 decibels 

- Replacement filters priced at ₹500-550 vs industry standard ₹2,500-5,000, with 60% margins maintained  

The simplified design with fewer parts reduces manufacturing costs by 25-30% compared to legacy designs from established players. 

### Manufacturing Strategy

Currently import-only business working with Poco, one of the world's largest air purifier OEMs in China, which manufactures for Midea, Haier, IFB, and Coway.   All engineering and mold design is proprietary, using industry-leading off-the-shelf components. 

**Future plans:** Target 100,000-150,000 units in first 12 months, then set up domestic manufacturing in Tamil Nadu for next 20-25 million units over 10 years.  Plant would be fully automated with Mika bots, capable of 3,600-7,200 units per day vs current OEM capacity of 800-1,600 units per day.   Also exploring contract manufacturing with Eyelure India for certain product lines.   

### Business Model & Margins

Current margins: 12-14% CM3 on purifiers, 60% on replacement filters.  These margins are intentionally lower during import phase to drive adoption through value proposition. Domestic manufacturing will increase margins to 50%. 

Product range includes 6 sizes up to 2,000 square feet, with higher margins on larger units.   Planning house-of-brands approach with snap-on cases (cloth, leatherette, Star Wars, Disney co-branded themes) and multiple brand tiers from ₹4,990 to ₹49,990 to capture 75-80% of market.    

### Go-To-Market Strategy

Multi-channel approach focused on mimetic desire and influencer nodes:

- Sub-30 minute delivery through Zepto across top 35-40 PIN codes; same-day delivery in top 20 cities   

- B2B partnerships: Target purifier of choice at 3.5+ star hotels with "Breathe easy, the air inside is purified by JB" stickers as new quality signal  

- AQI widget integrations on weather platforms to build brand association 

- Influencer strategy leveraging everyday tech enthusiasts, doctors, VCs, and wellness influencers rather than celebrity endorsements  

- Reviews-focused approach, confident in winning comparisons due to superior specs 

- Micro-market segmentation: treating every PIN code as different consumer segment
