<u>**Business Model**</u>

Mannjal functions as a co-lending platform between mid to large NBFCs and very small regional NBFCs or BC partners.

<u>**Call notes**</u>

**Business**

- Pivoted from SCF (2 types of anchors - digital (positive WC) + traditional (negative WC)]. Write offs was very high in digital and traditional was in negative WC. BBB market had players like progcap, ugro already which made differentiation difficult

- Moved focus to sub 50 lakhs segment lending. Replace B2B guys with lenders

- Fintechs have not made good NBFC > make good NBFCs great fintechs

- Go to sub GST market (MFI+) - where there is requirement of assessed income

- **Connect 100 crs entity (BCs + NBFCs) to 1000 crs+ entity (samunnati, Kinara, electronica)**

- Partner does origination + servicing

- Upgrading NGOs and trusts from MFIs to MicroLaps

- Right now not integrating with LOS

- **Break policies of large lenders for smaller guys and then process end to end**

- Tech platform for small NBFCs/BCs (80-100 crs AUM). Don’t have access to liquidity or tech/processes in place to integrate with larger lenders

**Traction**

- Went live Sept-23 live - first quarter did everything manually to learn everything (1 cr disb only)

- **Deployed product in May-24**

- **OND24 - 170 crs disbursement (cumulative 350 crs+)**

- AUM management and collections is still in WIP

- Take rate - 25 bps (lowest), today (50-70 bps)

- Revenue is at 4-5 lakhs

**Capital**

- Raising Pre-Series A round of 25 crs

- Raised ~$1 mn. in 2023 from Sparrow, All In and Gemba

### **Company Overview - Mannjal**

- Name means “turmeric” in Tamil - represents being a catalyst for positive change

- Focus: Credit enablement network for Bharat

- Target segment: Above MFI but below small enterprise segment

- Current status:

  - Live since September 2023
  - 48 engaged lenders (16 live on platform)
  - 14 active lending pipes
  - Rs 55 crores monthly disbursement (up from Rs 5 crores in Jan 2024)

### **Business Model & Operations**

- Revenue model:

  - 50-75 bps on processing fee (from smaller lenders)
  - Not yet charging larger lenders
  - Future potential to charge up to 1.5% of spread

- Platform handles:

  - Origination to disbursement
  - Document collection
  - Credit assessment
  - Collaboration between lenders

- Not yet handling post-disbursement monitoring/collections

### **Current Traction**

- Key partnerships:

  - Samriddhi accounts for 82-83% of disbursals
  - Choice NBFC contributes 10-12%
  - 11 originators and 5 lenders live on platform
  - 5-6 new originators onboarded in past 2 months

### **Fundraising Status**

- Raising $3M round:

  - $2M committed from B Capital (lead)
  - $500-550K from existing investors
  - Bhavesh Gupta participating as angel
  - ~$400K allocation remaining

- Previous round: $1.1m led by Sparrow Capital, Gemba, and Allin
