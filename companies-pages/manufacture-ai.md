**Company Overview:**

- **Manufacture AI** automates the **generation of SOPs** for **hardware manufacturers**, addressing inefficiencies in the current process of creating complex documents manually. The AI solution speeds up **SOP generation** and integrates **defect management** and **test procedure optimization**.

**Customer Insights & Hypothesis Evolution:**

- **Hypothesis**: The team’s initial hypothesis was that **hardware manufacturers** would pay for an AI solution to automate **SOP creation**. Customer feedback from early MVPs validated demand, as it **reduced time** spent on documentation.

- The product evolved from **SOP generation** to include **defect tracking** and **inventory management**, reflecting deeper customer needs.

### 

### Product Solution & Technology

- AI-powered platform generating SOPs from CAD files and manufacturing data

- Current MVP achieves 65% accuracy in SOP generation

- Features:

  - Generates full release package including bill of materials
  - Human-in-loop learning process for improvements
  - Change control system with memory storage
  - Integration with AWS tech stack (moved from Vercel for security)

**Challenges and Solutions:**

- Early products faced **security issues**; they had to **switch from Vercel to AWS** to address enterprise-grade security concerns (needed for hardware manufacturers).

- **Accuracy of SOPs** was a challenge, requiring continuous **model refinement**. The solution now uses **iterative learning**, where users can modify outputs, enhancing the **AI system’s efficiency** over time.

### Market & Customer Traction

- Initial customer pipeline: 15 potential customers

- Target customers: Hardware companies with:

  - Semi-manual to manual processes
  - High complexity hardware
  - Rapid iteration needs

- Early adopters include PCB suppliers and aerospace contractors

- Expanding to include defense and ITAR compliance
