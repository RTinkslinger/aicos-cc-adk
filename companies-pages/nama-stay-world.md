## Deck Notes — February 25, 2026

The Entourage Layer for Complex Countries — making VIP travel concierge accessible to mid/upper-middle class international travellers visiting India, Egypt, Morocco, Peru, Colombia.

### Problem

50%+ leisure travellers report being scammed/overcharged in India, Egypt, Morocco. Planning = 25+ hours across Reddit/IG/YouTube/blogs, 6-8 apps to book. No single accountable on-ground companion. Existing: DIY chaos, high-end advisors (Kimkim $2,500+ remote only), or package tours (rigid, inauthentic).

### Solution

1) In-person Buddy: Regional veteran travels with you ($50-100/day) OR virtual WhatsApp support ($10/day). Handles discovery, safety, logistics, bargaining. 2) AI-powered app: Learns from buddy trip data. Self-serve discovery, itineraries, bookings, SOS triage. Over time app handles bulk, human reserved for premium. Managed marketplace, not listing platform. India broken into tight clusters each owned by a senior buddy.

### Moat — Ex-Military Buddies

100,000+ retired JCOs and Jawans (age 35-50). Strong community clout, operational instincts, high trainability, deep local knowledge. Creates proprietary geodata graphs that AI itineraries cannot replicate.

### Market

India alone: $8.6B opportunity. 9.95M FTAs in 2024. 2.68M leisure travellers from target geos. Avg spend $3,382/visitor (higher than Japan). Global play: replicates across complex destinations where tourism arbitrage >$50B.

### Business Model

~20% blended take rate on $5,000 avg trip. Revenue: stays (7%), experiences (20%), conveyance (25%), concierge fee (40%), food (30%), shopping (30%), flights (7%). Path to $100M: 20,000 trips/year, ~833 buddies. Each buddy: 2 trips/month, $120K annual revenue, ~$525/month net at 50% occupancy.

### Traction

Pilots: Aug 2025 (Hexaware corporate), Nov 2025 (B2C via Reddit), Jan 2026 (B2C direct).

### GTM

Trust-first ($0 CAC): Reddit, Instagram storytelling, ChatGPT recs, Couchsurfing/GetYourGuide/Airbnb Exp. Partners: micro-influencers, intl universities, boutique agencies, corporates, Indian diplomats in Europe. Paid: $150-200 CAC (retargeting + Google).

### Fundraise

Raising INR 6 Cr seed (~2 Cr committed). Takes to 200 trips in 16 months, $1M revenue. Apr 26: 30 pilots (W/N Europe). Dec 26: 50 E2E + app launch (+N America). Apr 27: 100 trips. Dec 27: 200 trips (+S America). Cost: 50% team, 30% GTM, 10% product, 10% admin.

### Team

Medha Meenal (CEO) — 9 yrs ops, two 0-1 journeys, 30 countries, SRCC + ISB. Lt Gen VK Mishra Retd (COO) — 4 decades Indian Army, PVSM + AVSM. Ishan Sinha (Marketing) — 4 yrs, WWF-India, Pawfectly (acq by Licious), MICA.

### Competitive Positioning

vs Kimkim ($2,500+ remote): $50-80/day on-ground. vs WithLocals/Airbnb Exp (2-4hr): Full-trip companion. vs GetYourGuide/Viator (standalone): Companion travels WITH you. vs Reddit (3-24hr wait): Vetted ex-military <10 min response. Parallel: Fora Travel — except started on-ground first.

Nama Stay World is building an “entourage layer” for ordinary travelers visiting “complex” countries (starting with India) where trust, safety, and execution are hard.

Key points:

- Problem: Travelers spend 25+ hours researching across fragmented sources and use 6–8 apps to book pieces of a trip, but still have no accountable on-ground help. In places like India/Egypt/Morocco, many report scams/overcharging; many avoid these destinations due to low trust.

- Solution: A managed marketplace combining:

  - A human “buddy” (ex-military regional veteran) who either travels with you ($50–$100/day) or supports 24/7 over WhatsApp (~$10/day), handling logistics, safety, cultural context, real-time decisions, bargaining, and issue resolution.
  - An AI-powered app that learns from buddy playbooks and trip data to make discovery/itineraries/bookings/SOS increasingly self-serve over time, routing edge cases to humans.

- Operating model: India is split into tight geographic clusters. Each Regional Head owns partnerships (stays, drivers, experiences), recruits/trains buddies, and is accountable for NPS. They prioritize excellence in the first ~200 trips over maximizing GMV (accept calendar clashes).

- Moat claim: Ex-military buddies generate proprietary, high-quality local “geodata graphs” (verified inventory, scam patterns, SOPs, routes, nuanced curations) that generic AI itinerary tools can’t produce.

- Market: India inbound leisure from higher-spend foreign travelers is framed as an $8.6B spend pool (using Govt of India tourism stats), with a broader thesis that the model expands to other high-asymmetry destinations globally.

- Economics: They target ~20% blended take rate via end-to-end destination management (across accommodations, food/experiences, transport, buddy services, etc.). They argue currency arbitrage enables scaling to $100M revenue with ~20,000 trips/year and ~830 buddies (at ~2 trips/buddy/month, ~$5k revenue/trip).

- GTM: Trust-first channels (Reddit credibility, Instagram storytelling, SEO/LLM discovery) plus partnerships (micro-influencers, international universities, boutique travel agencies, corporate bookings, diplomats). Paid ads as acceleration (claimed $150–$200 CAC).

- Traction: Mentions pilots—Aug ’25 corporate (Hexaware), Nov ’25 B2C via Reddit, Jan ’26 B2C via “CS” (customer support/community).

- Fundraise: Raising INR 6 Cr seed to reach ~200 trips in ~16 months and ~$1M revenue; roadmap includes app launch, verified stays, community support, and international UPI payments.

Team: CEO Medha Meenal (ops background), COO Lt Gen VK Mishra (Retd), and marketing lead Ishan Sinha.

### Business Overview

- Namaa World connects Western travelers (primarily European millennials) with retired Indian military personnel who serve as local guides and "buddies" for authentic travel experiences in India   

- Current AOV: ~$5,000 per trip with 12-15% gross margins in short term, targeting higher margins (25%) through owned accommodation partnerships and experiences  

- Origin story: Megha identified the problem in 2016 while interning in Bangladesh, where Western colleagues were "fascinated with India but too scared to travel"  

- Recent trips to Morocco and Egypt validated that information asymmetry, not safety itself, is the core problem for travelers

### Business Model & Operations

- **Target Market**: European millennials 30-40 years old with minimum $150/day budget  

- **Supply Strategy**: Exclusively recruiting ex-military liaison officers (LOs) who served Brigadiers and above - chosen for trustworthiness, cultural training, and ability to handle VIP experiences  

- **Training**: 3-day module per trip (1 day tech, 2 days cultural sensitivity) 

- **Geographic Focus**: Four clusters account for 40%+ of foreign travel - Ladakh, Himachal Pradesh, Kerala, Rajasthan  

- **Key Partnerships**: Just closed B2B partnership with Postcard Hotels offering one complimentary night for first 100 trips - $3,000 per person for 7 nights/8 days  

- **Accommodation Strategy**: Targeting boutique stays (6-10 rooms, family-run) at 25% margins vs. 15% from larger chains  

### Go-to-Market Strategy

- **Demand Generation**: Reddit is strongest channel (all 12 pilot customers came via Reddit), followed by Instagram organic content  

- **Avoiding Performance Marketing Trap**: Learned from Mesa experience that jumping on performance marketing flywheel too early creates unsustainable CAC:LTV dynamics  

- **Quality Over Scale**: Deliberately capping at 200 trips over next 15 months to prove quality, not scale - "first 200 trips are proof of quality"  

- **Repeat Behavior**: Western travelers revisit countries multiple times with longer stays and more people, unlike Indian travelers who prefer new destinations each trip 

- **LTV Opportunity**: Potential for 7-8 trips per customer over lifetime (college, first job, marriage, kids, pre/post-retirement) with increasing AOV  

### Product & Supply Strategy

- **Four Pillar Approach**: Context (planning/booking), assurance/support, and delight experiences - planning to productize 2.5 pillars while keeping 1.5 with buddies to prevent platform disintermediation   

- **AI Integration**: Built proprietary database of boutique hotels, restaurants, cocktail places across India to make buddies "10x smarter" - AI empowers buddies rather than replacing human interaction  

- **Differentiation from Competitors**: Unlike G-Adventures and Intrepid Travel (both non-Indian companies operating via DMCs with 6-person group minimums), Namaa World offers personalized experiences without packaged tour constraints  

- **Target Customer Preference**: Western TG "hates packages" and being boxed into itineraries - wants flexibility while maintaining safety and local expertise 

- **Margin Drivers**: Highest margins from accommodation and owned experiences (working to productize surprise elements like hot springs in Ladakh, anniversary dinners, etc.)
