## Z47 conversation with Founder 





### Notes:

Surfacing [Navanc](https://www.navanc.com/about-us), they're building a tool for property evaluation which is used by lenders in collateral assessment for secured loans. Essentially replacing the local valuer model which has issues such as opacity, TAT, fraud, etc. Founder is an EO with extensive experience in the space. Early traction is also decent - ~30 lakhs MRR, cracked mid-sized NBFCs and in final stages of cracking large NBFCs. The company is raising $2-3 mn. currently.

Please find [vault](https://deltazen47.sharepoint.com/:f:/s/TheVault/EoVazkQZAohMugwEJVG_hCUBzM4qFPjTsHBLgOKa2tFOEg?e=czvCd3) link, customer refs + our eval views below -

<u>**Eval Views**</u>

**Taha - Maybe**

+ EO building in domain with significant hours in the space, good set of initial clients as well- IRMA + ICICI+ NBFC (pattern match)

+ Depth in the space --- had clear answers on property diligence(state level), valuation

? Unsure on what this product is — BRE, AI assisted workflow, services with a CRM

? End Scale outcome-- can he expand into legal workflows as well

**Dhairen – Maybe**

+ Domain native

+ Addressing a major pain point for lenders, can see a play here - need to do ref on delta in efficiency + pricing

+ Interesting traction - Billing 7-10 lakhs p.m. to mid scale lenders quickly

? Product v/s company? Ability to expand into adjacencies?

? There's a large services angle at play so will need understand if there's potential for a business with quality rev. or is it just an aggregator driving margins through scale

I feel this could be a very attractive acquisition target for someone like Perfios if they don't choose to build it internally in the first place. Understand Square yards has also already ventured into it, so multiple players already coming in. Product v/s company continues to be the key concern?

**Nipun – Maybe+**

+ Liked Naga; comes with a rich background too

+ Thoughtful - had decently thoughtful answers to almost all the questions that we asked

+ Like the product suite and found it comprehensive

+ Initial traction – landed 14 clients (though have not got to larger guys yet)

? Need to understand

- why is he winning the clients (pricing only?)

- is the AI that he talks about real

- how much is customizable vs standard (hence scalable)

- his ambition and how will this business become big
