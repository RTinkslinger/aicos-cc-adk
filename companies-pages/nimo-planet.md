## Product Updates 2025-10-23 

Nimo Beta launched on Product Hunt

**Our vision for the future of work**
At Nimo, we believe the future of AI interaction isn't browsers - it's a orchestrator, where we delegate tasks to AI inside a beautiful canvas. We will be collaborating with a mix of AI agents and human-centered teams for the best outputs. I'm super excited to build Nimo as the next home for AI.

For a long time the browser has been a door to the internet. But with AI, it's not just about browsing the internet, but about getting things done. Although browsing still stands as an entry point, it limits your capabilities to get things done with AI.

**Connect your favorite apps­**
First, connect your apps.
Our App Store features 100+ apps that you can connect to your canvas. Your data stays on your computer & iCloud, never on our servers or with partners making it a completely private and secure experience.
**­
Start easy with templates­**
We've put together a beautiful set of template cards to get you started and familiarize with the canvas. Once you start a task on Nimo, it creates an independent window within the canvas. That's your playground for that task and everything related to it.­

**New Task Composer­**
The new task composer is always accessible from the bottom of the canvas. Access it quickly with the Keyboard Shortcut: Command + N
­
**No tabs. It’s AI Cards­**
We completely reimagined the the traditional tab interfaces to dedicated AI Cards with detailed context in Nimo. Each card combines multiple connected apps based on your task and provides live status and insights.
­
**Create Dynamic Apps­**
One of my favorite feature from the beginning has been Nimo’s ability to create consumable information at a glance in the form of instantly generated apps based on your data. We call them Dynamic Apps, and we have improved them a lot.

Just enable the Dynamic App toggle to start creating apps.
