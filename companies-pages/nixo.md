### Company Overview

- Nixo is building a specialized platform for Forward Deployed Engineers (FDEs)

- Founded by Priya and Preeti, Stanford CS graduates with complementary AI and engineering backgrounds

- Genesis: Priya's experience as an FDE at Kumo AI, where she noticed inefficiencies in client implementations

- Product launched 3 weeks ago with 8 paying customers ($40K ARR)

- Pipeline of approximately $150K ARR with Series B, C, and mid-market companies

- Raising $2M at $25M cap, with $600K already secured

### Problem & Solution

- FDEs lack specialized tools tailored to their unique workflows

- Current tools (CRMs, project management) miss critical context between sales, support, and engineering

- Nixo serves as an automated System of Record (SOR) for FDEs

- Key features:

  - Integrates with HubSpot, Intercom, Linear, GitHub, PostHog, Sentry
  - Automatically captures customer context from various sources
  - Knowledge graph of code repositories to surface relevant, reusable work
  - Management dashboard for FDE bandwidth and customer status tracking

### Market Opportunity

- FDE is emerging as a distinct role separate from Solutions Architects

- 117K new FDE roles in August vs. 25K SDE roles (Bay Area LinkedIn data)

- Major companies like OpenAI establishing dedicated FDE teams

- Target customers: AI-native B2B companies across various verticals (grocery tech, security tech, healthcare)

### Business Model

- Tier-based pricing based on number of customers served

- Base platform fee + integration or custom fees

- Currently in price discovery mode - first tier has increased from $3K to $12K

- Customer size ranges from teams of 3 FDEs to 20+ FDEs
