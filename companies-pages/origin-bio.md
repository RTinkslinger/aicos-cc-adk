## Company Overview
AI platform for designing safer cell & gene therapies. Builds DNA switches and dials that program gene expression with unprecedented precision. Flagship product Axis (launched Oct 2025) is the first AI model that generates regulatory DNA elements AND predicts their function — programming what genes do in specific disease cells.
