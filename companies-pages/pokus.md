### Pokus AI — Investor deck summary
- Problem
  - Urban consumers spend significant time on operational chores and recurring tasks. These involve research, coordination, booking, negotiation, and follow‑ups.
- Solution
  - A WhatsApp-first “personal assistant in your pocket” that combines AI automation with human ops for edge cases and physical execution.
  - Aim: move from reactive task handling to proactive, workflow-led assistance via a memory layer and recommendations
- Product and workflows
  - Handles ad‑hoc and recurring tasks: home repairs, vendor coordination, cab bookings, refunds, gifting, licenses, etc.
  - Examples of automated or semi‑automated workflows: weekly groceries, daily porter, routine cab bookings, medicine reminders/refills, restaurant reservations, gifting cadences.
- Approach and tech
  - Hybrid Human + AI architecture.
  - Modular stack: AI Relationship Manager for memory and recommendations, Executor OS for task capture, grading, SOP generation, and a copilot for human operators.
  - Progressive automation roadmap across planning, search, subjective research, booking, calling, form filling, coordination, summarization.
- ICP and use cases
  - 25–28: lifestyle and exploratory tasks.
  - 28–35: self and parents use cases, home management becomes sticky.
  - 35+: higher value “function outsourcing” such as home manager, recurring logistics, and family routines.
  - Positioning gravitates toward a “household COO” for families and busy professionals.[
- Traction
  - ~60 paying subscribers.
  - ~20 tasks handled per day on average.
  - Reported NPS 77 (from 30 respondents)
- Long‑term vision
  - 24/7 personal assistant on WhatsApp that learns preferences, automates end‑to‑end flows, and invokes humans only when subjective judgment or physical execution is required
