## Analysis by dev - 2026-03-05
### Company Snapshot
Problem & solution
Qila Games pursues a social-first hybrid-casual model aimed at delivering premium games with high LTVs in India, MENA, and LATAM. Hybrid-casual combines simple mechanics with the monetisation depth of mid-core IAP-heavy games, targeting the region’s rising willingness to pay and microtransactions. First IP Ludo Hero is positioned to hybrid-casualise a globally familiar mechanic (Ludo) to drive retention and monetisation at scale.

Ideal customer profile and pricing
Target markets: India, MENA, LATAM.
Pricing: IAP-heavy monetisation; exact pricing model not disclosed.

Status of product & traction highlight
Ludo Hero live on Android and iOS; game trailer available; early traction indicates strong retention and engagement; on track to reach $7M ARR by 2027.
