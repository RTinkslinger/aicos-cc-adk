### Summary
At Quivly, we are building an AI-native customer intelligence platform for post-sales teams - think "Clay for post-sales."
We help customer success, account management, and revenue operations teams drive usage, expansion, and retention in the new era of usage and outcome-based pricing.
As B2B software and AI companies shift to hybrid, usage, or outcome-based models, revenue is no longer earned upfront at the point of sale - it's earned post-sales by driving customer adoption and value realization. Quivly gives post-sales teams the intelligence and automation they need to maximize lifetime value in this new revenue reality.

But they lack the intelligence, automation, and tooling that sales teams have had for decades.

### **FAQs**
### 1. Why are you building this?
Both of us have spent 10+ years building and scaling B2B products and companies. Despite all of the tools at our disposal throughout this time, neither us nor the revenue teams we worked with found a scalable and effective way to understand, keep and grow existing customers easily.
### 2. What unique insight do you have about where the world is headed? What does the world look like in 10 years if all goes well?
**The fundamental shift**: B2B revenue is moving from "sell upfront, earn upfront" to "sell upfront, earn over time." With usage and outcome-based pricing, companies are essentially running a continuous earn-out with every customer. This means post-sales teams are now the primary revenue engine.
**AI as the great enabler**: Just as AI-powered tools have allowed sales teams to do more with less, AI will transform post-sales. In 5 years, every CSM and AM will have an AI assistant that knows their book of business better than they do, proactively surfaces the right actions, and automates the busy work.
**With Quivly, the future looks like this:**
- Post-sales teams operate with the same sophistication and efficiency as elite sales organizations
- Every customer interaction is informed by real-time intelligence and predictive insights
- CSMs and AMs spend their time on strategic, high-value conversations instead of data wrangling
- Companies maximize lifetime value by proactively driving usage and expansion with every customer
The companies that invest in their post-sales intelligence infrastructure today will dominate retention and expansion tomorrow.
### 3. What do companies use today?
The typical B2B software or AI company's post-sales tech stack is fragmented and inadequate:
- **Spreadsheet based**: Post-sales teams (CSMs, AMs, RevOps) manually track account health, expansion opportunities, and usage metrics in spreadsheets. Data is stale, siloed, and prone to human error.
- **Generic CS platforms**: Tools like Gainsight and ChurnZero were built for the subscription era. They don't handle usage data well, lack AI capabilities, and require heavy manual configuration. Teams end up with rigid health scores that miss nuanced signals.
- **Point solution overload**: Teams cobble together 5-7 tools (product analytics, billing, support, CRM, BI tools) and spend countless hours manually connecting the dots across systems.
- **Built for sales, not CS**: The best AI and intelligence tools—Clay, Apollo, Outreach—are built for sales teams. Post-sales teams are left making do with tools that weren't designed for their workflows or the usage-based revenue model.
