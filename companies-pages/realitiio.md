## Realiti Q1 FY25 update

### **Broker SaaS**
We have been iterating hard on the core of Realiti’s SaaS foray, the Broker GTM. After lot of experiments we are ready to publish authoritative numbers on our first sales campaign in June 2024.
**Total Leads - 600**
**Leads in active follow up - 380**
**User Self Signups - 90 **
**Realiti Microsite activation  - 35 **
**Paid customers - 10**
Some key points to note here:
- All of these are **Self Signups** originating purely from Google and FB campaigns and not by forced cold calling
- The** Microsite activation** is a very important step. It is an industry first product which puts a ultra modern variant of a self branded ‘99 acres’ or ‘MagicBricks’ in the hands of each broker. And again these are fully user initiated activations
- **> 50 % **of the unconverted leads from this campaign are fully warm and our conversions and word of mouth is only improving day by day
Most importantly this is totally in addition to our existing **$200K ARR** from our builder line of business. Our immediate goal is to increase magnitude of these campaigns and hit predictable monthly revenues from the Broker SaaS line of business
