**Founder Introduction:**
Rushabh, previously involved in building businesses in the home and furniture sector, is now exploring opportunities in the fashion industry with a focus on functional apparel.
**Business Idea:**
He is considering launching a brand called PRBT, inspired by the concept of peak performance and ascension. The brand aims to create functional, stylish, and comfortable apparel using innovative materials that cater to the evolving lifestyle needs of consumers.
- **Brand Concept:**
  - PRBT is inspired by the idea of peak performance and ascension, aiming to offer apparel that is both functional and stylish.
  - The brand will focus on integrating innovative materials to meet the evolving lifestyle needs of consumers.
- **Product Line:**
  - Initial offerings will include tops, bottoms, and outerwear for men, with plans to expand into women's apparel.
  - Products will be designed to provide temperature regulation, moisture management, and overall comfort.
  - The product range will cater to everyday, exercise, and travel/adventure use cases.
- **Material Innovation:**
  - Emphasis on using advanced fabrics that offer cooling effects and moisture-wicking properties.
  - Collaborations with experts and manufacturers to develop unique fabrics that enhance comfort and performance.
  - Plans to introduce signature design elements that incorporate functional benefits.
- **Market Positioning:**
  - Targeting a mid-premium segment, focusing on consumers who value functionality and style.
  - Building a brand narrative around comfort, versatility, and understated style with flauntable elements.
- **Sales and Distribution:**
  - Initial focus on direct-to-consumer channels to establish brand presence and gather consumer feedback.
  - Potential expansion into retail partnerships and international markets as the brand grows.
- **Marketing Strategy:**
  - Develop a strong brand identity and visual aesthetic to attract the target audience.
  - Utilize digital marketing, social media, and influencer collaborations to build brand awareness and loyalty.
  - Highlight product innovation and unique fabric features in marketing campaigns.
- **Financial Projections:**
  - Aim to achieve a gross margin of 70% and an EBITDA margin of 15-20% in the long term.
  - Will start with COGS of 8-10 USD per piece
  - Aims for revenue growth to reach 400-500 crore INR scale within five to seven years.
  - Initial funding of $500,000 to support inventory, prototyping, and marketing efforts.
- **Long-term Vision:**
  - Establish PRBT as a leading brand in functional apparel, known for innovation and quality.
  - Expand the product line to include additional categories like accessories and innerwear.
  - Build a loyal customer base through exceptional product experiences and community engagement.
**Next Steps:**
Plans to finalize designs, begin prototyping, and secure funding. He is also working on brand development and aims to have a clear product and funding plan ready for potential investors.
