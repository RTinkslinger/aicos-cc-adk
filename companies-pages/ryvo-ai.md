    - Revo aims to be a credit intelligence platform that helps consumers understand and manage their credit profile

    - Initial focus on credit management with plans to expand to a comprehensive financial companion covering payments, insurance, and investments

    - Eventually aiming to serve the broader middle-class segment with comprehensive financial guidance
    ### Strategic Considerations
    - Considering both "deep stack" (focusing deeply on lending) vs. "broad" (covering multiple financial services) approaches

    - Building a revenue-based structure rather than focusing solely on customer growth
    - Starting with a proof-of-concept to validate the model before expanding
