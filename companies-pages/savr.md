  ### Company Snapshot
  Problem & solution
Savr aims to organize surplus liquidation in India through an omnichannel value-retail model, starting with F&B and BPC and expanding to multi-category formats.

Ideal customer profile and pricing
Deal‑savvy aspirational urban youth (10–50 L household income) attracted to experiential offline treasure-hunt formats; offline-first with transparency on shelf-life pricing.
Pricing: discounts targeting 5‑15% of MRP (retailer price), with potential 15‑35% COGS realization relative to MRP; some categories shown as higher ASP with frequent purchases; price transparency on shelf life used to build trust.

Status of product & traction highlight
Piloted via six weekend pop-ups in Bangalore; 1000+ customers; ~₹3 L revenue across pilots; AOVs: F&B ~₹250, BPC ~₹730; F&B sell-through ~95%, BPC ~70%; gross margins 30–50%.
