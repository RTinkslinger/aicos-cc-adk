### Business Overview 2025-07-03T15:34:00.000+05:30 
<!-- file block -->
Based on the document "Business Overview.pdf," Serendipity Space is developing reusable satellites for pharmaceutical manufacturing in space with these key aspects:
**Company Overview**
Serendipity Space is building autonomous satellites to manufacture pharmaceuticals in Low Earth Orbit and return them safely to Earth. The satellites consist of three components:
- Satellite bus for power and communication (commercially available)
- Pharmaceutical module for manufacturing (proprietary IP)
- Lightweight re-entry heatshield for safe return (proprietary IP)
**Business Model**
The company operates in two vertical markets:
**1. Structure-Based Drug Discovery (SBDD)**
Crystallizing proteins in microgravity that cannot be crystallized on Earth, enabling:
- 50% reduction in preclinical development timelines
- More precise drug design through high-quality protein structures
- Potential deals worth $2-5M upfront plus milestone payments
**2. Formulation Development**
Manufacturing pharmaceutical ingredients in microgravity to solve issues with:
- Particle size uniformity
- Viscosity and solubility
- Potential deals worth $10-100M based on per kilogram pricing
**Market Strategy**
Serendipity uses a flywheel approach:
- Start with SBDD services (smaller satellites, gram-scale production)
- Use revenue to fund larger satellites for formulation development (kilogram-scale)
- Target pharmaceutical companies developing high-value therapeutics
**Development Timeline**
- 2025: Space qualification of pharma module and engineering of heatshield
- 2026: Manufacturing and integration of the entire satellite system
- 2027: Commercial launch of the complete satellite
