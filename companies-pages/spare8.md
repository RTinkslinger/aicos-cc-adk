**Brief Overview:**
Spare8 is a B2B2C Gold Leasing platform that lets users lease their gold to jewellers, earn interest on it, auto-invest, along with opting for purchasing and delivery of their existing gold by leveraging upon UPI 2.0
With the launch of Gold Leasing and our unique APIs, we're now letting MFDs, banks and embedded fintech platforms seamlessly integrate with our stack, making us the one-stop gold hub for end-users and businesses alike.
