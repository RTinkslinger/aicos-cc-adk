    ### Company Background
    - Trac is a trading platform built specifically for Gen Z users, founded by Vedant along with Aryan and Siddharth.  
    - The founders discovered that while account opening and order execution were easy, young users struggled with picking their first stock due to information overload.  
    - They identified that Gen Z users have grown up using thumb-friendly platforms like Instagram and gaming apps, but traditional trading platforms require navigating multiple screens. 
    - Trac started as a stock discovery platform and later added execution capabilities based on user feedback.  
    ### Target Audience
    - Users aged 20-23, primarily first-time traders (85% using Trac as their first trading platform). 
    - Located in tier 1 and tier 2 cities, typically recent college graduates in their first jobs. 
    - Average income of 5-6 lakhs per year (₹35,000-40,000 monthly). 
    - Many live with parents, reducing expenses and allowing higher risk appetite.  
    ### Product Features
    - Free stock reports with clear buy/sell verdicts, analysis, targets, and stop-loss recommendations.   
    - Natural language search function that works in Hindi and English.  
    - Detailed insights with scores, social media sentiment, and event sensitivity index.  
    - Single-screen trading mode allowing users to trade with their thumbs. 
    - Focus on helping users minimize losses rather than maximize profits. 
    ### Business Metrics
    - 30,000 DMAT accounts opened through the platform. 
    - 3,000-3,500 daily active traders, with 5,500-6,000 daily trades.  
    - Customer acquisition cost of ₹350-400 vs. industry standard of ₹2,000.  
    - 10-12% of users become intraday traders, remaining 90% trade over 0-3 month periods.  
    - 70% M10 retention rate. 
    ### Business Model
    - ₹20 per trade.  
    - Top 10-12% of users (intraday traders) generate ₹8,000-10,000 per user annually. 
    - 8-10 month payback period on customer acquisition. 
    - Free insights and reports serve as acquisition tools.  
    ### Acquisition Strategy
    - Partner with lifestyle influencers rather than finance influencers.  
    - Lead with value proposition of insights rather than trading account benefits.   
    - Focus on specific events like IPOs to attract users seeking information. 
    ### Current Status & Future Plans
    - Recently received broking license and built tech stack from ground up. 
    - Raising $3.3 million with $2.6 million already secured from Lightspeed and $700K from another investor.  
    - Planning to launch full trading platform in 1-1.5 months after exchange approval. 
    - Future focus on developing Margin Trading Facility (MTF) product.   
    - Aiming for Series A funding in 6-8 months.
