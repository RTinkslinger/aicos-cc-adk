About the Company
UGX.AI is building a full-stack Industrial DataOps platform to enable manufacturing companies to access and leverage real-time machine data for productivity, cost efficiency, and sustainability improvements.
They provide an integrated hardware, middleware, and software platform including:
1. Universal Edge Gateway: Low-cost, industrial-grade device for machine connectivity.
1. Industrial Protocol Library: Supports 40+ machine models, converting complex machine protocols into software-friendly APIs.
1. Unified Industrial Data Platform: Transforms machine data into actionable insights.
1. Industrial Apps: Including MES traceability, energy management, and remote asset management.
UGX has achieved significant traction with over 20 paying customers and more than 100 indirect customers, with deployments in India, and early interest from USA, South Africa, and other geographies.
Revenue:
- FY24: INR 48L (Order Book INR 1.8Cr)
- FY25 (est.): INR 2-2.5Cr
Founders
- [Ankur Singh](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ankurkrsingh/) (ISB) Sr. Manager – Energy Utilities @ Cognizant
- [Abhishek Kumar](https://www.linkedin.com/in/abhishek-kumar-5ba83914/?originalSubdomain=in) (IIT BHU) – Founder @ SchoolMitra (Exited)
- [Ankit Agarwal](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ankit-agarwal-6239078/) (IIT BHU) – Product Innovation @ GreyOrange
Combined experience across deeptech, IIoT, robotics, SaaS, and embedded engineering.
Refs on the founders:
Manish and RK have been tracking the team for the last 6 months. The founders are seen as iterative operators with clarity on solving the 'data plumbing' problem in factories. Their focus on building the protocol library and device stack as the core IP here with early customer validation is driving the excitement. The key question being tracked by the collective is their strategic thinkings and speed of market adoption/ ability to win more mid-market and enterprise accounts outside the current early adopters. Plus their ability to run a tight global GTM remains untested.
Investment Considerations
- Strong IP led DataOps Platform: Tackling a fundamental data gap in the industrial space with a full-stack approach; hardware margins improving from 50% to 80% as they scale.
- Demonstrated Early Traction: Revenue growth from INR 48L to a projected INR 2-2.5Cr; validated across sectors including automotive, electronics, and sustainability use cases.
Investment Risks
- Out of domain 2nd time founders
- Sales Cycle & Adoption: As with most industrial SaaS, the GTM cycle is long and needs strong channel partnerships to scale faster.
- Hardware Dependence in Early Model: While shifting to middleware and API monetization, hardware still contributes a significant part of revenue today.
- Competitive Pressure: Need to accelerate their protocol library and integrations to maintain differentiation against global players like PTC.
