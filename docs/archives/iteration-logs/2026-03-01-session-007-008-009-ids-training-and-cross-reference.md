## What is IDS?

Aakash's definition: "Increasing Degrees of Sophistication — a way to describe our way of working which is always be getting smarter over time about a person or company over time with our work."

IDS is the core operating methodology for Z47 and DeVC. It's not a template or a document format — it's a compounding intelligence philosophy. Every interaction, reference check, data point, and team discussion adds a layer of sophistication to the understanding of a person or company. The IDS "document" is the living accumulation of all these layers.

## Universal IDS Elements

### Notation System
- `+` or `++` — Positive signal (++ = table-thumping)
- `?` or `??` — Concern or question (? = minor, ?? = significant)
- `+?` — Positive but needs validation
- `-` — Neutral or negative observation

### Conviction Spectrum
100% Yes → Strong Maybe (SM) → Maybe+ → Maybe → Maybe -ve → Weak Maybe (WM) → Pass → Pass Forever

### Scoring Framework (DeVC)
- **Spiky Score:** 7 criteria scored out of 1.0 total
- **EO/FMF/Thesis/Price Score:** 4 categories scored out of 40 total
- Both scores captured in structured tables on Notion company pages

### Key IDS Principles
1. **Compounding over time** — Every interaction adds a layer; nothing is wasted
2. **Multi-source triangulation** — Never rely on one source; always cross-reference
3. **Explicit uncertainty** — `?` marks are not failures; they're honest intellectual humility
4. **Individual views preserved** — Each team member's voice is captured, not averaged
5. **Time-stamped layers** — Every entry is dated, showing the progression of understanding
6. **Reflexive self-critique** — "Reflections" sections after meetings capture meta-learning

### Critical Structural Insight: Where IDS Lives

**Companies DB pages are the primary IDS repository**, not Network DB person entries. The Network DB entries for people are mostly blank pages that serve as relationship nodes (Current Co, Past Cos, Angel Folio, C+E Attendance links). The actual IDS intelligence — meeting notes, references, team views, scoring — lives on the Company pages in the Companies DB.

**Email-based IDS content gets copy-pasted into Notion.** The Shashank Shekhars Company page contains the full email threads (EF Mining + BRCs) preserved verbatim. This shows the workflow: IDS accumulates in email threads, then gets transferred to Notion for permanent storage.

**Notion contains MORE data than the training docs.** The xPay Notion page has customer call notes not present in the IDS PDF. The Confido Health page has 15+ dated interactions beyond what's in the training documents. The Flent page has structured scoring tables that weren't in the email thread. **Notion is the living, updated version; the training docs are point-in-time snapshots.**
