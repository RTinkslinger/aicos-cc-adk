## Signal Capture: The 5-Surface Problem

Aakash has 5 capture surfaces held together by a fuzzy mental memory map:

| Surface | What goes there | Retrieval method |
|---------|----------------|-----------------|
| **X bookmarks** | Posts, profiles, content | Platform-specific, fuzzy recall |
| **LinkedIn saves** | People, articles | Platform-specific, fuzzy recall |
| **Medium/YouTube saves** | Articles, videos, watch later | Platform-specific, fuzzy recall |
| **WhatsApp self-channel** | Distilled high-signal links (cross-platform) | Long scroll, hard to navigate |
| **Phone screengrabs** | Profiles, posts showing thinking — used as ad-hoc CRM | Context-triggered recall, hundreds, rarely revisited |

### The Signal Flow for People
```
Discovery (content/events/intros)
    → Fuzzy capture (screengrabs/bookmarks/WhatsApp self)
        → Team handoff (WhatsApp "Network DB" group)
            → Notion (when team has bandwidth)
```
**Leakage at every transition.** Screengrabs that never get sent to team. Bookmarks never circled back to. Team backlog on Notion updates. The WhatsApp Network DB group is "not exhaustive."
