## 1. The Core Insight: Network as Operating System

The AI CoS is a **Network Operating System**. Aakash manages 1,000–2,000 active relationships. He is an outbound-first networker. The system breaks down after meetings — follow-ups fall through cracks, interactions go unlogged (mostly stay in memory), relationship health is untracked.

Everything flows from the network: deal flow, portfolio support, IC management, content, LP relations.

### The Network Intelligence Stack (Target)
- **CAPTURE:** Log every interaction with minimal friction
- **CONTEXTUALIZE:** Enrich contacts with background, history, relevance
- **PRIORITIZE:** Surface who to meet, follow up with, reconnect with
- **PREPARE:** Pre-meeting intelligence briefs
- **FOLLOW THROUGH:** Post-meeting actions, commitment tracking, nudges
- **GROW:** Proactive network expansion (outbound research)
