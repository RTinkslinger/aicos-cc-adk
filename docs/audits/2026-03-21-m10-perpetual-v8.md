The current approach (terminate idle connections every 15 min) is reactive. A circuit breaker would:
1. Before heavy cron job execution, check `pg_stat_activity` count
2. If utilization > 70%, defer the job (re-enqueue for next slot)
3. Log the deferral in `cir_propagation_log` with significance=medium
4. The `cir_self_heal` weekly job would catch any permanently deferred items
