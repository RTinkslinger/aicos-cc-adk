## Loop Details

### L26-27: Page Content Quality Upgrade
- **Specialist 1 (Analyst):** Sampled 50 medium page_content companies. 100% were field concatenation: "Name\nSector: X\nDeal Status: NA\nPipeline: NA". Zero business descriptions. Confirmed M9 audit finding.
- **Specialist 2 (Engineer) Batch 1:** Updated 1,959 companies with current employee context by joining entity_connections (network->company, current_employee/past_employee) to network.person_name. Added "Key People" and "Notable Alumni" sections.
- **Specialist 2 Batch 2:** Updated 298 more companies with thesis relevance and sector peer context (no employees but have other connections).
- **Specialist 2 Batch 3:** Rebuilt page_content for remaining 2,318 companies using consistent field format (removed NA padding).
- **Specialist 2 Append passes:** Added thesis_relevance to 855 companies, sector_peers to 1,410 companies, interaction_discussed_in to companies with meeting context.
- **Specialist 4 (QA):** Rich content (300+) improved from 7.8% to 16.5%. Cybersecurity search: 0 -> 56 results.

### L28-29: Sector "Other" Reclassification
- **Specialist 1 (Analyst):** Sampled 100 "Other" companies. Found 3 categories: educational institutions (~40%), well-known companies with obvious sectors, unnamed/placeholder companies.
- **Specialist 2 (Engineer):** Three-pass reclassification:
  1. **Pattern matching:** 338 educational institutions reclassified to "Education" (University, School, College, Institute, Academy, IIT, IIM, BITS patterns)
  2. **Name-based:** Financial services (bank, capital, finance keywords), B2B (consulting, solutions, technologies keywords), Consumer (well-known brands), SaaS
  3. **Connection inference:** For each "Other" company, found its employees' other companies, used the most common sector among those companies. Reclassified 497 companies.
- **Specialist 4 (QA):** "Other" dropped from 27.6% (1,264) to 9.2% (420). Target <20% achieved.

### L30-31: Network Data Quality
- **Specialist 1 (Analyst):** Only 13 network members without email are currently employed at companies with known domains. Domain coverage is sparse (757/4,575 companies).
- **Specialist 2 (Engineer):** Generated email candidates for 12 eligible members (excluding URL-as-name entries). Added 3,525 name identifiers to identity_map for person resolution. 98% of network members now have 2+ identifiers.
- **Specialist 3 (QA):** Email coverage unchanged at 30.6% (limited by sparse domain data). Identity resolution coverage improved: 3,454/3,525 (98%) have 2+ identifiers for M8 Cindy.

### L32-33: Embedding Quality
- **Specialist 1 (Analyst):** Embeddings temporarily dropped from 80.2% to 3.8% due to `clear_companies_embedding_on_update` trigger. Expected behavior -- embeddings are regenerated asynchronously.
- **Specialist 2 (Engineer):** Verified `embedding_input_companies()` function includes page_content (up to 2000 chars). All enriched content (thesis names, people names, sector peers) will be embedded when queue processes.
- **Specialist 3 (QA):** 15,215 embedding jobs queued in pgmq `embedding_jobs` queue. Edge function processes these asynchronously. Archive shows 0 processed so far (queue may need manual trigger or cron activation).
