## L46-47: Audit Findings — Real vs Fake Content

### The Problem
Every single portfolio page_content (142/142 = 100%) used the same template format:
```
Portfolio: [name]
Stage: [value]
Health: [value]
Category: [value]
Entry Cheque: [value]
...
Key Questions: [value]
High Impact: [value]
```

This is not enrichment — it's reformatting structured fields into a text block. The REAL intelligence (human-written insights from Notion) was present in dedicated columns but used only as label:value pairs.

### Intelligence Available But Underutilized

| Rich Column | Records with Real Content | Avg Content Length | Intelligence Type |
|-------------|--------------------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| high_impact | 21 (15%) | 50 chars | What Aakash/DeVC actually did for this company |
| key_questions | 11 (8%) | 90 chars | Open strategic questions being tracked |
| external_signal | 2 (1%) | 42 chars | External market signals (funding interest, etc.) |
| scale_of_business | 74 (52%) | 22 chars | Revenue/metrics snapshot |
| note_on_deployment | 31 (22%) | 48 chars | Follow-on strategy context |
| thesis_connection | 45 (32%) | 70 chars | Which thesis threads this company maps to |

### Additional Untapped Data Sources
- **142 research files** in `portfolio-research/` directory — deep research reports (avg ~2500 words each) generated by Parallel Deep Research
- These contain: executive summaries, funding history, competitive analysis, team background, traction metrics, market position — none of which is reflected in page_content
- **Research file integration is a future loop priority** (requires reading files from disk, which SQL alone cannot do)

## L48-49: Portfolio page_content — Template to Brief

### Before (Template Format)
```
Portfolio: Confido Health
Stage: early revenue
Health: Green
Category: Cat B SaaS
Entry Cheque: $100000
Ownership: 6.71%
Scale: $670K ARR as on Feb'25
Key Questions: - How have they expanded the ACVs...
High Impact: Committed before Together...
Spikey: 1
Check-in: Monthly
HC Priority: P0
Sector: SaaS
Website: https://www.confido.health/
Tags: AI, Healthcare
```

### After (Intelligence Brief)
```
Confido Health (SaaS) — https://www.confido.health/
Early Revenue | Health: Green | 6.71% ownership | $100K entry
Scale: $670K ARR as on Feb'25
Entry at $10.0M (best case: $350M)
Deployment: Next round will be < $30M post.

Our Impact: Committed before Together and helped resolve issues with IQ during closure

Open Questions:
- How have they expanded the ACVs and captured mid market clients / thoughtful GTM
- Has the customer pull deepened
- Ability to attract US talent

Cadence: Monthly | Priority: P0 | Category: Cat B SaaS | Spikey: 1
```

### Key Changes
1. **Status line** — single line with stage, health, ownership, entry cheque (not 6 separate lines)
2. **Valuation trajectory** — entry → current → best case (was just entry_round_valuation in a separate field)
3. **Human intelligence promoted** — Our Impact, Open Questions, External Signal get their own sections with visual separation
4. **Deployment context inline** — follow-on strategy visible at a glance
5. **Thesis connection** — included where present, linking company to strategic themes
