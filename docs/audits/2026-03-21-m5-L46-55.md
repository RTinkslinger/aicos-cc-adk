## What Was Built

### L46-47: Cindy Intelligence Multiplier
**Function:** `cindy_intelligence_multiplier(action_row)`
- Integrates M8's `interaction_action_links` table and `interaction_intelligence_score()`
- Actions linked to HIGH intelligence interactions (score >= 6) get 8-12% boost
- Actions with financial signals (deal_signals JSONB) get additional 4% boost
- Multi-link density bonus: 3+ links = +3%, 2 links = +1%
- Cap: max 20% boost
- **Coverage:** 26.5% of proposed actions (13/49) have Cindy signal
- **Max multiplier observed:** 1.16 (AuraML portfolio actions with financial signals)

### L48-49: Thesis Momentum Multiplier
**Function:** `thesis_momentum_multiplier(action_row)`
- Integrates M6's `thesis_health_dashboard()` momentum scores
- High momentum (>=8): +8% boost. Active (>=6): +4%. Moderate (4-6): neutral. Declining (<4): -4%. Dead (<2): -8%
- Smart freshness adjustment: stale thesis + research action = BOOST (need investigation), stale thesis + routine action = gentle penalty
- Health grade bonus: A/A- theses get +2%
- Matching: thesis_connection field or action text substring match
- Cap: -15% to +15%
- **Coverage:** 34.7% of proposed actions (17/49) have thesis momentum signal
- **Max multiplier observed:** 1.10 (most theses have high momentum currently)

### L50-51: Portfolio Health Multiplier
**Function:** `portfolio_health_multiplier(action_row)`
- 6 factors: health status (Red/Yellow/Green), ops priority (P0/P1), overdue obligations, check-in cadence overdue, fumes date proximity, action due date proximity
- Red health: +8%, Yellow: +4%. P0 ops: +5%, P1: +2%
- Overdue obligations: +4% per obligation (cap +10%)
- Check-in cadence overdue: proportional boost up to +8%
- Fumes date < 90 days: +8% (company running out of runway)
- Action due date past due: +6%
- Cap: max 25% boost
- **Coverage:** 22.4% of proposed actions (11/49) have portfolio health signal
- **Max multiplier observed:** 1.06 (AuraML with P1 ops priority)

### L52-53: Score Trend Analysis
**Table:** `score_history` (auto-populated, 10-snapshot rolling window per action)
**Functions:**
- `snapshot_scores()` — captures current scores, 1-hour throttle, auto-cleanup to 10 per action
- `score_trend(action_id)` — returns trend direction (RISING/FALLING/STABLE), change_pct, volatility, min/max/avg, full history
- `scoring_velocity()` — system-wide: per-type trends, top 5 risers/fallers, overall direction
- `refresh_action_scores()` updated to auto-snapshot after each refresh
- **Initial snapshot:** 144 history records captured

### L54-55: Intelligence Report + Expanded Regression

- Top-20 actions with explanations (via explain_score)
- Health metrics (10/10)
- Factor distribution averages
- New multiplier impact stats (coverage, avg/max multipliers, actions boosted)
- Type breakdown with avg scores and confidence
- Confidence stats (avg 0.80, 81.6% high-confidence)
- Velocity analysis
- Auto-generated recommendations

## Model Architecture (v3.2-L55)

```
Final Score = sigmoid(Base_Score x Combined_Mult x Time_Decay)

Base_Score = 1 + 9 * weighted_sum(7 z-score-normalized factors)
  Factors: time(0.115), conviction(0.17), stakeholder(0.17), effort(0.115),
           novelty(0.08), irgi(0.15), strategic(0.20)

Combined_Mult = product of 13 multipliers, capped [0.5, 1.8]:
  1. priority_mult      (P0: 1.15, P1: 1.07, P3: 0.90)
  2. type_mult           (portfolio: 1.15, pipeline: 1.12, thesis: 0.82-0.92)
  3. source_mult         (Cindy: 1.08, ContentAgent: 1.02)
  4. network_mult        (Core: 1.12, High: 1.08, Medium: 1.04)
  5. depth_mult          (grade 4: 1.15, grade 1: 0.93)
  6. freshness_mult      (exponential decay on age)
  7. interaction_mult    (recency boost, 5 strategies, 53% coverage)
  8. obligation_mult     (overdue obligations urgency)
  9. preference_mult     (historical accept/dismiss patterns)
  10. acceptance_mult    (per-type acceptance rate)
  11. cindy_mult    [NEW] (M8 intelligence score + financial signals)
  12. thesis_mom_mult [NEW] (M6 thesis momentum + health grade)
  13. portfolio_mult [NEW] (health + ops_prio + obligations + cadence + fumes)

Time_Decay = 0.97^max(0, days_old - 14)
Sigmoid bounds: soft-cap at 1.0 and 9.0
```
