## L41-42: Interaction-Aware Intelligence

### `find_companies_with_active_deals(p_days DEFAULT 30)`
- **Purpose:** Returns companies with recent interactions containing deal signals (funding, valuations, term sheets)
- **Data sources:** `interactions.deal_signals` + `interactions.relationship_signals` + `entity_connections` (thesis) + `portfolio` (health)
- **Test result:** 18 companies with active deals in 90-day window
  - Active deals: Kilrr, Quivly (WhatsApp deal groups)
  - Seed rounds: Avii ($2-3M), DubDub ($500K), Levocred ($2M)
  - Angel: Cultured Computers ($150-300K at $30M cap)
  - Portfolio monitoring: AuraML, Intract, Skyra
  - 5 portfolio companies identified among active deals
- **Latency:** 110ms (PASS, under 200ms threshold)

### `relationship_strength_score(p_person_id, p_company_id DEFAULT NULL)`
- **Purpose:** Composite score from interaction frequency, signal richness, entity connection strength, obligations
- **Scoring model:**
  - 30% interaction frequency (0-10 scaled, 5+ interactions = max)
  - 25% entity connection strength (0-1 from entity_connections)
  - 20% recency (decays over 90 days)
  - 15% signal richness (deal + relationship signals present)
  - 10% obligations
- **Grades:** A (>=7), B (>=5), C (>=3), D (>=1), F (<1)
- **Test result:** Person 259 (Mohit Gupta) → Levocred: 6.5 (B - Active), deal signals present
- **Latency:** 3.6ms (PASS)

## L43-44: Portfolio Intelligence Graph

### `portfolio_intelligence_map(p_limit DEFAULT 200)`
- **Purpose:** "God view" of entire portfolio — per-company intelligence score, thesis connections, interactions, obligations, actions, deals, key people
- **Columns:** portfolio_id, company_name, fund, health, stage, entry_cheque, valuation, ownership, intelligence_score, thesis connections, interaction count, obligations, pending actions, active deals (JSONB), key people (JSONB), freshness grade, attention_needed flag + reason
- **Intelligence score model:** content depth (0-3) + entity connections (0-3) + people connections (0-2) + thesis links (0-2) = 0-10
- **Freshness grades:** FRESH (7d), RECENT (30d), AGING (90d), STALE (>90d)
- **Test result (142 companies):**
  - Average intelligence score: 6.3/10
  - Attention needed: 137/142 (96%) — mostly "No recent interactions" (stale freshness)
  - Health: Green 84, Yellow 33, Red 18, NA 7
  - Freshness: FRESH 3, RECENT 2, AGING 0, STALE 137
  - Top intelligence: Apptile (8.3), Atica (6.9), Confido Health (6.1)
- **Latency:** 16.9ms for top 20 (PASS)

## L47-48: Predictive Intelligence

### `predict_next_actions(p_thesis_id, p_limit DEFAULT 5)`
- **Purpose:** Based on current evidence state, interactions, and entity graph, predicts what actions Aakash should take next for a thesis
- **Prediction types (5 categories):**
  1. **evidence_gap** — Detects confirmation bias (FOR evidence >> AGAINST). Suggests contrarian research.
  2. **key_question** — Open key questions from thesis_threads.key_questions_json become predicted research actions.
  3. **company_coverage** — Companies connected to thesis with no recent interactions.
  4. **network_outreach** — People connected to thesis but not recently engaged.
  5. **cross_thesis** — Theses sharing companies for cross-pollination.
- **Test result (thesis=Cybersecurity):** 12 predictions generated
  - Evidence gap detected (FOR >> AGAINST ratio)
  - 3 key questions surfaced (StepSecurity expansion, MCP attack surface, ARR timeline)
  - 3 company coverage gaps (StepSecurity, Clam, PodArmor — no recent interactions)
  - 3 network outreach suggestions (Ashwini Prabhu, Anshul Paul, Nikhil Prabhakar)
  - 2 cross-thesis links (AI-Native Non-Consumption shares 278 companies, Healthcare AI shares 253)
- **Latency:** 20.1ms (PASS)

### `detect_emerging_signals(p_days DEFAULT 14)`
- **Purpose:** Finds entities whose activity spiked recently — new deal flow, active negotiations, sudden engagement
- **Signal types:**
  - **new_entrant:** First appearance in interactions within window
  - **activity_spike:** 3x+ baseline activity
  - **sustained_activity:** Consistent recent activity
  - **engagement_spike:** Person with 2x+ interaction increase
- **Test result (30-day window):** 21 signals
  - 15 company signals (mostly new entrants given young interaction data)
  - High urgency signals with deal signals: Skyra, Avii, Intract, Kilrr, MSC Fund, Ditto Labs
  - Function correctly identifies deal-signal-bearing interactions as high urgency
- **Latency:** 3.8ms (PASS)
