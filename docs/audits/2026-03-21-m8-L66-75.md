## What Was Built

### L66-67: `cindy_obligation_full_context(obligation_id)` — EA-Ready Tool
Returns EVERYTHING about an obligation in one call:
- Obligation details (type, status, due date, source quote, priority)
- Person profile (via `person_communication_profile()`)
- Portfolio connection (ownership %, health, follow-on decision, cash runway)
- Company context (deal status, pipeline, sector, thesis connections)
- Source interaction (the meeting/chat that spawned the obligation)
- Full interaction history with this person (chronological)
- Similar obligations (same person + same category resolved/dismissed)
- Linked action context (action queue item status + scores)
- **EA briefing** — intelligent one-paragraph narrative like:
  > "Ayush Sharma — Founder, AuraML. PORTFOLIO FOUNDER of AuraML. You own 2.22%. Obligations here reflect on your brand as an investor. Overdue by 8 days. Needs immediate action or explicit deprioritization. YOU owe this: 'Provide investor endorsement to Siddharth at Schneider Electric Ventures for AuraML'. Your credibility is at stake on this one."

**Performance:** 27ms

### L68-69: `cindy_interaction_pattern_data(person_id)` — Pattern Analysis
Returns interaction-level intelligence:
- All interactions chronologically with full signal data
- Frequency analysis: avg gap days, channel diversity, engagement trend
- Sentiment signals per interaction: enthusiasm (POSITIVE/NEUTRAL/NEGATIVE), relationship warmth (WARM/COOL), action density
- **Portfolio founder benchmark**: avg interactions, channels, deal signal % for portfolio founders
- **Passed company benchmark**: avg interactions for contacts at companies user passed on
- **Pattern assessment** narrative:
  > "Mohit Gupta: Interaction volume matches portfolio founder baseline. Engagement: ACCELERATING. Every conversation has deal substance. Single-channel (granola) — relationship depth uncertain."

### L70-71: Obligation Actionability
**Schema changes:**
- Added `suggested_action TEXT` column to obligations
- Added `suggested_action_options JSONB` column to obligations

**Function: `generate_obligation_suggestions(obligation_id)`**
Produces structured, context-aware action options:

| Action | When Generated | Context Example |
|--------|---------------|-----------------|
| REMOVE | Always available | "Overdue by 16 days. Not every commitment is worth keeping." |
| ACT_NOW | I_OWE_THEM, active | "This is a portfolio founder. Your brand depends on follow-through." (0.95 confidence) |
| FOLLOW_UP | THEY_OWE_ME, overdue | "A brief 'hey, any update on X?' is appropriate." |
| RESCHEDULE | I_OWE, overdue, <0.9 priority | "Not urgent enough today, but don't want to drop it." |
| DELEGATE | I_OWE, delegatable categories | "Have Rajat or team handle the intro." |

Persists primary suggestion + all options on the obligation row for WebFront rendering.

**Current suggestion distribution (14 obligations):**
- ACT_NOW: 8 (all I_OWE_THEM with portfolio or overdue context)
- REMOVE: 4 (THEY_OWE_ME stale >14 days)
- FOLLOW_UP: 1 (THEY_OWE_ME overdue, recoverable)

### L72-73: `cindy_person_intelligence(person_id)` — Person Package
Comprehensive package for WebFront /comms expanded view:

- Pattern assessment narrative
- Sentiment signals timeline
- All obligations with suggestions
- Interaction timeline with spawned obligations + actions
- Entity connections (companies, people, theses)
- Relationship graph (strength, trend, channels)
- Related actions in queue

- **EA summary** paragraph:
  > "Rajat Agarwal (VC Partner). Communication: GOOD (7.80/10). Trend: warming. NEEDS_ATTENTION. You owe 1 thing(s), they owe you 0. Last contact: 0 days ago. Engagement: ACCELERATING."

**Performance:** 98ms
