### Skild AI’s Approach to Robotics

- Hyper research-focused robotics company

  - 10+ PhD researchers from founders Deepak and Abhinav’s circles

  - Founders are established professors in robotics/AI/computer vision

  - Software-only approach - models can operate on any hardware

- Core philosophy: Single generalized model across three axes

  - Same model running across different robot morphologies

  - Same model performing different tasks

  - Same robot working in different environments

- Three core robotics problems being tackled:

  - Locomotion (considered solved - 2 years progress industry-wide)

  - Navigation (unsolved, very difficult - SLAM systems failed previously)

  - Manipulation (challenging due to hardware limitations)

- Platform agnostic approach

  - Same model works on bipedal humanoids, quadrupeds, propeller-based robots

  - Action space and actuation method irrelevant

  - Example: Humanoid training model successfully operated quadruped without fine-tuning
