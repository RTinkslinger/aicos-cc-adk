## Latest deck 2025-09-03 

**Aptile (Tile)** describes itself as **"The OS for the Agentic Workforce"** — a platform for AI agents to collaboratively build and ship **production-grade apps**.

Their central vision is to enable **software manufacturing by fleets of AI agents**, starting with mobile app development.

---

**📊 Key Business Metrics**

**Current Snapshot (as of the deck)**

- **ARR:** $1M

- **Apps Launched:** 500+

- **Active Users:** 3M+

- **Agents Live:** 5+ specialized AI agents available in the Agent Store

- **Enterprise Certifications:** SOC2, ISO, GDPR compliant

- **Focus Vertical:** Mobile apps (scalable and compliance-ready infrastructure)

**Market Opportunity**

- **Total Addressable Market (TAM):** $10B (AI dev tools for enterprise apps)

- **Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM):** $1.9B

- **Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM, 5-year goal):** $180M

- **Market validation:** 90% of Fortune 100 companies are using AI coding tools.

---

**🧠 Product & Technology**

**Agentic Core (p.5)**

Tile operates through a **multi-agent architecture**, mimicking a human dev team:

1. **Planner Agent:** Creates design and execution plans.

2. **Specialist Agents:** Build modular "Tiles" (functional components).

3. **Verification + Orchestration Agents:** Assemble and validate the final app.

These agents leverage integrations with **Stripe, Shopify, Twilio, HubSpot, Supabase, OpenAI, and Google Analytics**.

---

**🧰 Platform Features**

**Human-Supervised Agentic Development (p.6)**

- **Purpose-built AI Agents:** 95% of generations pass automated tests (vs. 60% for generic LLMs).

- **Visual Builder:** 200+ reusable tiles, real-time preview, and one-click publishing.

- **Mobile Stack:** Push notifications, in-app purchases (IAP), CMS, and builds ready to scale.

**Developer-First Tooling (p.7)**

- **SDK + CLI:** Export React Native code, edit transparently.

- **AI Productivity:** Context-aware suggestions, debugging, and automated scaffolding.

- **Full-Stack Workflow:** Design → code → test → deploy, all within one stack.

**Enterprise-Ready Infrastructure (p.8)**

- **Security & Compliance:** SOC2 and GDPR-compliant; role-based access control.

- **Deployment Flexibility:** Cloud or on-prem options.

- **Built to Scale:** From startups to enterprise workloads (millions of users).

---

**🚀 Growth Strategy & GTM**

**Three GTM Engines (p.10)**

| Channel | % of SOM | Details | Target by 2030 |

| Prompt-to-Prod PLG | 20% | Free tier (5% conversion), CAC $500, 10-month payback | $36M |

| Agencies / Developers | 40% | 400 agency partners, ARPU $1200, +25 partners/quarter | $72M |

| SaaS White-Label | 40% | 25 vertical SaaS providers, 5-year ramp to $5M per partner | $72M |

**ARR Growth Plan (p.11–13)**

- **Y1:** $1M → $6M (20+ agents, 20+ agencies, 1000+ apps)

- **Y2:** $6M → $18M (100+ agents, 50+ agencies, enterprise pilots)

- **Long-Term:**

- 10M ARR from PLG channel

- 5M ARR from SaaS integrations (Shopify, Marmeto, Shoplazza)

---

**🧬 Proprietary Advantage (p.14–15)**

**Self-Training Agents:**

Every new app built enriches the training data — improving reliability and unlocking enterprise use cases.

**Platform Roadmap:**

- **Now:** 5 agents

- **Q4 2025:** 20+ agents (including Stripe, CMS, CRM, Inventory)

- **2026:** 1000+ agents + "Agent Forge" (auto-generates new agents in <48h)

- **Expansion:** Move beyond mobile to web apps and vertical SaaS integrations (Microsoft, Salesforce, SAP).
