- **Washing Machine Descaler** emerging as a new hero SKU

- **Chennai warehouse** operations started — expected to reduce logistics costs and improve CM1 over coming quarters

- **Q-commerce** (Zepto, BigBasket) in setup phase; aggressive push planned from Mar–Apr



**Funding**

- Planning to close next round by **Feb 2026** with Sharrp Ventures, Arjun Vaidya, Priyanka Salot, and Pawan Jaggi joining the cap table



**Outlook (Jan–Mar '26)**

- Target: sustain ₹1 Cr+/month and exit March at ₹1.5 Cr+

- Focus on CM1 improvement via Chennai ops, scaling Washing Machine Descaler, and Q-commerce ramp-up



**Bottom line:** Strong topline momentum (~60% YoY growth in MoM terms) but unit economics remain deeply negative, especially CM2. The Chennai expansion and channel mix optimization are the levers they're banking on for margin improvement.
