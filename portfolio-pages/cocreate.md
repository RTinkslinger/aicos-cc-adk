## Update 2026-03-10T19:57:00.000+05:30 



Hello Everyone,

This month comes with a small team update. Sid has decided to step away from Cocreate and is no longer involved as a co-founder. After some reflection, he realized his long-term interests weren’t as closely aligned with the video production space, and we agreed it made sense for him to pursue other directions.

We’re grateful for the role he played in the early stages of the company and wish him the best moving forward.

The rest of the team remains fully committed to Cocreate and more energized than ever about the opportunity ahead. We’ve been making strong progress on the product and continuing to push forward on our mission. Here are a few updates from the past month:

### Customers

- We have began beta rollout Cocreate to a select group of post-houses and freelance editors, with positive feedback and excitement so far. We’re continuing to expand to more post houses and editors.

### Product

- We made significant improvements in how the system watches and interprets video content.

- Better integration of production data and documentation for more reliable editorial feedback.

- Analysis performance is now up to **2x faster** than the February version.

- We're now compatible with **Adobe Premiere** and **Final Cut Pro**.

- Added new features specifically developed for use in the field to assist production teams in real-time.

### What’s next

- Continue to expand through New York post houses, targeting ~150 editors over the next two months

As always, thank you for the belief, the advice, and the push. We’re excited about the momentum heading into this year and look forward to sharing more as we continue to build.
