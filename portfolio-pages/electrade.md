## Closure Update 2025-10-06 

Dear Investor,

I hope this message finds you well.

After deep reflection and extensive evaluation of our current financial and operational standing, we have taken the difficult decision to **proceed with the closure of Electrade**.

This decision comes in light of **recurring monthly losses**, **growing operational complexities**, and our **inability to secure the next round of funding** required to sustain and scale operations. Despite our collective efforts, the current ecosystem conditions — including the **slower-than-expected pace of government and industry adoption** — have made it increasingly difficult to achieve sustainable growth.

From the very beginning, our ambition was bold: **to transform India’s ESDM ecosystem** and digitize the electronics component supply chain. We always knew the journey would be uphill and that the **probability of success was small**, but the potential impact was too significant not to pursue.

While the **vision remains as strong as ever**, the **realization of it demands massive capital investment**, along with a **mindset shift among vendors** and a deeper **digital transformation in supply chains**. These changes, unfortunately, require a scale of support and patience that exceeds our current resources and market maturity.

On a personal note, I want to sincerely **thank you for your trust, belief, and partnership** throughout this journey. Your support gave us the courage to dream big and attempt something truly transformative for the country.

Though this chapter closes, my commitment to **creating impact, generating value, and contributing to India’s progress** remains unwavering. After a break, I will continue to pursue ventures that create employment, accelerate innovation, and strengthen India’s industrial backbone.

Should you wish to connect further — to discuss Electrade, exchange learnings, or simply catch up on new life adventures — I would be more than happy to do so. Please consider me available for any conversation or assistance that is possible and meaningful.

Once again, thank you for your faith and encouragement. It has been an honour to have you as part of this journey.
