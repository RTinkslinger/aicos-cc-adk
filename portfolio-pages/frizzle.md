## Investor Update 2025-12-02 

**Quick Summary:**

A strong product-building month. We launched three major grading modes, secured four public-school pilots, and deepened workflow adoption across existing teachers. Revenue held steady as we prioritized raising product quality ahead of scaling distribution.

---

### **Metrics**

- **Revenue:** (flat from before; holding steady at **~$70k ARR**)

- **Cash:** **$1.1M**

- **Burn:** **$35k**

- **Runway:** **31 months**

---

### **Asks**

- **Engineering hiring: **Please send us **any leads for exceptional full-stack engineers** (international preferred).

- **Distribution: **If you know **school leaders, principals, superintendents, district admins, or math teachers**, we'd love introductions, especially public schools or high-school math departments.

---

### **Highlights**

- **Launched three new grading modes:**

- **Updated Marking Mode** (faster annotation, clearer teacher UX)

- **AP Mode** (AP Exam style scoring + FRQ support)

- **IGCSE Mode** following the Cambridge marking scheme

- **Four new public-school pilots signed** — major milestone for top-down distribution. All four are actively onboarding and using Frizzle across math departments.

- **Teacher experience upgrades:** significant improvements to accuracy, speed, and rubric clarity across all marking flows.

---

### **Lowlights**

- **ARR growth below expectations. **We paused aggressive sales in favor of improving core product quality. Our belief: sustainable revenue growth requires a tighter, faster, more delightful grading experience, and that became our primary focus first.

---

### **Goals & Priorities for December**

**Full product sprint to lock in quality before the new year.**

- **Launch Rubric Mode:** teachers upload any rubric; Frizzle handles partial credit, structured scoring, and feedback.

- **Integrations:** Canvas, Google Classroom, PowerSchool (LMS + SIS sync).

- **Mobile Scanning App:** drastically lower friction by letting teachers scan worksheets directly from their phone.

- **Prepare for January sales push** once core quality is dialed in.

Thank you for your support and believing in us! Wishing you a happy holidays and a happy new year!
