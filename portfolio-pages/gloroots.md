## Update shared on 2024-10-14 

Wishing you and your family a Happy Dussehra!Sharing our company updates:

**Current Performance**

- ARR: $195K (up from $100K)

- Customers: 28

- Cash on hand: ~$400K

- Inbound leads: 30-35 per month (10-12% conversion rate)



**Market Dynamics and Strategic Evolution**

Our journey in the EOR (Employer of Record) business has provided valuable insights into the market dynamics. Some of the factors that are weighing against us to grow at a considerable pace

- High Customer Acquisition Costs (CAC) in a competitive landscape

- Challenges in customer retention due to market volatility - Churn due to cost optimisation of larger customers by setting up their own entities

- Increasing demand for cost optimization from clients - we do not have an upper hand as we rely on a partner network

- Slowdown in funding globally

These factors have led us to reassess our approach and refine our strategy. Our original vision was to create a full-stack platform for the entire candidate journey, from discovery to separation. As part of this plan, we've always intended to develop AI-powered recruiting tools.

Given the current market conditions and our core strengths, we're accelerating the development of our recruiting AI capabilities while optimizing our EOR operations.



**Optimizing EOR Operations**

We've taken steps to enhance the efficiency of our EOR business:

- Reduced team from 25 to 10 members

- Reduced overall monthly burn to ~$18K

- Reposition EOR as a profitable services business

This optimization allows us to maintain a stable revenue stream (with limited ops and sales resources and the tech we have built), while redirecting resources towards high-growth opportunities.



**Accelerating Recruiting AI Development**

Our tech and product teams are intensifying efforts on developing LLM-based tools and agents to revolutionize the recruiting process. This aligns perfectly with our original vision of a comprehensive candidate management platform.

Key developments:

- Launching a self-serve LLM-based resume parser early November

- Engaging with 3 staffing agencies as potential design partners

- Target market: recruiting agencies and enterprises with large TA teams (100+ people)

**Looking Ahead**

By optimizing our EOR operations and accelerating our recruiting AI development, we're positioning ourselves for sustainable growth in the evolving HR tech landscape. This approach allows us to leverage our existing expertise while tapping into the potential of AI in recruiting.
