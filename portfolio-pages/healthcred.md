## Update 2025-06-04T17:31:00.000+05:30 → Pivot or die



There have been certain challenges that have come up with the lender partners recently which has impacted the original business thesis of working capital financing solutions to hospitals against delayed health insurance claims.

The early signs for that started showing in the month of November itself when the risk weight changes on LoC products (announced by RBI) impacted the lender partner's underwriting process, making the same stricter, with some partners stopping the open market Line of Credit (LoC) and non-anchor backed Supply chain financing solution altogether.
