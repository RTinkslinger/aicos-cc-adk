<details><summary>AI synthesis of deck</summary></details>

**Hotelzify** is a vertical AI platform empowering hotels through direct bookings in a market where only 15% of bookings happen through hotel websites, while OTAs like [Booking.com](http://booking.com/) and Expedia capture 45% of the $950 billion global hotel booking market. Hotels currently pay up to $75 billion annually (around 20% of revenue) to OTAs, and a typical 100-room hotel loses $200K yearly from missed calls alone.



The platform addresses three critical pain points: **outdated booking systems** (85% bounce rate, 36% of hotels lack websites), **ineffective marketing strategies** (hotels spend less than 2.5% of revenue on marketing vs OTAs at 40-50%), and **high operational inefficiencies** (1 in 5 calls unanswered, 67% of hospitality roles remain open).



**Hotelzify's Vertical AI OS** consists of three integrated engines: the **AI Website** (personalized retargeting, search-to-checkout on one platform, Google optimization), the **AI Marketing Engine** (multi-platform zero-touch campaigns, real-time analytics, profitable direct marketing), and the **AI Sales Concierge** (24/7 AI-powered chat and voice engagement that converts drop-offs into bookings). These deliver outcomes including 50% increase in organic traffic, 40% increase in conversion rate, 30% lower marketing spend, $200K revenue boost per hotel, and 40% improvement in ROAS.



The company has achieved **$1M in signed ARR** as of July 2025, with recent deals including Fern Hotel Group ($120K ARR), Treebo ($120K), Ownia USA Luxury ($108K), Olive Hotels by Embassy ($50K), and Elivaas ($50-100K). The pipeline for the next quarter stands at $550K+ ARR across India, US, Middle East, and the AAHOA association. Notable customers include Club Mahindra, OYO, Best Western, Howard Johnson, and multiple regional brands.



Their **ICP** targets hotel chains ranging from 20-1,000 properties (average ACV $202K-$2.1M) and vacation rental chains of 20-1,000+ properties (average ACV $235K-$2.3M), representing a combined addressable market opportunity exceeding $1 trillion in gross booking value.



The **GTM strategy** for the next 24 months includes activating outbound sales via Apollo and LinkedIn, launching city-based microsites across US/Europe/Middle East, running LinkedIn ABM campaigns to 1,000+ chains, and building channel partnerships with PMSs and RMCs. The team plans to hire 5 AEs, 2 SDRs, 2 marketing managers, 1 BD ops, and 3 hospitality tech consultants, with $530K allocated for events (ITB, HITEC, BITAC, AAHOACON) and digital marketing. This is projected to drive **$12M ARR** through 64 small logos ($50K ACV), 24 mid-market logos ($100K ACV), 10 larger deals ($200K ACV), and $1.3M from channel partnerships.



The **business model** is commission-based with no upfront costs: 4-8% per booking on the booking engine, 2-8% per booking on marketing, and fixed fee plus 3% on the AI Sales Concierge. This outcome-as-a-service model aligns incentives so Hotelzify only makes money when hotels do. Customer reviews show a 4.8/5 rating on HotelTechReport with 97% recommendation from limited service/budget hotels and 96% from branded hotels.



The company is **raising $2.1M** ($1.4M for sales/marketing, $0.7M for tech/product) for 24 months runway to reach $10M ARR, with existing investors committing to participate. The outcome will be access to high-value markets in US, Middle East, and Europe with enhanced global brand credibility.



**Founders** are Anirudh Ganesh (CEO), who was Lead PM at Perpule (acquired by Amazon), and Unnikrishnan CJ (CTO), who was founding engineer at Yulu and CTO at FarmNet. Both are NITK Surathkal alumni.
