We are writing to share an update regarding Bantr and the path ahead for our team.

After careful consideration, we decided to shut down Bantr in early January. As you may have observed across the InfoFi landscape, recent API restrictions and policy shifts from X have created unsustainable structural constraints. These dependencies make it impossible to scale the current model responsibly, and we believe winding down now is the right decision to preserve resources and focus.

### The Current State of the Team

To remain lean, we have reduced our headcount. Our remaining team of 7 is currently focused on two pillars:

- **Authena:** A few team members are dedicated to the continued growth of this product. If growth and revenue do not sustain, we will reevaluate our position in three months.

- **Exploration:** The remaining members are supporting existing clients while aggressively researching our next major problem statement.

### Our Focus Areas

We are currently focusing our research on the Institutional and SMB sectors, specifically:

- **Capital Flow & Investment:** How SMBs and Family Offices move money and manage their investment portfolios.

- **Complaince** : We already have Authena clients whom we can upsell other services.

- **Agentic Payments:** Exploring the intersection of AI agents and payment rails.

### Our Ask of You

We believe the best way to move forward is to leverage the collective expertise of our investors. While we are deep-diving into the sectors mentioned above, we remain open to exploring other spaces based on your insights.

**If there is a specific vertical, high-impact problem statement, or market gap you believe our team should explore**, we would value your perspective immensely. We are ready to build, but we want to ensure our next direction is informed by the best possible market intelligence.

Thank you for your continued support through this transition. We look forward to hearing your thoughts.
