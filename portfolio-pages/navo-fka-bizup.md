## Update 2025-12-11T21:42:00.000+05:30 from founders



Here is an update on our journey over last few months



**Bizup -> Navo**

We pivoted, and relaunched as Navo in May 2025. Same space (fashion wholesale), same users (retailers beyond Tier 1 cities), different promise and business model.

- Navo adds 5,000+ fast-selling designs each month, identified using our AI powered data models

- Retailers browse on Nao app and and order via a LIVE video call

- We provide a unique 30 day sale guarantee - free exchange if the retailer is not able to sell our products within 30 days

- We have set up a tech-enabled supply chain with enables us to operate with zero credit and limited inventory

Think of it like Meesho for retailers.



**Numbers**

- Revenue at Rs 1.1. cr in Nov '25, growing at 45% mom over last 4 months

- 40% of buyers buy again within 60 days, with AOV 1.7x of 1st order

- For power users, revenue retention of ~100% over next 3 cycles (60 days periods)

- Target for next 6 months: Grow to 10 cr+ monthly revenue, with 50%+ power users



**Fundraise**

Plan was to hit the market in Jan for a raise. Post discussions with existing investors (India Quotient), we thought it might be good to do a quick bridge so that we can maintain the growth momentum and then raise in Sept-Oct next year when we are at Rs 10-15 cr/ monthly revenue, with more cohort data.



**We have signed a term sheet with IQ for a raise of Rs 15 cr. Rs 10 cr at Rs 100 cr post money valuation + Rs 5 cr convertible at 25% discount to next round.**
