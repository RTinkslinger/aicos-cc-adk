## May 2024 update



Sharing update for May's business.

To influence sales in unaided stores, we have experimented with 1) Remote skincare consulting and 2) Focused sampling in select stores.

Our remote skincare consulting hasnt worked. Customers, women (18-30 y.o) weren't comfortable discussing their concerns in a retail environment & our efforts to keep it discreet wasnt of much help.

Focused sampling is happening in select stores and we believe it will take an overall 30-45 period for us to get data on its success/failure.

Given we're testing on growth levers, we've not expanded aided store count. Our business numbers broadly remain the same as last month.

- Count of brands: 19 (+1 vs las month)

- Count of retail: 145 (same as last month)

- May GMV: INR 12.92 L

- Revenue: INR 7.54 L

- Team: 11 (Core team) + 17 (Promoters/BA).

**Financials**:

- Cash on account: INR 2.72 Cr on 7th May.

- Last month's burn: INR 5.1 L

We're also exploring adjacent categories that require offline distribution and possible opportunities to leverage our existing network of retailers.







## June 2024 update

Below is ofScale's June Business Update.

Summary:

Our best sales-driving formula so far, aided stores, at peak sales will need **existing brands sharing more commissions** (>70%, unsustainable for brands) or **better performing brands **in sales. Both of these are limiting for ofScale. On the other hand, unaided stores were ineffective in driving sales for brands given their inadequate awareness.

Way Ahead: We are seeing tailwinds for a few product categories X price points. We are exploring a few experiments in this direction. Success will mean a playbook for brands to emulate and scale with ofScale. Period: 45-60 days.

2. Is there any reason for this decline?

---



A bunch of retailers onboarded 8-10 months back and who had stocks (GMV and revenue recorded in those months), have not been able to sell those and we'd done a collection or stock-return drive last month. Stock returns resulted in lower **net** GMV & Revenue in June.

We're not actively onboarding brands because we're unable to figure out the economics of making a brand sustainably work offline even with our current portfolio - which is fairly representative of brands across the spectrum. Our goal right now is to make unit(store) economics work.

## December 2024 update

Dear Investors,

Over the last two months, we've conclusively decided that we cannot meet the minimum required **brand revenue per store** metric. Our efforts to increase it via interventions fell flat and at this moment, we also believe we're ill-equipped for demand generation. It is largely a function of brand awareness which the brands are better positioned to influence.

Given this, we're **considering a hard pivot**. In this process, we've started scaling down ofScale operations and hence the revenues will fall to zero from January.

A small core team will continue and we'll be using this time to explore other business ideas and build conviction.

Regards

Suvigna, ofScale
