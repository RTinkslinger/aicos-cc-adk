**What They Do**

AI-powered equity research platform deploying custom AI agents for buy-side research workflows. Focused exclusively on public equities.

**Problem & Solution**

Equity research analysts wrestle with generic horizontal AI tools that add hours vs. remove them. Pinegap embeds tailored AI agents directly into analyst workflows to save time and expand stock coverage.

**Competitive Positioning**

Never lost a deal to newer AI players (Rogo, Hebbia, ModelML).
