### Summary (Aug 2025 – Feb 2026)

Polytrade positions the last six months as a shift from being primarily an RWA marketplace to becoming an *infrastructure participant*, with deeper ecosystem integrations, a major staking-driven utility moment for $TRADE, and a broader multi-asset RWA catalogue (real estate, equities, commodities, fine art, and music royalties).

### What changed / key milestones

- **Multi-chain + distribution**

- **BNB Chain integration (Aug 2025)** with $TRADE bridging and PancakeSwap liquidity.

- **Monad testnet integration (Sep 2025)** with initial projects onboarded.

- **Binance Wallet integration (Nov 2025)** and listing as an official dApp, framed as a major distribution and credibility step.

- **Integra Layer (Dec 2025): deeper infra commitment**

- Joined **Integra Layer Consortium** as **Lead Development Anchor** (real estate–focused L1).

- Launched **$TRADE staking** on Polygon, BNB, and Ethereum, earning “Integra Layer XP”, with funds locked until $IRL TGE.

- Reported **~16.5M $TRADE staked** within days (about **~40%** of circulating supply).

- **Confirmed $IRL allocation** for $TRADE holders tied to Polytrade’s consortium role.

- **Validator node operations** funded with treasury $TRADE to earn validator rewards.
