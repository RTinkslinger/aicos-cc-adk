## October Business Update 2025-11-04 

This was a really important month for us,

**perhaps one of the most defining since launch.**

We had to prove 3 big things: can we scale on new acquisitions, close our fund-raise smoothly and get back to building, get our Elite pricing strategy right.

**We made meaningful progress on all the 3 fronts.**

*1. New user acquisition has bounced back really strong*

Context : New user growth had slowed a bit last month(we were flat in Sep vs Aug at 40K per month),

**so this one was really critical.**

**The good news — we bounced back and how :**

- Users grew from 40K to 97K in October (~150% MoM growth)

- On track for 1.2L in November and 1.5L+ in December

- CAC is also stabilising around ₹170,

**a strong validation of the large TAM  and resonance of what we’re building.**

*2. Fundraise closed smoothly and as desired*

Context : We were double-guessing if we were early for the raise and hitting the market pre-maturely. And coupled with acquisition numbers this had intensified my worries a bit.

**Good part is it worked out well and the round came together faster than expected.**

The timely and $12M fund-raise gives us enough firepower to scale over the next 12 months and aim for $5M ARR and 2M customers and beyond.

**We are now well positioned to become India's preferred investment advisory app!**

Huge thanks to all of you for your support and belief in us through this process.*3. Elite Pricing : Choosing Margin vs Momentum*

This was** A BIG LEARNING CURVE.**

Context : We had launched Elite at ₹999, and promised this for the first 10K members. We thought of using this as an opportunity to move to ₹1,499 post Diwali thinking it would drive better monetisation. Our expectation was to see ~80% Day 1 conversions and 1.2x revenue initially, ramping to 90% conversion and 1.3x revenue over a few months.

**This plan failed. **From 22nd-30th October our conversions dropped to 55-60% or previous numbers.

**We were torn between holding on to the new pricing OR ensuring our momentum doesn't break. **After a lot of internal debate, **we chose momentum over margin : prioritising scale, user base, and network effects over near-term revenue lift.**

Especially that we have such a high quality user with world class retention and engagement, it was the right thing to do. We estimate making ₹5-6K/year from elite members in steady state and this optimisation of ₹500 didn't seem right at the cost of breaking momentum.

**And that turned out to be the right call.**We reverted to ₹999 from November 1st, and **the response has been phenomenal:**- 900 new Elite members added in the first 3 days of November- On track to add 7,500 new Elites and cross $1M ARR this month- We aim to be at 2L Elite members and $5M ARR over the next 12 months

Thanks Aakash for sharing his learnings from scaling Hotstar subscription business and helping us validate our thought process.

*4. Near term business goals and focus areas*

While we continue to focus on Elite and improving Elite conversions aiming for 12%, we’ve also started work on identifying the next big bet.

Over the next 6–9 months, not only will we launch PowerUp Infinite (our managed advisory service) but likely one more business line to diversify our revenues and make the business stronger.

It’s been a big month for us with lots of learning, and strong execution by the team.
