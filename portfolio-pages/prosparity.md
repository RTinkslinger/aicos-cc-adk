## Company Overview

**Prosparity** is an electric vehicle financing platform focused on 3-wheelers in Madhya Pradesh, India. The company was founded by Saurabh, who has a background in vehicle financing but is new to this specific customer segment.

## Current Performance (as of July 2025)

- **Scale**: ₹8Cr+ in loans disbursed across 450+ loans

- **Operations**: 12+ locations in MP, covering 59%+ of the E3W market

- **Partnerships**: 15+ OEM partnerships, 65+ dealers onboarded

- **Team**: 35+ members (30+ in MP, 6 in Mumbai)

- **Financial Metrics**: Average ticket size ₹1.5L, 29% IRR, 27-month tenure

- **Collections**: Maintaining 100% T+15 collections

## Business Model

- **Phygital approach**: Digital origination with offline risk assessment and collections

- **Target market**: Electric 3-wheeler financing in tier-2/3 cities

- **Differentiation**: Fast TAT (same day to T+3 settlement), strong dealer relationships

- **Revenue**: 30%+ IRR with secured lending model

## Key Strengths

- Strong operational execution with consistent month-over-month growth

- Secured ₹200Cr+ debt capital commitments from multiple lenders

- Good market penetration (26%+ wallet share at partner dealers)

- Experienced on-ground team with 5+ years in vehicle financing

## Key Concerns & Risks

- **Founder fit**: Limited experience with this customer segment (50%+ NTC customers)

- **Market challenges**: Unclear resale potential for EVs, tough customer segment

- **Capital raising**: Described as "poor capital raiser"

- **Limited differentiation**: Mainly competing on speed rather than unique value proposition

- **Regulatory**: Still awaiting NBFC license (expected by April 2025, now delayed)

## Investment Status

- **DeVC Position**: Early investor with ₹75K entry cheque

- **Current Assessment**: Rated as "Maybe" for $500K follow-on

- **Health Status**: Green

- **Follow-on Priority**: P2

- **Investment Recommendation**: Weak maybe - concerns about founder-market fit and segment attractiveness

## Future Plans

- Targeting ₹120Cr+ annual disbursals by September 2025

- Planning $7M Series A fundraise

- Expansion to 2 states and launch of E-2W, E-L5 vehicles

- NBFC license expected soon

## Bottom Line

Prosparity shows strong operational execution and market traction but faces questions about founder-market fit and the attractiveness of the EV financing segment. The company has good momentum but operates in a challenging market with limited differentiation beyond execution speed.
