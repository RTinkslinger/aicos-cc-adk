We’d love to continue this journey with our original backers, but we also want to be fair to everyone. ### AI notes

[Pooch.ai](http://pooch.ai/) is built on four core principles for success in the Indian market:

- Completely free for consumers (acknowledging Indians rarely pay for consumer tech)

- Available on WhatsApp (the most pervasive platform in India)

- Multi-language support for all Indian languages

- Voice capabilities for accessibility (as not everyone is literate or comfortable typing)

The company's engineering team has focused on creating something beyond simple fine-tuning, with significant technical work to handle Indian languages effectively.
