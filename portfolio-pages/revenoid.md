## Revenoid — company summary

**What it is:** Revenoid is an **end-to-end, AI-native “system of intelligence” for enterprise GTM teams**. The pitch is to **redefine outbound GTM with AI** without forcing enterprises to stitch together “10 new tools” or retrain teams—embedding intelligence across existing workflows and tools (Salesforce + Salesloft/Outreach + Gong/Chorus). 


**Who they sell to (ICP):** Enterprises with **outbound-led sales motions**, typically **$50K+ ACV (often 6–7 figures)**, **ARR $50M+ (mostly $100M+)**, complex product suites, and established enablement + tooling (Salesforce, Salesloft/Outreach, Gong/Chorus). 




**Core product/modules:**

- **Account prioritization + scoring + deep account research** (“UrgencyIQ”)

- **Custom RAG on MongoDB** for proprietary analysis over large enterprise data

- **Tool-agnostic AI messaging orchestration** (customer-facing + internal)

- **Agentic lead identification + enrichment**

- **“Data plane” positioning** to let customers swap other tools while keeping intelligence in place

- **Per-customer fine-tuned SLMs** to reduce cost and “co-own” a model trained on their data

- **Salesforce admin workflow agents (beta)** + an “FDE approach” to design/deploy end-to-end AI workflows



**Claimed wedge / why they win:** They’re positioning against point solutions (Clay, Regie, Actively, etc.) by delivering **custom, enterprise-grade workflows fast**, plus deeper integration into existing GTM systems. The deck emphasizes speed of custom delivery (e.g., building a complex custom agent for a customer in days) and enterprise sales execution. 



**Traction (from deck)**

**Customers:** Named early customers include **Pinnacle Group (5K+ employees; renewed with 3x expansion)**, **Jellyfish**, **ASAPP (renewed)**, **HireEz (renewed)**, **Lucidworks**, **VWO**, **Veritext**, and others. 


**Pipeline:** Deck shows **$1.028M current pipeline**, including items like SentinelOne (POC won, $75K) and multiple $50–75K opps, plus “15 more.” 


**Usage metrics:** Claims include **500K accounts scored/researched**, **1M messages generated**, **$75M+ pipeline impacted**, and **50M+ pages researched**. 


**ARR curve:** A chart shows ARR rising over recent quarters to roughly **~$1M+ run-rate** by Q4 (visual in the deck). 


**Team & backing**

**Founders / leadership:** CEO **Rabi Gupta** (3rd-time founder; 1 exit; built profitable AI startups), **Satwick Saxena** (CGO; Hotstar, Leo Burnett; scaled Evabot to $4M ARR), **Ashish Kumar** (CTO; 1 exit; scaled AI products to 2M+ MAU), plus enterprise sales and ML leadership.
