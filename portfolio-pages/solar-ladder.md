**Company Overview**

Solar Ladder is a B2B full-stack platform empowering solar installers (EPCs) in India's rooftop solar market. They've built an integrated ecosystem combining SaaS, procurement, and financing to digitize and support the entire lifecycle of rooftop solar deployments.

**Three-Pillar Business Model**

1. **SaaS Platform**: End-to-end project management tool with 3D design, lead tracking, proposal creation, remote monitoring & O&M

- 500+ clients, 1.3 lakh+ projects created, 345+ MW designed

2. **Procurement & Supply**: B2B marketplace for solar components serving three segments:

- Small EPCs (residential/retail)

- Warehouse operations (launched 300 sq ft in Pune)

- Large EPCs (C&I projects - drives 90%+ of revenue)

- 6-7% gross margins, 10% ROCE

3. **Finance**: Two-pronged approach

- Working capital financing for EPCs via NBFC partners (₹45-50 Cr cumulative AUM)

- End-customer solar loans (₹1.2 Cr monthly disbursement, ₹11 Cr+ total)

- Planning own NBFC license post-Series A to capture 4-5% NIMs

**Traction & Financials**

- **Revenue**: Q3 FY25 at ₹18.8 Cr (up from ₹5.4 Cr in Q1 FY24)

- **ARR**: ₹100 Cr

- **Projects**: ~60 MW, 500+ orders

- **Profitability**: EBITDA+ in Q4 FY24, 4% CM3

- **Capital efficiency**: 8x capital turnover ratio, only ₹40 lakh monthly burn vs ₹8 Cr for heavily capitalized competitor

- **Wallet share**: 11% median

**Market Opportunity**

- India targeting 100 GW cumulative rooftop solar by 2030 (currently 11 GW installed)

- TAM of $260 Bn for B2B marketplace, $200 Bn for financing

- SAM of $7 Bn and $5 Bn respectively (2040 horizon)

**Team**

- **Founders**: Manan Mehta & Abhishek Pillai (ex-Unergia co-founders)

- **Core team**: Experienced hires from Waaree (10 yrs), OfBusiness/HDFC (10 yrs), [Sell.Do](http://sell.do/) (10 yrs), CSTEP/ETH Zurich (7 yrs)

- Team size: 27 people

**Fundraising**

- **Current round**: Raising $4M

- 54% for working capital

- 16% for NBFC license

- 14% for setting up NBFC

- 6% for tech development

- 10% buffer

- **Previous round**: ₹10 Cr seed at ₹5 Cr post-money valuation

- In discussions with Rockefeller Capital, Rising Tide (potential lead at ~$3M), Axilor ($1.5M follow-on)

**Key Differentiators**

- Full-stack solution vs fragmented competitors

- Capital efficient growth (8x capital turnover vs 2.5x for most capitalized competitor)

- SaaS-led customer acquisition driving cross-sell into procurement and finance

- Higher LTV/CAC and retention rates vs competitors

**Go-Forward Strategy**

- Scale SaaS to 1,700 EPCs by FY27, 2,500 by decade-end

- Expand warehousing to 5-6 locations in Tier II/III cities

- Launch NBFC by FY26-27

- Scale end-customer loans to ₹7.5 Cr/quarter by FY25-26

- Roll out remote monitoring in 2 phases (WiFi-based, then standalone IoT devices)
