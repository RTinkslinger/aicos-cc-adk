## Monthly Update 2025-03-25T13:20:00.000+05:30 



**Steady Progress, Big Steps Forward**

Hey everyone,

We’re excited to share our latest progress on MOKSHA as we move closer to the Alpha build. The last couple of months have been all about refining the experience, strengthening the foundation, and making sure that when we do launch, we’re putting forward something truly special.

There’s been a lot of work across the board—fine-tuning gameplay, locking in core tech, and giving the game a real visual identity that ties everything together. We’re feeling the momentum and can’t wait to get MOKSHA into the hands of players soon.

---

### **Design Updates – Getting the Core Right**

- The first part of our **First-Time User Experience (FTUE)** is now in the build. It’s a crucial step to make sure new players feel welcome, get into the action quickly, and start to understand the depth of MOKSHA without feeling overwhelmed.

- We’ve taken a fresh look at several key characters, making sure their **abilities match their personalities and narratives** more naturally. It’s been a rewarding process, bringing them more in line with the mythology and making them more exciting to play.

- We’re also in the middle of prototyping a **new progression system** that’s shaping up to be something really unique. Our goal is to make sure players feel rewarded and motivated without unnecessary complexity. We want MOKSHA to stand out in how it keeps players engaged over time.

---

### **Tech Advancements – Bringing Battles to Life**

- We’ve now got a **fully implemented animation system**, so showdowns and character card attacks feel much more dynamic and exciting.

- Every **character card now has its first round of animation designs**, which is a big step toward making the game feel more alive.

- The **mission system** is almost done, which will give players structured goals and a strong sense of direction as they play.

### **Art & Visuals – A Fresh, Cohesive Look**

- We’ve done a **full redesign of all character card text**, making it clearer and more polished.

- The **base art for 27 characters has been upgraded**, improving their look while keeping them consistent with the game’s overall style.

- We’re working with **Gamefuel**, a fantastic new contractor, to **redesign the UI and create a style guide** that will make the game’s visual identity stronger and more cohesive. This is a major effort that’s really going to elevate the look and feel of MOKSHA.

- The **final gameplay screen design** is now in the build, bringing everything together for a more immersive experience.



### **Team Expansion – Strengthening Our Vision**

- We’ve brought on **Karna Krishnan as an Alpha Consultant** to help us navigate the Alpha launch and make sure we’re positioning the game in the best way possible.

- We’ve also welcomed **Venkat R as our new Art Director**. Venkat is doing an incredible job helping us unify the game’s at style while also working on marketing materials that will be key once we hit Alpha.

---

### **What’s Next?**

The next stretch is all about **refining the Alpha build, making sure the core gameplay is as strong as it can be, and preparing for a smooth and successful launch**. There’s still work to do, but we’re in a really good place, and the team is pushing hard to get things across the finish line.

We appreciate your support and trust in us as we build something we truly believe in. We’ll keep you posted with more updates soon!
