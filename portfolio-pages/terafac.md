## Update 2025-10-30T17:26:00.000+05:30 



Manufacturers are projected to lose $500B annually due to global labor shortages. They struggle to adopt automation without re-engineering their entire factory, because today’s systems are rigid, expensive, and service-heavy.

Terafac changes this! We are building an AI-Vision platform that transforms standard robots into adaptive , intelligent systems that autonomously perform complex tasks like welding, buffing, and more in unstructured factory environments.

Our first product, WeldT, makes welding truly autonomous - delivering 3× output with superior quality, no programming needed. WeldT runs on off-the-shelf robots and handles part deviations automatically.

WeldT is live on shop-floors, being piloted for multiple SMEs and OEMs, with 2 POs under finalisation.

We raised $800k pre-seed in Dec’24 to build & validate WeldT. We’re now raising a $4M Seed round to scale across India (TAM $6.5B), validate global market (TAM $45B), and deepen our software IP moat.

Our team blends deep expertise across AI, automation, and manufacturing. Founders:
