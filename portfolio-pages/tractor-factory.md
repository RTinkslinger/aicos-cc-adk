- Currently operating in Madhya Pradesh

- COCO (Company Owned Company Operated)

- Gross margins: 24%

- Net margins: 8-12%

- Store profitability timeline: 2-3 months for operating profitability

- 10% direct from farmers

- Online lead generation: 87% leads with zero marketing spend

- Average order value: 4L
