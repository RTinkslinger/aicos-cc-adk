- Payables (payment to suppliers) was a very big open point to be figured

- Card payment to vendors protects us against fraud case as well + offers us 30 day credit period + we can make payment to vendors upfront

- Accounting: Quickbooks 

- Tax calculation: Taxcloud API 

- Address verification: Radar API
