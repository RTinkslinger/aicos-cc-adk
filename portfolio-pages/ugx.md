### **Key Highlights**

- Founded SchoolMitra to scaled to 1,000+ schools - exited at 15× ARR

- [Ankit Agarwal](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ankit-agarwal-6239078/) (Sales & BD)

- Early operator at GreyOrange (6 yrs), Delhivery (Founding Team), Adani

- Led B2B product expansion across geographies

- [Prakhar Nigam](https://www.linkedin.com/in/prakhar-n/) (Tech)

- Embedded systems specialist (Havells, ISRO, GreyOrange)

- Built mission-critical systems for spacecraft, fighter jets, energy systems

Core Technology & IP

- Developed proprietary Edge IoT devices with on-device processing capabilities to analyze machine data

- Built 35+ communication libraries covering ~70% of industrial machines, aiming for 250-300 libraries

- Universal Communication Framework allows connection to diverse machine types and protocols

- Complete software stack from production planning to final goods production

- The team designs PCBs in-house while contract manufacturing from Korea, Taiwan, and India

Market Approach & Business Model

- Sweet spot: Companies with revenue between 100-500 crore that want to modernize legacy systems

- Land and expand model - starting with 40-50 lakh contracts that grow to 80+ lakhs

- Targets discrete manufacturing (automobile and electronics) with high-frequency, high-volume production

- Sells directly to promoters/owners, bypassing plant heads

- Expected FY26 revenue of 8-10 crore with 30% contribution margin and 2-month working capital cycle

- Currently 22 customers with 8 crore executed revenue expected by March and 12 crore order book

Their Competitive Advantage

- Fills gap between legacy systems and modern automation for mid-sized manufacturers

- Addresses fundamental problem of data infrastructure in factories

- Edge processing reduces cloud data transfer costs

- Partnership strategy with machine manufacturers to embed UGX technology

- Rapidly developing new features (35 in the last 6 months)

- Team has studied manufacturing facilities globally, including in China and Saudi Arabia

**Fundraise**

- Raised $1M from investors including Utsav Somani, DEVC, Ramakant, and Prateek of Physics Wallah

- Currently raising $3-3.5M pre-Series A funding
