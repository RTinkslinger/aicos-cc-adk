**What are they currently building?**

- Developing India’s first Low Earth Orbit (LEO) Position, Navigation & Timing (PNT) satellite constellation to deliver highly precise, secure, and jam-resistant navigation signals.

- Initially started building drone swarms and then decided to pivot.

**Technology Overview**

- RTK engine (2–3 cm accuracy) already built and validated through swarm-drone deployments.

- Current milestones: static payload tests, drone-based dynamic tests, and hosted payload launch prep.

- Remaining gaps: in-orbit validation, timing-sync accuracy, and receiver-side integration with Indian OEMs.

- Development Plan:

- Phase 0 (18 months): Space-borne payload demonstration

- Phase 1 (12 months): City coverage with 4-6 satellites

- Phase 2 (15 months): Asia Pacific coverage with 50 satellites

- Phase 3 (15 months): Global coverage with 125 satellites

### Commercialization Strategy

- Co-designing with industry partners for specific products

- Early adopters will come from two fronts:

- Positioning applications (requiring 4 satellites)

- Timing applications (requiring just 1 satellite)

- Fastest time to market will be via telecom sector

- Revenue generation through services - backhaul systems can be replaced with atomic clocks

- Commercialization is at least 2 years away.

### Team and Funding

- Raised $1.8M from investors including Speciale and DEVC

- Current team growing to 13 members by month end

- Plan to expand to 25 team members in next 6 months
