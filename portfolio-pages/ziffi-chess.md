# **Chess Industry (Avadh Shah - Chess.com)**

Fri, 02 May 25

### [**Chess.com**](http://chess.com/)** Market Size & Users**

- Global: 200M+ registered users

- India metrics:

- 20M registered users lifetime

- ~9M Monthly Active Users (MAU)

- 900K-1M Daily Active Users (DAU)

- India is #2 market globally after US

- [Chess.com](http://chess.com/) has 80% market share in India’s online chess space

### **User Behavior & Monetization**

- Primary user activities:

- Recreational gameplay with friends/family

- Puzzle solving

- Learning chess

- Watching chess content

- Monetization streams:

- Main revenue: Subscriptions

- Secondary: Ad revenue

- Tertiary: Event sponsorships

- India v/s Globe:

- Monetization weaker in India

- % in line with D2C apps (free to paid)

- MAU 2nd but monetisation very far behind

- Western countries (US, Europe) monetize significantly better

### **Growth & Engagement**

- Content engagement: ~1 billion organic views on chess content last year

- Growth drivers:

- Special events (e.g., Praggnanandhaa’s victory: 20-25% bump in subscribers)

- Festival promotions

- External factors (COVID, controversies)

- Company doesn’t set specific user growth targets due to unprecedented growth

### **Real Money Gaming (RMG) Potential**

- Assessment: Not considered scalable model for chess

- Challenges:

- Chess has definite skill levels (ELO rating)

- Recreational players unlikely to bet due to skill certainty

- Limited to players competing at similar levels

- Lack of luck/variance element unlike poker/rummy

- [Chess.com](http://chess.com/) philosophically stays away from RMG
