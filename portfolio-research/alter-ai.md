# Alter AI – Zero-Trust Identity & Access Platform for Enterprise AI Agents

## Executive Summary
Alter AI represents a critical infrastructure layer in the emerging AI landscape, providing zero-trust identity and access control for autonomous AI agents [1]. While the company is based in New York rather than India [1], its compliance-first architecture is highly relevant to global and Indian enterprises deploying AI. **Crucial Disambiguation:** The market currently features two distinct active AI startups operating under the "Alter" brand: **Alter AI** (an enterprise security platform at alterai.dev) [1] and **AlterHQ** (a consumer/prosumer macOS AI assistant at alterhq.com) [2]. Additionally, Google acquired an unrelated 3D avatar startup named Alter for $100 million in 2022 [3]. This report synthesizes data on both active "Alter" AI entities to provide a complete market picture.

## 1. Company Overview – Product & Value Proposition
**Alter AI (alterai.dev)** is a zero-trust authorization platform purpose-built for AI agents [1]. It solves the critical security vulnerability of giving AI agents long-lived credentials or blanket root access [1]. The platform wraps every agent tool call in strong authentication, fine-grained parameter-level authorization, and real-time guardrails [1]. This ensures that unsafe actions—such as a rogue "DROP TABLE" command or unauthorized payments—are blocked before reaching production [1]. The system is designed to keep enterprises fully compliant with SOC 2, HIPAA, and GDPR standards [1].

**AlterHQ (alterhq.com)**, conversely, is a native macOS AI assistant built in Swift [4]. It allows users to trigger complex actions via voice commands, integrates with over 2,000 apps, and provides access to over 50 AI models (including local offline models) [2]. 

## 2. Founding Team & Human Capital
The founding teams of both entities possess deep technical and regulated-industry expertise:
* **Alter AI (YC S25):** Led by Co-Founder & CEO Srikar Dandamuraju, former Platform Lead at Goldman Sachs where he scaled post-trade infrastructure [1]. The CTO is Kevan Dodhia, who previously co-founded Compute.ai (acquired by Terizza in 2025) [1]. 
* **AlterHQ:** Co-founded by Olivier Legris, who also serves as an Anthropic Ambassador [5].

## 4. Market Position & Competitive Landscape
Alter AI occupies the emerging "AI-Agent Identity and Access Management (IAM)" niche. Traditional IAM providers (like Okta) secure human-to-app access, but Alter AI specifically targets machine-to-app interactions, issuing ephemeral, scope-narrowed access tokens that expire in seconds [1].

## 7. Indian Market Context & Relevance
While Alter AI is headquartered in New York [1] and AlterHQ operates primarily in Western markets (London/Paris/Barcelona) [5], their technologies are highly relevant to the Indian tech ecosystem. India's rapidly expanding SaaS and IT services sectors are aggressively adopting AI agents. However, strict data localization laws and RBI compliance mandates for Indian fintechs make blanket AI agent deployments risky. Alter AI's zero-trust, parameter-level authorization [1] represents the exact architectural framework Indian enterprises will need to adopt to deploy AI agents safely within the country's regulatory environment.
