# Atica Global 2024-2026: How an India-founded "last-mile" hotel-revenue platform is scaling in the U.S. market

## Executive Summary
Atica is an India-founded, US-focused hospitality technology startup that provides last-mile sales and revenue management solutions for hotels. By combining a proprietary lead generation and CRM tool with hands-on revenue management, Atica claims to boost annual hotel revenues by up to 40% [1] [2]. Backed by prominent Indian investors like Titan Capital and DLF Family Office, the company has successfully onboarded properties associated with major global brands such as Marriott, Hilton, and Hyatt [3] [2]. While the company shows strong operational momentum with a growing talent hub in Pune, India, specific financial metrics such as its exact funding amount and Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) remain undisclosed [3] [4].

## Company Snapshot
Founded in 2020/2021, Atica (operating as Atica Global Inc and Atica Hospitality Pvt Ltd) is a technology company that helps hotels in the highly competitive US market achieve commercial success [3] [1] [5]. The company disrupts traditional, manual proactive sales processes by securing incremental revenue from local businesses using its proprietary technology [3] [4]. 

## Founding Team & Governance
Atica was founded by industry veterans Akash Goel (CEO) and Bonish Gandhi (CTO) [6] [4]. The founders bring over five years of experience building disruptive technology for the global hospitality industry, having worked across markets including the USA, Mexico, Europe, and Asia [2]. Their deep understanding of the hospitality sector's technology gaps and talent shortages has been a critical driver of Atica's early product-market fit [2].

## Product & Technology Deep-Dive
Atica's core value proposition centers on its proprietary lead generation and CRM tool [7] [4]. The platform integrates:
* **Proactive Sales Automation:** Securing incremental revenue from local businesses [3].
* **Revenue Management:** Utilizing data analytics and dynamic pricing to optimize room rates and inventory [4].
* **Digital Channel Management:** Managing hotel listings and pricing on online travel agencies (OTAs) to maximize visibility [4].
* **Reactive Sales:** Managing Request for Proposals (RFPs) to capture maximum revenue from brand and third-party websites [4] [1].

## Market Position & Competitors
Atica operates in the hotel revenue management software category, targeting independent, boutique, and mid-scale chain properties in the US [3] [4]. 

| Competitor | Core Focus | Differentiation vs. Atica |
|---|---|---|
| Atica | Last-mile sales & revenue management | Combines proprietary lead-gen CRM with hands-on reactive sales [4] [1] |
| ZUZU Hospitality | Revenue management & distribution | Lacks Atica's specific emphasis on proactive local B2B lead generation [4] |
| GuestRevu | Guest feedback & reputation management | Focuses on post-stay analytics rather than pre-booking revenue optimization [4] |

*Takeaway:* Atica differentiates itself by acting as an outsourced, tech-enabled sales and revenue team, rather than just providing a passive software dashboard.

## Business Model & Traction
Atica operates a B2B model serving hotel owners. The company has demonstrated significant traction by working with clients who own properties across major global brands, including Marriott, Hilton, IHG, Hyatt, Choice, Wyndham, and Best Western [3] [2]. By promising up to 40% annual revenue growth, Atica targets high retention and recurring revenue [1] [2].
