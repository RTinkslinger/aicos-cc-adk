# Bidso 2022-2026 Deep-Dive: India's Fast-Growing Tech-Enabled OEM Platform for Consumer Goods

## Executive Summary
Bidso is rapidly emerging as a key player in India's tech-enabled contract manufacturing sector, specifically targeting the global consumer goods and toy markets. Capitalizing on the "Make in India" initiative, the Bengaluru-based startup has shipped over 5 million products and is currently in advanced talks to raise ₹40-50 Cr ($4.3-$5.4M) in a Series A round led by Blume Ventures [1] [2] [3]. For investors and global brands, Bidso represents a strategic bridge to reliable, scalable Indian manufacturing, though due diligence is required regarding undisclosed revenue metrics.

## 1. Company Overview – What Bidso Does

### 1.1 Value Proposition & Core Services
Founded in 2022, Bidso operates as a tech-enabled Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) and contract manufacturing platform for general merchandise [2] [4]. The company provides an end-to-end digital workflow that encompasses conceptualization, industrial design, engineering, manufacturing, and compliance [1]. By digitizing the supply chain, Bidso allows brands to effortlessly source customized, high-quality products with full transparency [4].

### 1.2 Product Portfolio & Scale
Bidso initially focused on kids' mobility toys, including kick scooters, tricycles, ride-ons, and baby walkers [5] [2]. The company has since scaled its operations to encompass 250,000 square feet of factory space, manufacturing over 200 product lines and delivering more than 5 million products [1]. Notably, Bidso is also a licensed manufacturer for leading global character IPs, ensuring products are safe, authentic, and retail-compliant [1].

## 2. Founding Team & Leadership

### 2.1 Founder Bios & Relevant Experience
Bidso's founding team brings deep expertise from India's leading e-commerce and FMCG giants. The company was founded by Vivek Singhal (CEO), Rahul Agarwal, Ashwin Jain, and Aditya Krishnakumar [2] [6]. Their collective backgrounds include leadership and operational roles at Flipkart, Amazon, Udaan, and ITC [5] [4]. This pedigree provides Bidso with immediate credibility and a vast network in B2B supply chain management.

## 7. Risks, Information Gaps & Due-Diligence Checklist

### 7.1 Data Gaps
While Bidso's operational scale (5M+ units) is public, **there is a critical lack of public financial data**. Specific revenue figures, Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR), gross margins, and customer concentration metrics are entirely undisclosed in public sources. 

### 7.2 Mitigation Strategies
Investors and partners must request audited financials, detailed unit economic models (CAC vs. LTV), and a breakdown of revenue by client to assess concentration risk before committing capital.

## 8. Strategic Recommendations for Stakeholders
