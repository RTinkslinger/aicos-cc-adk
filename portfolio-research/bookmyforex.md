# BookMyForex – India’s Fast-Growing Zero-Markup Forex Platform & Its Path to a Travel-Super-App

## Executive Summary
BookMyForex has evolved from a retail foreign exchange marketplace into a comprehensive travel-fintech platform. Backed by MakeMyTrip's fintech arm, TripMoney, the company has seen significant growth, reaching $28 million in revenue and 3 million customers in 2024 [1]. By offering zero-markup forex cards and leveraging a vast distribution network, BookMyForex addresses the growing demands of Indian outbound travelers and students. However, a recent 2026 data breach incident highlights the operational risks inherent in scaling digital financial products [2].

## Company Profile and Value Proposition
BookMyForex operates as India's first e-commerce initiative in the retail foreign exchange and international money transfer space [3] [4]. The platform functions as a marketplace, partnering with banks and exchange companies to fulfill forex requirements across more than 185 cities and 5,000 locations in India [4]. 

Its core product suite includes:
* **Multi-Currency Forex Cards:** Featuring zero-markup interbank rates and zero cross-currency or ATM withdrawal charges [5] [6].
* **Currency Notes & Remittances:** Doorstep delivery of foreign currency and international wire transfers for students and travelers [5] [4].
* **Ancillary Services:** Travel insurance and international SIM cards [5].

## Founding Team
The company is led by a lean core team with deep domain expertise in banking and technology:
* **Sudarshan Motwani (Founder & CEO):** Former Head of Foreign Exchange Services at HDFC Bank and ING Vysya Bank, bringing decades of traditional banking experience to the fintech space [7] [8].
* **Nitin Motwani (Co-Founder & CTO):** Drives the technological architecture and platform scalability [4].

## Business Model and Traction
BookMyForex monetizes through a mix of transaction commissions, forex spreads, and card issuance/reload fees. The company has demonstrated strong financial traction:
* **Revenue Growth:** Revenue doubled from $14.3 million in 2023 to $28 million in 2024 [1].
* **Customer Base:** Reached 3 million customers by 2024 [1].
* **Platform Volume:** Facilitates over $250 million in annual forex volume with more than 11 million annual website visits [3].
* **Profitability:** The company reported achieving operational profitability by 2025 [3].

## Recent News and Milestones (2024-2026)
The 2024-2026 period has been marked by rapid product innovation and a significant security challenge:
* **April 2024:** Launched a new mobile app featuring instant digital reloads for forex cards, allowing travelers to top up balances in real-time while abroad [6].
* **April 2025:** Introduced a new US dollar-denominated Global Forex Card with zero ATM withdrawal fees and zero cross-currency conversion charges, heavily targeting the student demographic [6].
* **February 2026 (Security Incident):** Multiple users of the co-branded Yes Bank-BookMyForex card reported unauthorized transactions originating from Brazil. Yes Bank reported that fraudulent transactions worth approximately ₹7.63 Crore ($840K) were approved across 5,000 customers. BookMyForex denied a direct data breach on its systems, stating that affected cards were proactively blocked and replacement cards were issued [15] [2].
