# Brance & 30 Sundays: AI-First Travel-Tech Play in India's Outbound Tourism Surge

## Executive Summary
Brance Technologies Private Limited operates at the intersection of generative AI and travel-tech, functioning both as a B2B AI sales agent provider (Brance) and a B2C AI-first travel agency for couples (30 Sundays) [1] [2]. Backed by prominent Indian venture capital firms like InfoEdge Ventures and Eximius Ventures, the company leverages AI to automate lead qualification and itinerary generation, achieving up to a 5x improvement in operational efficiency [3]. Positioned to capitalize on India's booming outbound tourism market, the startup is scaling rapidly but faces typical early-stage opacity regarding its exact revenue and profitability metrics.

## 1. Company Overview & Value Proposition
Incorporated in November 2022 and based in New Delhi/Gurugram, Brance Technologies Private Limited develops AI-driven chatbots and sales co-pilots [4] [5] [6]. 

The company operates two primary distinct offerings:
* **Brance (B2B):** An AI sales agent designed for complex sales in sectors like hotels, villas, real estate, and edtech [1]. The product promises ultra-fast response times (under one minute), optimized follow-ups, and automated lead qualification across WhatsApp, Instagram, and Google Maps [1].
* **30 Sundays (B2C):** A consumer-facing brand offering curated, personalized holiday packages specifically for couples [2]. By utilizing generative AI, 30 Sundays can plan detailed, personalized holidays in minutes instead of days, automating mundane tasks like supplier auditing and itinerary updates [3].

## 2. Founding Team & Talent
Brance is led by a highly credentialed founding team with backgrounds spanning elite institutions (IIT, IIM, Stanford) and major tech/consulting firms (Apple, BCG, OYO) [1]. 
* **Kshitij Chaudhary (Co-Founder & CEO, 30 Sundays):** An IIT and IIM Bangalore alumnus with prior experience at BCG and Shuttl [1] [7] [2].
* **Anuj Punjani (Co-Founder):** Brings product and technical expertise, previously associated with Apple [1] [4] [5].
* **Sumit Mishra (Chief Business Officer & Founding Member):** Brings a decade of travel industry experience, including a stint at OYO [1] [4] [2].
* **Jaikishan Dulani:** Listed as a Founding Member [4] [3].
The company currently maintains a lean team of 11-50 employees [1] [4].

## 5. Business Model & Traction
While Brance's B2B SaaS pricing is not publicly detailed, its B2C arm, 30 Sundays, monetizes through travel bookings. The integration of AI into their sales and operations has yielded up to a 5x improvement in efficiency [3]. 

**Key Metrics:**
* **Current Traction:** As of late 2024, 30 Sundays reported a monthly booking value of Rs 50 lakh [9].
* **Growth Target:** The company aims to reach Rs 1.3 to 1.4 crore in monthly booking value by the end of the financial year [9].
* **Lead Funnel:** The company utilizes an Instagram chatbot to qualify leads (destination, duration, headcount) before routing them to human sales agents [9].

## 6. Recent News & Milestones (2024-2026)
