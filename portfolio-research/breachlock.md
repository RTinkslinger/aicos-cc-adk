# BreachLock 2024-2026 Playbook: How a Human-AI PTaaS Pioneer Is Scaling in a $300M Market

## Executive Summary
BreachLock is a cybersecurity firm specializing in Penetration Testing as a Service (PTaaS), blending artificial intelligence with in-house certified hackers to deliver scalable offensive security [1] [2]. Despite raising only a modest $3M Seed round in 2022 [3], the company has achieved significant traction, boasting over 1,000 clients and 100% year-over-year growth [4]. Operating globally with a dedicated Indian entity in Noida [5] [6], BreachLock differentiates itself from crowdsourced competitors by relying entirely on an in-house testing team [7]. While the company is recognized as a market leader by GigaOm and Gartner [7] [8], critical information gaps remain regarding its current valuation, annual recurring revenue (ARR), and exact pricing structures.

## Company Snapshot & Value Proposition
BreachLock delivers a comprehensive PTaaS platform that solves the scalability, accuracy, and agility challenges of traditional penetration testing [4] [1]. The company's core value proposition is its "Human Augmented AI" approach, which uses artificial intelligence to scale the capabilities of in-house pen testers rather than replacing them [2]. This model results in faster time-to-value, lower lead times, and evidence-backed reporting for enterprise clients [2].

## Product Portfolio & Business Model
BreachLock operates a unified, cloud-native platform offering PTaaS, Attack Surface Management (ASM), Red Teaming as a Service (RTaaS), Adversarial Exposure Validation (AEV), and Continuous Threat Exposure Management (CTEM) [7] [8]. 

The company utilizes a tiered subscription model for its penetration testing services:
* **Standard:** Designed for small to medium-sized web apps and basic internal/external networks [13].
* **Extended:** Targeted at medium-sized apps, complex networks, and APIs requiring advanced testing [13].
* **Extensive:** Built for large-scale enterprise apps and multi-layered network environments [13].
