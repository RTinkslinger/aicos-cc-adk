# Caddy Ventures – India’s Founder-First Consumer-Brand Builder (2022-2026)

## Executive Summary
Caddy Ventures is a New Delhi-based venture-building and investment firm that partners with ambitious Indian direct-to-consumer (D2C) founders. Operating on a unique "Venture Equity + Founder Backing" model, Caddy goes beyond traditional venture capital by providing both growth capital and hands-on operational expertise [1]. Founded by serial entrepreneur Vishal Kaushik, the firm acts as a "caddy"—a quiet strategist and guide—helping emerging brands navigate market complexities [2] [1]. With a concentrated portfolio featuring high-growth brands like SpeedX, Kibbo, and Denim Project, Caddy Ventures focuses on building sustainable, high-margin businesses poised for global expansion and strategic exits [2] [3].

## Product and Service Offering
Caddy Ventures differentiates itself by offering a full-stack ecosystem for brand building rather than just financial backing. Their core services span the entire lifecycle of a consumer brand:
* **Growth Capital:** Providing tailored funding from early seed stages to large-scale expansion [1].
* **Brand Building & Elevation:** Shaping brand identity, voice, and creative positioning from the ground up [1].
* **Digital & Performance Marketing:** Utilizing data-driven strategies to accelerate customer acquisition, retention, and maximize return on ad spend [1].
* **Deep Supply Chain & Retail Access:** Assisting founders with reliable sourcing, manufacturing, and omnichannel retail distribution to scale efficiently [1].
* **Strategic Exits:** Guiding founders on long-term scaling pathways, including global expansion, acquisitions, and IPOs [1].

## Founding Team and Advisory Board
The leadership at Caddy Ventures brings deep operational experience in the Indian consumer market. 
* **Vishal Kaushik (Founder & Managing Director):** A serial entrepreneur who previously co-founded Upakarma Ayurveda, scaling it into a highly successful wellness brand [4] [1]. He established Caddy Ventures to fuel the next wave of iconic Indian consumer brands [1].
* **T Koshy (Chief Advisor):** Serving on the Advisory Board, Koshy is the founding MD & CEO of the Open Network for Digital Commerce (ONDC) and brings unparalleled expertise in digital public infrastructure and market democratization [1].

## Funding History and Capital Structure
Unlike traditional venture capital firms that raise massive external funds, Caddy Ventures operates with a highly independent capital structure. The firm is currently unfunded by external institutional investors, relying instead on founder capital and reinvested profits from previous successful ventures [5]. This bootstrapped approach allows Caddy to maintain a "Human-First" philosophy, aligning its long-term interests directly with the founders it supports without the immediate pressure of traditional fund lifecycles [2].

## Market Position and Competitive Landscape
In India's crowded startup ecosystem, Caddy Ventures positions itself as a boutique venture-builder rather than a volume-driven VC. The firm deliberately maintains a concentrated portfolio, backing a select few brands across different industries [2]. This specialist-driven model ensures that each brand receives dedicated operational support, setting Caddy apart from traditional investors who often spread resources thin across dozens of portfolio companies [2].

## Business Model and Revenue Streams
Caddy Ventures operates on a "Venture Equity + Founder Backing Model" [1]. The firm deploys capital in exchange for equity stakes in early-to-growth stage consumer brands. Revenue and returns are generated through the aggressive scaling of these brands into high-margin, profitable entities [2]. By embedding their own operations teams and industry specialists into the portfolio companies, Caddy actively drives revenue growth, optimizes cost efficiencies, and builds brand equity, ultimately targeting lucrative strategic exits or global expansions to realize their investment returns [2] [1].
