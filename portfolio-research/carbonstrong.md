# CarbonStrong – Scaling India’s First‑Mover Low‑Carbon Concrete Solution

## Executive Summary
CarbonStrong is a Bengaluru-based climate-tech startup pioneering carbon reduction technologies for the Indian construction industry [1] [2]. Founded in 2022, the company addresses the massive environmental footprint of cement—which accounts for 8% of global greenhouse gas emissions—by offering engineered Supplementary Cementitious Materials (SCMs) and carbon mineralization technology [3] [1] [2]. With India's cement consumption projected to surge from 445 million metric tonnes in FY24 to 670 million metric tonnes by 2030, CarbonStrong is uniquely positioned to capture a rapidly expanding market [3]. By enabling a 30% to 70% reduction in traditional cement usage without compromising the structural integrity of load-bearing concrete, the company offers a commercially viable, "drop-in" solution for Ready Mix Concrete (RMC) manufacturers [3] [1]. 

## Company Overview & Founding Team
Headquartered in Bengaluru, Karnataka, CarbonStrong was established with a vision to decarbonize the built environment [1] [2]. The leadership team brings a strong blend of mobility, energy, and materials science expertise:
* **Harsh Jain (CEO & Co-Founder)** and **Vikramaditya Singh (COO & Co-Founder)** previously collaborated at Sun Mobility and helped establish EY's electric mobility practice in India [3] [2].
* The core technical team includes **Dr. Revanth K** (Concrete Scientist) and **Arvind M** (Design & Engineering), ensuring deep methodical R&D [3] [2].

## Product & Technology
CarbonStrong’s core innovation lies in its advanced materials and binder technologies designed to produce cleaner concrete [1]. 
* **Engineered SCMs:** The company's low-carbon binder mixes industrial by-products like fly ash and Ground Granulated Blast-furnace Slag (GGBS) with proprietary additives [4] [1]. This allows concrete makers to replace up to 70% of traditional cement [1].
* **Load-Bearing Capability:** Unlike many alternative green cements in India, CarbonStrong's innovation is certified for use in load-bearing structures, significantly expanding its total addressable market [3].
* **Carbon Mineralization:** The startup also integrates CO2 injection and mineralization processes to further reduce the carbon footprint of concrete production without adding prohibitive costs [3] [1].

## Market Position & Business Model
CarbonStrong operates on a B2B model, targeting Ready Mix Concrete (RMCs) companies, as well as block and paver manufacturers [5] [3]. By providing a "drop-in" solution, the company minimizes adoption friction for traditional concrete makers [3]. 

The market opportunity is substantial; reducing cement usage by just 30% could unlock approximately $6 billion (INR 50,000 Cr) in value annually in India [3]. CarbonStrong currently enjoys a significant first-mover advantage in the region, with patents pending for its unique technology, creating a strong defensible moat against future competitors [3].
