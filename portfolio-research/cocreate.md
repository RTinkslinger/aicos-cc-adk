# Scimplify (Cocreate Global Technologies): Research Report

## Executive Summary
Cocreate Global Technologies Private Limited, operating under the brand name **Scimplify**, is a rapidly scaling, science-first specialty chemicals platform based in Bengaluru, India [1] [2]. Founded in 2023, the startup has quickly disrupted the traditional chemical supply chain by combining in-house R&D with a vast, tech-enabled manufacturing network [1] [3]. Having secured $54 million in total funding from top-tier investors like Accel and Bertelsmann India Investments, Scimplify achieved a staggering ₹204 Cr in revenue for the financial year ending March 31, 2025 [4] [5]. The company is currently aggressively expanding its global footprint across the U.S., Japan, and the Middle East, positioning itself as a premier Contract Development and Manufacturing Organization (CDMO) for global enterprise buyers [5].

## Product and Service
Scimplify operates a full-stack platform that manages the entire specialty chemical lifecycle, from molecular research to commercial-scale manufacturing [3]. Through its proprietary digital platform, ATOMS, the company provides businesses with a seamless solution for discovering chemicals and matching with global manufacturers [3]. 

The company's core offerings include:
* **Extensive Catalog:** Delivery of over 3,000 chemical products across diverse industries, including pharmaceuticals, agrochemicals, flavors and fragrances, industrial chemicals, and personal care [1] [3].
* **R&D Capabilities:** An in-house team of over 40 scientists providing custom synthesis, route scouting, and the development of novel chemical products [3].
* **Manufacturing Scale-up:** Transitioning products from pilot-scale to commercial manufacturing, ensuring strict quality and regulatory compliance (such as FDA/EPA readiness) [1] [3].

## Founding Team
Scimplify was founded in 2023 by a trio of entrepreneurs with deep expertise in B2B supply chains and manufacturing [4] [5] [3]:
* **Sachin Santhosh:** Previously worked at B2B startups Bizongo and OfBusiness, gaining extensive experience in streamlining procurement and supply chain processes [5].
* **Salil Srivastava & Dheeraj Dhingra:** Both co-founders previously worked at the manufacturing supply chain unicorn Zetwerk, bringing critical knowledge of scaling asset-light manufacturing networks [5].

## Market Position and Business Model
Scimplify operates on a highly scalable, "fungible manufacturing" business model [3]. Rather than relying solely on owned assets, the company leverages a vetted network of over 250 manufacturing plants across India and other low-cost regions [1] [3]. This allows chemical manufacturers to monetize unutilized capacity while Scimplify takes full accountability for quality, compliance, and delivery to customers across more than 20 countries [1] [3].

Financially, this model has driven explosive growth. The company generated ₹204 Cr in revenue in FY25, representing a 1019% year-over-year growth rate [4]. Headquartered in Bengaluru, Scimplify has established subsidiaries in Dubai and Indonesia, and is actively setting up offices in the U.S. and Japan to capture global demand [5] [3].
