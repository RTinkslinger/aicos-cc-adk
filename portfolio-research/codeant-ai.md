# CodeAnt AI 2024-26 Deep-Dive: AI-Powered Code-Health Platform, Funding Trail & Play in India's Dev-Tools Market

## Executive Summary
CodeAnt AI is a rapidly growing developer tools startup operating out of San Francisco and Bengaluru, India [1] [2]. Founded in 2023, the company addresses a critical bottleneck in modern software engineering: the validation and review of AI-generated code [3] [4]. By automating code reviews, security checks, and quality assurance, CodeAnt AI reduces review times by up to 80% [5]. Backed by a $2 million Seed round in May 2025, the company has quickly scaled to serve over 50 enterprise organizations and approximately 10,000 developers [6] [7]. 

## What the Company Does - Product & Value Proposition
CodeAnt AI provides an "AI Code Health" platform that unifies AI Code Review, Code Security, Code Quality, and Engineering Metrics [5]. As AI coding assistants like GitHub Copilot dramatically increase code output, peer review has become a severe bottleneck [3]. CodeAnt AI solves this by integrating directly into developer workflows (GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket, Azure DevOps) and providing instant feedback and one-click fixes across more than 30 programming languages [5] [4].

A key differentiator for CodeAnt AI is its enterprise-grade security posture. The platform is SOC 2 Type II and HIPAA compliant, offers on-premise deployment options, and strictly guarantees that it never trains its models on customer code [8] [5] [4]. This makes it highly attractive to regulated industries in both the US and India.

## Founding Team & Key Executives
The company was founded by two engineers with deep ties to the Indian tech ecosystem:
* **Amartya Jha (Co-Founder & CEO):** Previously led DevOps and Infrastructure at Indian unicorns Zeta and ShareChat, scaling systems to handle hundreds of millions of requests [5] [4].
* **Chinmay Bharti (Co-Founder & CTO):** An IIT Bombay alumnus (BTech/MTech in Electrical Engineering) who previously served as founding engineer at Zevi AI and a quant developer at Blu Analytics [5] [4].
