# Confido Health — AI Voice-Agent Playbook for Scaling Healthcare Operations (2024-2026)

## Executive Summary
Confido Health is a rapidly scaling enterprise health-tech startup that deploys AI-powered voice agents to automate front-desk and back-office healthcare operations. Founded by Indian-origin entrepreneurs and backed by prominent India-linked venture funds (Blume Ventures, Together Fund), the company has grown its patient reach from 150,000 in December 2024 to over 1 million by late 2025 [1]. By resolving 70-80% of patient calls autonomously and integrating directly with Electronic Health Records (EHR), Confido addresses critical healthcare labor shortages while increasing practice collections by 15-20% [2]. 

## 1. Company Snapshot: AI-Powered Digital Workers
Confido Health provides AI-driven automation tools designed to replace traditional healthcare phone trees [3] [1]. The platform's voice agents answer calls on the first ring, authenticate patients, and autonomously resolve requests such as appointment scheduling, prescription refills, payments, and referrals [1]. 

Crucially, the system writes all interactions directly back to the provider's EHR or Practice Management System (PMS) in real time [1]. The AI operates 24/7, supports over 20 languages and accents, and seamlessly routes complex edge cases to human staff [1] [4]. 

## 2. Founding Team & Leadership
The company was founded in 2022 and is headquartered in New York, with a team size of 11-50 employees [2]. The leadership team combines deep technical expertise with firsthand healthcare experience:
* **Chetan Reddy (Co-Founder & CEO):** An Indian entrepreneur who was inspired to start Confido after experiencing the severe administrative burden of the US healthcare system during his wife's spine surgery [5].
* **Vichar Shroff (Co-Founder & Chief Product Officer):** Leads the technical architecture, focusing on turning fragmented legacy data into actionable, real-time workflows [5].
* **Raihan Faroqui, MD (VP of Partnerships):** An internal medicine physician who drives strategic partnerships and clinical validation [2].
