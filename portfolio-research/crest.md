# Crest AI: Scaling AI-Driven Inventory Planning for India's Fast-Growing D2C Market

## Executive Summary

Crest (legally Conifer Innovations Private Limited) is a Bengaluru-based enterprise tech startup that provides AI-powered automated inventory planning software tailored for direct-to-consumer (D2C) brands and SMBs [1] [2]. Founded in 2022, the company addresses a critical pain point in the rapidly expanding Indian D2C market: supply chain inefficiencies and frequent stock-outs [1]. By leveraging machine learning to accurately predict demand and automate purchase and replenishment planning, Crest has helped early customers reduce stock-out frequencies by half and increase top-line revenue by 15% within three months of engagement [1]. With $1.38 million in total seed funding and an annual revenue of approximately ₹2.92Cr as of March 2025, Crest is positioning itself as a vital infrastructure layer for India's retail and e-commerce ecosystem [2].

## Product & Technology

Crest operates as a comprehensive supply chain control tower, bringing enterprise-grade planning methods to fast-growing startups [3] [4]. The platform is designed to eliminate manual spreadsheet-based forecasting, which often fails to account for real-time market signals or logistics disruptions [5]. 

### Core Feature Matrix

| Feature | Description | Value Proposition |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Accurate Demand Forecasting** | Dynamically predicts future demand across all channels at high granularity [5]. | Accounts for trends, promotions, and disruptions that traditional forecasts miss [5]. |
| **HELIX: S&OP Workflow** | Custom Sales and Operations Planning (S&OP) workflow automation [5]. | Provides a bird's-eye view of the supply chain to enhance productivity [5]. |
| **Automated Purchase Planning** | Constant inventory tracking and automated PO generation [5]. | Ensures brands know exactly how much to order and when, improving with every cycle [5]. |

## Founding Team

Crest was founded in 2022 by a team of supply-chain experts who spent years assisting businesses in optimizing their operations [1]. The founding team includes Rahul Vishwakarma (CEO), Akhil Kumar (Chief Growth Officer), Yogesh Byahatti (CTO), and Abhishek Surendran [2] [6]. Prior to Crest, Vishwakarma was the Co-Founder of Raven Protocol [7]. As of August 2025, the company has grown its team to 29 employees [2].

## Business Model

Crest operates on a B2B Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) business model [1]. It targets D2C brands and SMBs across various industries, including cosmetics, organic foods, QSRs, mobile accessories, and agri-tech [3]. By delivering immediate ROI through reduced stock-outs and optimized warehouse space, Crest justifies its subscription fees. As of March 31, 2025, the company reported an annual revenue of ₹2.92Cr [2].
