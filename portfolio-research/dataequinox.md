## Company Overview: End-to-End AI Agent Ecosystem
DataEquinox positions itself as a next-generation AI products and consulting company [1]. Operating from the Infopark Special Economic Zone in Thrissur, Kerala, the company targets industries such as FinTech, Retail, and Communications [1] [2] [4].
