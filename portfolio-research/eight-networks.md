# Eight Networks Deep Dive: India's Fast-Growing Interactive Audio OTT Platform

## Executive Summary

Eight Network is an emerging Indian interactive audio OTT and social radio startup that is reshaping the digital audio landscape for young audiences. Founded in 2021 and operating out of Bengaluru and Gurugram, the company provides a unique platform for audio stories, podcasts, live shows, and micro-dramas. Backed by $3.14M in seed funding from prominent venture capital firms including Kae Capital, Venture Highway, and Z47, Eight Network has established a creator-focused ecosystem. With its innovative approach to immersive audio content, the platform is well-positioned to capture the growing demand for localized, interactive media in India.

## Company Overview & Founding Team

Registered legally as Eight Network Creations Private Limited, the startup was founded in 2021 [1] [2]. The company maintains operations in key Indian tech hubs, specifically Bengaluru and Gurugram [2] [3]. 

The founding team consists of three entrepreneurs: Mohit Paliwal, Mohit Goswami, and Yugal Tamang [2] [3]. Their collective vision has driven the company to become a modern media entity focused on changing the landscape of content in India [4]. 

## Product and Service Offerings

Eight Network operates primarily as an "Interactive Social Radio" and Audio OTT platform [5] [4]. The company's product suite is designed to offer immersive content tailored specifically for young audiences [6]. 

Key offerings include:
* **Audio OTT Platform:** Users can listen to India's best audio stories, podcasts, and live shows for free on both web and mobile applications [7].
* **Micro Drama App:** A specialized application focusing on short-form, serialized audio drama content [4].
* **Creator Broadcasting:** An audio broadcasting layer that actively helps creators grow their audience and manage their content [8].

## Market Position and Recent News

Eight Network distinguishes itself as the first and only Audio OTT platform dedicated to serving young Indian users with highly immersive, tone-appropriate content [6]. As of February 2026, the platform reported having 4,075 active listeners [2]. 

Recent company activities indicate a strong focus on expanding in-house content generation. The company has been actively recruiting young professionals, recently opening roles for Audio Production Interns to support its Interactive Social Radio initiatives [9]. 

## Business Model

Eight Network's business model centers on building a robust, creator-led audio broadcasting ecosystem [8]. By providing the infrastructure for creators to host shows and grow their listener base, the platform drives user acquisition through content diversity. Currently, the platform offers its core listening experience for free across its web and app interfaces [7], focusing on scaling its user base and creator network as its primary growth engine in the competitive Indian digital media market.
