# Ergeon: How a Remote-First Marketplace Is Redefining U.S. Outdoor Home-Improvement (2024-2026)

## Executive Summary
Ergeon is transforming the U.S. residential construction sector by applying consumer-software convenience to outdoor home improvement. By combining a fully remote global workforce with proprietary quoting technology, the company has rapidly scaled to cover nearly half of all U.S. households [1]. While well-capitalized with over $51.9 million in funding [2], Ergeon operates exclusively in the U.S. market, offering no current exposure or operations within the Indian startup ecosystem. 

## End-to-End Project Management Replaces Traditional Contracting
Ergeon operates a technology-enabled construction marketplace specializing in outdoor living projects, primarily fences, artificial grass, and decks [3] [4]. Unlike traditional contractors, Ergeon utilizes satellite-based measurements and video consultations to deliver transparent quotes in as little as 10 minutes [1]. The company owns the entire customer relationship—from initial remote assessment to billing and warranty—while utilizing local contracting partners to execute the physical installation [5]. This model removes the front-office and back-office burden from local installers, allowing them to scale their small businesses efficiently [5].

## Leadership Blending Construction and Tech Scale
The founding team brings a unique intersection of hands-on construction experience and elite tech-scaling pedigree:
* **Jenny He (Co-founder & CEO):** A former McKinsey consultant with a computer science background from Princeton, He became a licensed contractor across multiple states after experiencing the broken home-renovation process firsthand [6] [7] [8].
* **Odysseas Tsatalos (Co-founder & CTO):** A seasoned serial entrepreneur who previously co-founded Upwork and Intacct (acquired by Sage for $850 million) [5]. 
* **Key Executives:** The leadership bench includes Alina Jesien (Sr. Director of HR), Jorgos Tsatalos (Product Manager), and Merv Blakesley (Business Development) [6] [1].

## Market Position: Capturing Margin in a $14 Billion Industry
The U.S. fencing market was valued at $9.33 billion in 2024 and is projected to reach $14.86 billion by 2033, growing at a 5.3% CAGR [10]. Ergeon differentiates itself fundamentally from traditional lead-generation competitors.

| Competitor Type | Key Players | Revenue Model | Customer Experience |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Managed Marketplace** | Ergeon | Captures full project margin | End-to-end management, guaranteed work, remote quoting [5] |

*Takeaway: By acting as the general contractor of record rather than a mere lead broker, Ergeon captures significantly higher unit economics per project while ensuring quality control.*

## Recent Milestones and Operational Traction (2024-2026)
Ergeon has demonstrated aggressive scaling and industry recognition over the past two years:
* **Nationwide Expansion (Dec 2024):** The company announced its expansion to cover 40% to 48% of U.S. households (approximately 43 million single-family homes) [6] [4] [1].
* **Project Volume:** Ergeon has completed over 35,000 outdoor projects, maintaining a 4.7-star rating across more than 13,700 public reviews [6] [1].
* **Industry Awards:** The company secured the Angi Super Service Award for 2024 and the Best of Houzz 2025 award, validating its customer-first approach [6] [4].
* **Global Workforce:** Despite serving only U.S. homeowners, Ergeon operates a fully remote team of over 899 members spanning 45+ countries, utilizing Upwork as its operational backbone [1].
