# Finsire: Building India's Digital Rail for Secured Credit

## Executive Summary
Finsire is an emerging Indian fintech startup that provides the digital infrastructure required to collateralize assets, specifically focusing on Loans Against Mutual Funds (LAMF) and shares. Founded in 2021, the company operates as a B2B technology enabler, allowing banks, NBFCs, and wealth managers to offer instant, secured credit to their customers without liquidating investments. Backed by a $1.3 million seed round from prominent venture capital firms and fintech founders, Finsire has successfully integrated with major financial institutions like Bajaj Finance and City Union Bank. With recent recognition as a Top 20 finalist in the UAE-India Start-up Series in early 2026, Finsire is well-positioned to capitalize on the massive, untapped market of household asset collateralization.

## Product and Service
Finsire offers a plug-and-play asset collateralization API and lending infrastructure platform [1] [2]. Historically, pledging mutual funds or shares in India involved tedious paperwork and days of processing [1]. Finsire digitizes this entire journey, reducing the loan approval and lien-marking process to mere minutes [3] [1]. 

The platform integrates directly with major registrars like CAMS and KFintech, covering over 6,000 mutual fund schemes [3]. Customers can check their eligibility instantly and secure loan amounts ranging from 50% to 80% of their mutual fund value, depending on whether the assets are equity or debt funds [3]. The funds are disbursed directly to the borrower's bank account, while the underlying investments remain intact and continue to earn returns [3].

## Founding Team
Finsire was founded in 2021 by a team of product thinkers and finance professionals: Shreyans Nahar (CEO), Subramaniam Chintamani, and Mridul Manhas [4] [2]. The founders identified a significant gap in the Indian credit market, noting that while Indian households hold over $14 trillion in assets, these assets are notoriously difficult to pledge for liquidity [2].

## Business Model
Finsire operates on a B2B SaaS and API integration model. It provides a full-stack platform covering loan origination, collateral management, and loan servicing [1]. By integrating with financial institutions, fintechs, and asset-holding brokers, Finsire enables these entities to maximize their lending capabilities [2]. 

The core value proposition for lenders is the elimination of Non-Performing Assets (NPAs), as the loans are fully backed by highly liquid digital assets [2]. This risk-free return profile for lenders allows them to pass down lower interest rates to end-users, creating a win-win ecosystem driven by Finsire's technological rails [2].
