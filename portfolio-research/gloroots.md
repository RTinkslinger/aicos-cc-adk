# Gloroots – Affordable Global EOR with an India-first Edge (2022-2026)

## Executive Summary

Gloroots (legally Global Roots Inc.) delivers a highly competitive, AI-enhanced global Employer of Record (EOR) and payroll platform with a distinct advantage in the India and APAC markets. By offering transparent, flat-fee pricing starting at $299 per employee per month, Gloroots significantly undercuts legacy enterprise competitors whose costs often balloon with hidden markups. While its small team size and undisclosed revenue metrics warrant standard due diligence for enterprise clients and investors, its robust AI-driven sourcing workflow and zero-entity setup model make it an exceptionally strategic partner for mid-market companies scaling distributed teams across its 140+ supported countries.

## Company Overview & Founding Team

Founded in 2021/2022 and headquartered in San Francisco, California, Gloroots operates as a private, seed-stage company with an estimated 11 to 50 employees [1] [2] [3]. The company's technological edge is driven by a founding team with deep roots in artificial intelligence and machine learning. Co-founder and CTO Abhirup Nath brings over 15 years of AI/ML experience, including previous roles at Microsoft Research [4]. He is joined by co-founders Mayank Bhutoria, who specializes in AI-driven recruitment solutions [5] [6], and Yamini Jain, who brings product and operational expertise [7]. Together, they have built a platform that combines human recruiter judgment with AI-first workflows to accelerate global hiring [1].

## Product & Service Portfolio

Gloroots provides an all-in-one solution to discover, onboard, and manage employees globally without the need for local entity setup [2]. The core offerings include:
* **Global Employer of Record (EOR):** Enables compliant hiring across borders, managing local labor laws, taxes, and benefits [2].
* **Global Payroll Management:** Automates payroll processing for both employees and contractors across multiple countries [2].
* **Contractor Management:** Streamlines vendor setups and global contractor payments [8].
* **AI-Powered Sourcing:** Reimagines hiring by blending deep human judgment with AI-first workflows to find and screen talent [1].

In India specifically, Gloroots manages complex offboarding, statutory contributions (such as 12% PF and 3.25% ESI), and compliance end-to-end, allowing foreign companies to tap into India's massive talent pool with zero entity setup costs [9].

## Business Model & Pricing

Gloroots operates on a transparent, flat-fee SaaS and service model that sharply contrasts with the opaque, quote-based pricing of legacy competitors. 

| Feature/Metric | Gloroots | Globalization Partners (G-P) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **EOR Pricing** | From $299/employee/month [10] | ~$600–$750+ (effective $950–$1,500+ with markups) [10] |
| **Contractor Pricing** | From $29/contractor/month [10] | From $39/contractor/month [10] |
| **Pricing Transparency** | Itemized invoices, no hidden fees [10] | Quote-based, bundled invoices with FX/benefit markups [10] |
| **Global Coverage** | 140+ countries (Strong India/APAC focus) [10] | 180+ countries [10] |

*Takeaway:* Gloroots' pricing model offers predictable cost scaling, making it highly attractive for mid-market companies and Global Capability Centers (GCCs) prioritizing cost control over maximum global reach [10].

While the exact financial figures of the seed round remain undisclosed, the backing of prominent India-US corridor funds like z21 Ventures and Blume Ventures highlights strong institutional confidence in Gloroots' cross-border HR tech thesis [3] [11].

## Market Position & Recent News

Gloroots positions itself as a modern, agile alternative to traditional EORs, boasting a 4.9/5 rating on G2 based on 21 reviews [8]. Customers frequently praise the platform's transparent billing and exceptional, human-centric customer service, noting that the team helped them rapidly expand into Southeast Asian markets like Malaysia, Vietnam, Thailand, and the Philippines in under two weeks [8]. 

The company actively publishes thought leadership and expansion guides to capture niche markets.
