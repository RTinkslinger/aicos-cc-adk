# Grexa AI: Scaling India's SMB Marketing Engine with Agentic AI

## Executive Summary

Grexa AI is a Navi Mumbai-based B2B marketing technology startup founded in 2024 that provides an all-in-one, autonomous AI marketing platform tailored for Indian micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs) [1] [2] [3]. Built by the founding team behind the successful edtech platform Testbook, Grexa AI aims to democratize digital growth for local businesses by replacing expensive marketing agencies with an automated, full-funnel AI engine [4] [5]. Having recently secured Rs 15.5 crore ($1.85 million) in seed funding in July 2025, the company has rapidly scaled its user base, currently serving over 21,600 businesses across various local service sectors [6]. 

## Product and Service Offering

Grexa AI delivers an agentic AI marketing stack designed specifically for business owners who lack technical marketing skills [7] [5]. The platform consolidates multiple fragmented marketing functions into a single, automated application. 

Core capabilities of the Grexa AI platform include:
* **Full-Funnel Automation:** The engine autonomously manages Local SEO, Social Media posting, Online Ads, and Website management [8] [6].
* **Google Business Profile (GBP) Booster:** A specialized AI agent operating via WhatsApp that helps local businesses generate more leads and customer calls directly from Google [5].
* **Autonomous Execution:** The platform is designed to run "on auto-pilot," bringing in leads, closing deals, and driving customer retention without requiring manual intervention from the business owner [5].

## Funding History

In July 2025, Grexa AI successfully raised Rs 15.5 crore (approximately $1.85 million) in a seed funding round [6]. The round was led by Utsav Somani (founder of Offline Club) [3]. 

The cap table includes a strong mix of institutional seed funds and prominent angel investors:
* **Institutional Investors:** DeVC (Z47's seed fund), Bharat Founders Fund, and Vernalis Capital [6] [10] [4].
* **Angel Investors:** Revant Bhate, Vaibhav Domkundwar, Ramakant Sharma, and Sumit Gupta [6] [4].

The company stated that the proceeds from this seed round will be utilized to drive product innovation and enhance market reach across India [6].

## Market Position and Business Model

Grexa AI operates in a massive but historically underserved market: India's 6 crore+ small businesses, which include over 2.3 crore micro-businesses contributing to more than 32% of the country's GDP [4] [5]. 

**Business Model:**
The company operates on a B2B SaaS model. It positions itself as a highly cost-effective alternative to traditional marketing agencies, which typically charge Rs 2-8 lakh per month—a price point entirely out of reach for most local MSMEs [5]. By offering an affordable, software-driven alternative, Grexa eliminates the need for unreliable freelancers or complex enterprise tools [6] [5].

**Market Traction:**
The platform has achieved significant early penetration. It is already being utilized by over 21,600 businesses, including salons, clinics, gyms, and cafés [6]. The company currently maintains a headcount of approximately 45 to 200 employees at its headquarters in Vashi, Navi Mumbai [2] [11] [5].
