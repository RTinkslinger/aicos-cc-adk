# Grvt (India) – Privacy-First Decentralised Exchange: Funding Surge, Regulatory Paradox & Growth Playbook

## Executive Summary
Grvt is a decentralised exchange (DEX) focused on privacy in on-chain finance, powered by zero-knowledge (ZK) technology [1] [2]. Recently securing a $19 million Series A round in September 2025, the platform aims to challenge established players like Hyperliquid by offering scalable, privacy-centric trading [3] [2]. However, a significant regulatory paradox exists: its Indian legal entity, GRVT Private Limited, is registered in Chandigarh under the manufacture of chemicals and pharmaceuticals [4] [5] [6]. This mismatch between a high-growth Web3 fintech product and a traditional chemical manufacturing registration presents both unique operational risks and structural challenges that must be resolved to sustain its global momentum.

## Company Overview & Regulatory Paradox
Incorporated on January 5, 2022, GRVT Private Limited operates out of Chandigarh, India [4] [5]. Officially, the company is classified under NIC Code 24306, which pertains to the manufacture of chemicals and chemical products [4]. Online directories even list the company as a wholesaler of pharmaceutical products like "NICU D3 DROP" and "GRVCAL TABLET" [6]. Conversely, the global brand "Grvt" (grvt.io) operates as a cutting-edge on-chain investment and trading app [7]. This stark divergence suggests either a dual-business model or a severe regulatory mismatch that requires immediate corporate restructuring to avoid compliance friction in India's complex crypto regulatory environment.

## Product & Technology
Grvt positions itself as a pioneer in privacy-first on-chain finance [1]. The platform utilizes zero-knowledge (ZK) technology to ensure transaction confidentiality while maintaining the security and decentralization of the blockchain [1]. 

| Feature | Grvt Capability | Strategic Advantage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Core Technology** | Zero-knowledge (ZK) proofs [1] | Enables privacy-first on-chain finance [1]. |
| **Primary Function** | Decentralised Exchange (DEX) [1] | Allows users to trade, invest, and earn efficiently [7]. |
| **Market Positioning** | Hyperliquid Challenger [3] | Targets users needing high-speed execution with added privacy [3]. |

This architecture allows users to make their money work efficiently through trading and investing without exposing their financial data to the public ledger [7].

## Founding Team & Governance
The corporate filings for GRVT Private Limited list three primary directors: Nikhil Sharma, Sahil Sharma, and Riya Chabra, all appointed on the company's incorporation date of January 5, 2022 [5]. A former managing director, Narender Pal Garg, ceased his role in September 2023 [5]. On LinkedIn, the company reports a team size of 11-50 employees and is actively hiring for strategic roles, such as a Head of Marketing in APAC, to drive growth [8] [9].

## Business Model & Strategic Recommendations
Grvt's business model revolves around making "trading, investing, and earning more efficient" [7], likely generating revenue through trading fees and yield optimization on its DEX. 

**Strategic Recommendations:**
1. **Corporate Restructuring:** Immediately separate the Web3 DEX operations from the Chandigarh-based chemical/pharmaceutical entity (NIC 24306) to mitigate regulatory risks [4] [6].
2. **Capital Expansion:** Increase the authorized share capital of the Indian entity (currently ₹15 Lakhs) to properly reflect and absorb international venture funding [4].
3. **Talent Acquisition:** Continue leveraging the $19M funding to hire specialized Web3 marketing and technical leadership across the APAC region [9] [2].
