# Hapramp (GoSocial) – India’s Emerging Creator-Economy Platform: Market Position, Traction & Investment Outlook

## Executive Summary
Hapramp Studio is an Indian tech startup focused on the creator economy and Web 3.0 social networking. Founded in 2018 by five engineering graduates, the company gained early prominence after securing a $1 million seed investment from Mahindra Group Chairman Anand Mahindra in 2020 [1] [2]. While its flagship product, GoSocial, achieved early traction with 50,000 users within three months of launch [2], recent indicators from 2025-2026 suggest potential operational stagnation, highlighted by the company's primary domain (hapramp.com) being listed for sale [3]. 

## 1. Company Overview & Value Proposition
Based in Gurugram, Haryana, Hapramp Studio Pvt. Ltd. was established to address data security, user privacy, and fair content monetization in the social media landscape [1] [4]. The company's mission is to empower creators by allowing them to take control of their data and earn a fair fraction of its monetary value [1] [5]. 

### Core Product Ecosystem
Hapramp operates a multi-tiered product ecosystem targeting different facets of the creator economy and blockchain integration:

| Product | Description | Core Value Proposition |
|---------|-------------|------------------------|
| **GoSocial** | Flagship community platform and app | Enables creators to build engaged communities, host workshops, create challenges, and monetize expertise based on the #100TrueFans concept [4]. |
| **1Ramp.io** | Social media platform | Powered by the Steem Blockchain, functioning as a rewarding social media ecosystem [1] [2]. |
| **Asteria Protocol** | Data privacy standard | A protocol designed to help platforms privately and securely treat public data [1] [2]. |

The combination of a mainstream creator monetization app (GoSocial) with underlying Web 3.0 and blockchain technologies (1Ramp, Asteria) differentiates Hapramp from traditional social networks [2].

## 4. Business Model, Traction & Unit Economics
Hapramp's primary focus has been on user acquisition and community building, with monetization planned as a secondary phase.

### User Adoption Metrics
* **Initial Surge:** GoSocial garnered 50,000 users in less than three months after its 2020 launch [2].
* **App Store Performance:** The GoSocial app reached 500,000 downloads on the Google Play Store with a 4.1-star rating across 1.1k reviews [7].
* **Recent Web Traffic:** Crunchbase data from late 2025 indicates a severe drop in web traction, showing only 78 monthly visits [4]. Apptopia data also showed a low monthly download rate of 503 [4].

The business model relies on creators hosting offerings and challenges, though specific revenue metrics, commission rates, or subscription fees remain undisclosed [2] [4]. CEO Shubhendra Vikram explicitly stated in 2020 that the initial focus was on building the user base, with monetization to "happen eventually" [2].

## 5. Recent News (2024-2026) & Critical Information Gaps
Public information regarding Hapramp's operations between 2024 and 2026 is extremely limited, raising significant questions about the company's current status.

### Critical Information Gaps & Red Flags
1. **Domain Status:** As of late 2025 and early 2026, Hapramp's primary corporate domain (`hapramp.com`) is parked and listed for sale on HugeDomains [3]. This strongly suggests a lapse in domain renewal or a potential cessation of corporate operations.
2. **Funding Stagnation:** There are no public records of Hapramp raising Series A or bridge funding following its 2020 Seed round [4].
3. **Revenue Data:** There is zero public visibility into the company's Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) or whether the GoSocial monetization engine was ever successfully activated.

## 6. Strategic Recommendations
Based on the available data up to March 2026, stakeholders and potential partners should approach Hapramp with caution:
* **Verify Operational Status:** Before any engagement, explicitly verify if the corporate entity is still active, given the sale listing of their primary web domain [3].
* **Audit User Metrics:** The discrepancy between historical 500k app downloads [7] and recent metrics of 78 monthly web visits [4] requires a thorough audit of current Monthly Active Users (MAU).
* **Assess IP Value:** If the consumer-facing GoSocial app has stalled, the underlying Web 3.0 infrastructure (Asteria Protocol and 1Ramp.io) may hold residual intellectual property value for enterprise data privacy applications [2].
