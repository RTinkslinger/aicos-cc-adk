# Hexo AI Unpacked: How a Bengaluru-Based Generative-AI Startup Is Shaping the Indian Image-Creation Market

## Executive Summary

Hexo AI is a Bengaluru-based generative AI startup that is carving out a niche in India's rapidly expanding artificial intelligence ecosystem. Founded in 2022, the company provides an open-source image generation API and an autonomous agent platform designed to streamline the machine learning (ML) lifecycle. Having secured $520K in total funding from prominent early-stage investors like Antler India and VitalStage Ventures, Hexo is well-positioned to capitalize on the growing demand for predictable, enterprise-grade generative AI tools. For strategic partners and investors, Hexo represents a compelling blend of foundational ML infrastructure and accessible generative AI applications within a broader Indian market that has already attracted over $2.6 billion in AI investments.

## 1 Company Overview

### 1.1 Founders and Origins
Hexo AI was founded in 2022 by Kunal Bhatia and Vignesh Baskaran [1] [2]. The company is headquartered in Bengaluru, India, a major hub for technological innovation and startup incubation [1]. The founders have positioned the company to tackle the unpredictability often associated with generative AI outputs, focusing on building robust, developer-friendly tools.

## 2 Product & Technology

### 2.1 Open-Source Image Generation API
At its core, Hexo is building an open-source image generation API that provides users with a wide range of controls [3]. This focus on control is designed to make image generation significantly more predictable and accurate, addressing a major pain point for enterprise adoption of generative AI [3]. The platform also functions as an AI-based search engine for photos and a custom image generation builder [1] [4].

### 2.2 Agentic ML Lifecycle Platform
Beyond image generation, Hexo is expanding into broader ML infrastructure. The company offers an autonomous agent platform that handles the entire machine learning lifecycle [5]. This includes critical backend processes such as data preparation, enabling teams to build and deploy machine learning systems more efficiently [5].

## 3 Funding & Capital Structure

### 3.1 Investment Timeline and Backers
Hexo has raised a total of $520K to date [4]. The company's funding journey began with a $270K pre-seed round in December 2022, which was led by Antler India via the venture capital firm's Antler India Residency initiative [6] [7].

## 4 Market Landscape & Competitive Position

### 4.1 The Indian AI Ecosystem
Hexo operates in a highly dynamic and well-funded market. India is currently home to more than 170 AI startups [8]. Collectively, these companies have raised over $2.6 billion to date, building everything from foundational models to enterprise tools and devices [8]. Hexo's dual approach—offering both a specific generative AI application (image API) and broader ML infrastructure (autonomous agents)—allows it to compete across multiple segments of this booming market.

## 5 Strategic Recommendations

To maintain momentum in a crowded market, Hexo should focus on the following strategic initiatives:
* **Enterprise Monetization:** Transition the open-source API traction into a tiered enterprise SaaS model, offering premium features like guaranteed SLAs and advanced data privacy controls.
* **Capital Expansion:** With $520K raised, the company should prepare for a larger Seed or Series A round to extend its runway and scale its autonomous ML platform against well-funded competitors.
* **Ecosystem Partnerships:** Leverage its existing investor network (such as ML Elevate and VitalStage Ventures) to secure strategic partnerships with cloud providers, reducing compute costs and accelerating go-to-market efforts.
