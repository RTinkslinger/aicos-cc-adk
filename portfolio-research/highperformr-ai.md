# Highperformr AI – How a Gen-AI Social-CRM Platform Is Trying to Reinvent B2B GTM in India and Beyond

## Executive Summary
Highperformr AI is a generative AI-powered Go-To-Market (GTM) and social CRM platform designed to help B2B businesses identify high-intent leads and amplify their social presence [1] [2]. Founded by former Freshworks executives, the company secured a $3.5 million Seed round in May 2024 to build out its R&D operations in Chennai, India [3] [4]. By focusing on the "95% of users who are not actively searching for products online," Highperformr differentiates itself from traditional intent-data providers by turning social media engagement into actionable sales pipelines [2]. 

## I. Company Snapshot
Highperformr Inc. is a B2B SaaS startup incorporated in Delaware, USA, with its primary research and development (R&D) and operational headquarters located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India [1] [2]. The company's core product, "Highperformr for Teams," is an AI GTM platform that combines a database of over 450 million contacts and 50 million companies with real-time social intent signals, CRM enrichment, and automated social publishing workflows [1]. 

## II. Founding Team & Leadership DNA
The leadership team brings significant experience in scaling global SaaS products from India:
* **Srivatsan Venkatesan (Co-Founder & CEO):** Former Founder & CEO of CloudNucleus Technologies, with extensive experience building CRM products at Freshworks [1] [2].
* **Ramesh Ravishankar (Co-Founder & Chief Growth Officer):** Former product growth leader at Freshworks and Google, driving the company's thesis to target the 95% of non-active buyers [2].
* **Bhagirath Goud (Founding Member & Tech Architect):** Leads the technical architecture and AI/ML engineering [1].
* **Adeel Zaman:** Joined in 2025 as a technologist and executive leader with expertise in cloud computing and AI [5].

## IV. Product Portfolio & Go-to-Market Engine
Highperformr's platform is designed to replace fragmented marketing and sales tools with a unified Social AI Copilot [6]. Key modules include:
* **Intent Signals & CRM Enrichment:** Tracks job changes, new hires, and competitor social audiences, enriching CRM data with real-time social signals [6].
* **Engagement & Orchestration:** Features social publishing, employee advocacy, and automated inbound/outbound AI agents [6].

## V. Market Landscape & Competitive Map
The B2B GTM software market is highly competitive. Highperformr positions itself at the intersection of traditional data enrichment and social media management [6].

| Competitor Category | Key Players | Highperformr's Differentiation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Intent Data & Enrichment** | ZoomInfo, Apollo, Clay | Focuses heavily on real-time *social* intent signals and audience tracking rather than just static firmographics [6] [7]. |
| **Social Media Management** | Hootsuite, Sprout Social | Integrates direct sales orchestration, CRM enrichment, and lead prioritization directly into the social publishing workflow [6]. |

*Takeaway: Highperformr's moat relies on its ability to merge social CMS capabilities with deep sales-intelligence workflows.*

## VI. Traction & Business Model
Highperformr operates on a tiered SaaS subscription model, targeting everyone from solo creators to enterprise RevOps teams [7].

| Plan Tier | Pricing | Target Audience | Key Features |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Free** | $0 | Solo users | 1 profile, unlimited scheduling, basic AI copilot [7]. |
| **Pro** | $17/mo (Annual) | Creators/Solopreneurs | 2 profiles, detailed analytics, smart automations [7]. |
| **Team** | $97/mo (Annual) | B2B Marketing Teams | Unlimited users, 25 profiles, approval workflows, draft sharing [7]. |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Sales & RevOps | Competitor watchlist, lead enrichment, sales conversation starters [7]. |

**Customer Traction:** Early case studies indicate strong ROI. Mid-market SaaS company Everstage reported a 40% higher response rate, 25% more responses from ABM campaigns, a 44% increase in pipeline through social lead generation, and over 60 hours saved monthly on manual research after adopting Highperformr [8].

## VII. Indian Market Context & Expansion Playbook
While incorporated in the US, Highperformr is deeply rooted in the Indian SaaS ecosystem. The founders explicitly stated their intention to use the $3.5M seed funding to build their R&D team in India [3]. By leveraging the engineering talent pool in Chennai—a major hub for SaaS companies like Zoho and Freshworks—Highperformr benefits from lower operational costs while targeting global enterprise clients [3] [4].

## IX. Risks, Information Gaps & Mitigation
**Information Gaps:** As of March 2026, there is no publicly available data regarding Highperformr's Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR), customer churn rates, or subsequent funding rounds (Series A) following their May 2024 Seed round. 
**Risks:** The lack of announced funding in late 2025 or early 2026 suggests the company is either operating leanly on its initial $3.5M, has raised undisclosed bridge capital, or is facing growth bottlenecks. Furthermore, reliance on scraping social signals (like LinkedIn activity) exposes the company to platform API changes and evolving data privacy regulations.

## X. Recommendations & Action Plan
1. **For Investors:** Mandate strict due diligence on current cash runway and ARR growth before considering follow-on investments, given the 22-month gap since the last announced funding.
2. **For Enterprise Buyers:** Pilot the platform specifically for "cold" account nurturing, measuring the lift in response rates against traditional intent tools like ZoomInfo.
