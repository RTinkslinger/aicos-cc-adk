# Indiassetz 2024-2026 Playbook: Turning India's Real-Estate Wealth Gap into a ₹2,000 Cr-AUM Business

## Executive Summary

Indiassetz is India's leading real estate wealth management advisory firm, designed to function as a "banking solution for those with real estate wealth" [1]. As of 2025, the bootstrapped company manages over ₹2,000 crore in Assets Under Management (AUM) for more than 3,000 registered High Net Worth Individuals (HNIs) and Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) globally [2] [1]. By combining a full-stack mobile application with local expertise, Indiassetz bridges the gap between traditional financial wealth management and physical property assets [3] [1]. 

## Company Overview

### Value Proposition & Core Offerings
Indiassetz professionally manages the entire lifecycle of a client's real estate wealth [1]. The company facilitates property transactions (buying, selling, renting), digital document storage, property valuations, and interior management [3] [4] [5]. A key differentiator is its financial assistance product; Indiassetz has tie-ups with major banks to help clients finance their real estate investments directly through the platform [6]. 

### Technology Platform
The company operates a dedicated mobile application (available on Android and iOS) that provides personalized recommendations curated for individual portfolio needs [5] [1]. The app allows users to manage documents digitally and track their investments from anywhere in the world [3] [1].

## Founding Team & Leadership

### Founder & CEO
Indiassetz was launched in 2014 by a group of former bankers [7] [1]. The company is led by Founder and CEO Shivam Sinha, who previously attended the Institute Of Finance and International Management in Bangalore [8]. Sinha's stated vision is to bring professionalism, transparency, and client-centricity to Indian real estate investment [1].

### Board & Key Executives
While the broader C-suite is not heavily publicized, corporate filings for Indiassetz Infra Services Private Limited reveal a network of Key Management Personnel and directors associated with entities like Subex Limited, Honeywell Technology Solutions, Arca Alternatives LLP, Vastu Housing Finance, and Gosree Finance [9]. 

## Funding History & Capital Structure

### Equity Capital
Indiassetz appears to be largely bootstrapped, with no publicly disclosed venture capital funding rounds [4] [9]. According to ZaubaCorp filings, the company has an authorized share capital of ₹95,000,000 (₹9.5 Cr) and a paid-up capital of ₹80,100,000 (₹8.01 Cr) [9]. 

### Debt & Bank Partnerships
The company utilizes secured loans to fuel its operations and financing products. Filings show historical charges (secured loans) including ₹5 million and ₹4.3 million from Axis Bank in 2021, and a significant ₹250 million (₹25 Cr) facility from Kotak Mahindra Bank in 2022 [9].
