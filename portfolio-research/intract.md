# Intract 2026 Deep Dive: The Rise, Pivot, and Outlook of India's Web-3 Learn-Earn Engine

## Executive Summary
Founded in 2022, Intract emerged as a dominant force in India's Web3 ecosystem, initially building a massive "learn and earn" quest platform that amassed over 12 million users [1]. Backed by a $3 million seed round in late 2023 from top-tier crypto funds [2], the company successfully bridged Web2 users into Web3 through incentivized marketing campaigns. However, as of early 2026, Intract has reached a critical strategic pivot, officially sunsetting its flagship quest platform on January 12, 2026 [1]. The company is now heavily leveraging its dual-product architecture, focusing on a high-volume cross-border payments engine that processes over $50.2 million in monthly volume [3]. This transition from a decentralized marketing hub to a global fintech infrastructure player marks a significant evolution in its business trajectory.

## Product & Service Portfolio

### Web3 Quest Engine (Sunsetting)
Intract's foundational product was a Web3 growth platform that allowed users to learn about blockchain technologies through interactive "quests" [4]. Users were incentivized with crypto, NFTs, and loyalty points for completing tasks like product exploration, quizzes, and social media engagement [2] [4]. To protect the ecosystem from bots, Intract developed a proprietary "Proof of Humanity" digital identity platform to verify real users [5] [4]. 

### Global Payments Infrastructure
Operating in parallel, Intract has built a robust cross-border payments platform designed for businesses. The system facilitates real-time payment settlement in under 30 minutes across more than 5 countries [3]. Supporting over 30 currencies, the platform eliminates traditional FX spread losses and multi-day settlement delays for remittance platforms, SaaS companies, and global marketplaces [3].

## Founding Team & Leadership
Intract was launched in 2022 by co-founders Abhishek Aggarwal (also cited as Abhishek Anita), Apurv Kaushal, and Sambhav Jain [2] [4]. The trio built their foundational relationship as friends studying together at IIT Delhi [2]. Under the leadership of CEO Sambhav Jain, the team successfully scaled the platform to profitability early in its lifecycle by combining strong network effects with a clear B2B value proposition [2].

## Funding History & Capital Structure
In December 2023, Intract secured a highly strategic $3 million seed funding round to scale its platform adoption [6] [2]. The capital was earmarked to double the team size and expand the user base [2].

## Market Position & Competitive Landscape
At its peak in the Web3 marketing sector, Intract facilitated over $1 billion in volume through partnerships with more than 1,000 companies, including industry giants like Binance, Polygon, and MetaMask [2]. By the time the quest platform announced its closure, it had recorded over 12 million questers, distributed $10 million+ in rewards, and executed 5,000+ partnerships [1]. On Crunchbase, the company maintains a high Growth Score of 90 [5]. 

## Recent News & Milestones (2023-2026)
* **December 2023:** Successfully closed a $3 million seed round to build the world's leading learn-and-earn platform [2].
* **August 2024:** Integrated Neon EVM, launching a DeFi voyage campaign to expand dApp discovery within the Solana/Ethereum ecosystem [7].
* **January 12, 2026:** Officially sunset the Intract Quest platform, preserving user XP and achievements while closing the doors on its foundational Web3 community product [1].

## Business Model & Revenue Streams
Intract's business model has historically relied on B2B marketing services. Web3 projects paid Intract to host quests, utilizing the platform for customer relationship management, growth hacking, and token gating [5]. With the sunsetting of the quest platform, revenue generation is shifting entirely toward its fintech operations. The cross-border payments model generates revenue by offering businesses clean FX rates and capturing margins on high-velocity, multi-currency settlements across its $50.2 million monthly transaction volume [3].
