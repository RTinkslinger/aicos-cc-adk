# Research Report: Kairos Computer — Engineering the "AI Intern" for Global Workflows

## Executive Summary
Kairos Computer is a high-growth AI startup positioned at the forefront of the "Agentic AI" movement, moving beyond simple conversational models to autonomous "AI Interns" that execute complex, multi-step workflows [1] [2]. Founded by a high-pedigree team with roots in Stanford, Google, and Sarvam AI, the company successfully bridges Indian engineering talent with Silicon Valley capital [3] [2] [4]. With $2.7M in seed funding led by Afore Capital and a product architecture that prioritizes "doing" over "talking," Kairos targets the massive productivity gap faced by solopreneurs and SMEs [2] [5]. Their recent shift toward native document collaboration and browser-based session syncing marks a strategic move to become an invisible, background-operating "AI employee" [2].

## Product & Service: The Autonomous AI Intern
Kairos operates as a personal AI intern that automates repetitive computer tasks [6]. Instead of requiring complex API configurations, users can simply record their screen and narrate what they are doing; Kairos learns the workflow and replicates it [7] [6]. 

The platform's core capabilities include:
* **Dedicated Cloud Browser:** Kairos uses its own virtual browser to navigate sites, click buttons, fill forms, and extract data, allowing it to work even when the user's physical device is switched off [1] [7].
* **Deep Integrations:** It natively integrates with over 20 applications, including Gmail, Notion, Google Sheets, Slack, and Calendar [1] [7].
* **Kairos Brain:** A recently launched architecture that allows the AI to work natively inside living documents and spreadsheets, eliminating the friction of downloading, editing, and re-uploading CSVs or markdown files [2].

## Founding Team: The Stanford-Google Connection
The leadership team possesses a rare blend of Silicon Valley experience and Indian deep-tech engineering roots. 

* **Manas Bam (Co-founder):** Leading Go-To-Market and growth, Bam previously worked at the Indian AI startup Sarvam AI and holds academic background from Stanford and NUS [8] [3] [2].
* **Samarth P. (Co-founder):** An ex-Google engineer with background from Stanford and NUS, leading the technical architecture and development of the "Kairos Brain" [2] [4].
* **Engineering Talent:** The company actively recruits top-tier Indian engineering talent, including AI engineers like Shashwat Dubey and Yogesh Chebrolu, to build out its autonomous agent capabilities [9] [10].

## Market Position: Agentic AI vs. Conversational LLMs
Kairos competes in the emerging "Action-Oriented AI" segment. While traditional LLMs like ChatGPT are designed to converse, Kairos is designed to execute [7]. By utilizing a dedicated browser and screen-recording learning, Kairos bypasses the fragility of traditional Robotic Process Automation (RPA) and the technical barriers of API-based tools like Zapier [7] [2]. This positions the company as a direct replacement for Virtual Assistants (VAs) and entry-level interns for tasks like competitor research, CRM updating, and outbound sales [7] [2].

## Recent News & Business Model
In early 2026, Kairos introduced a terminal-based "Session Sync" feature that securely grabs active browser sessions (like Gmail or Slack) from a user's laptop and syncs them to the cloud agent, effectively solving the friction of manual MFA and login loops [2]. Additionally, in March 2026, the company launched "Hiring with Kairos," allowing companies to automate candidate screening and interview scheduling directly through the AI [2].

Kairos operates on a usage-based SaaS model tied to "compute units," aligning revenue directly with the intensity of the AI's background work [7].

| Plan | Price | Key Features |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Free** | $0 | Basic AI assistant, scheduled tasks, browser automation. |
| **Basic** | $12/month | 100M compute units/mo, 2x usage. |
| **Plus** | $37/month | 350M compute units/mo, advanced browser automation. |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Custom agents, internal tooling connectors, dedicated support. |

*Takeaway:* The tiered pricing structure allows Kairos to capture value from casual solopreneurs while offering scalable, custom agent deployments for larger enterprise teams [7].
