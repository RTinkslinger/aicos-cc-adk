# Kintsugi: AI-Powered Sales Tax Automation Poised for the Indian GST Wave

## Executive Summary
Kintsugi is a San Francisco-based AI tax automation startup that transforms complex sales tax, VAT, and GST compliance into a streamlined, automated process [1] [2]. Valued at $150 million as of April 2025, the company has achieved hyper-efficient growth through a product-led model, boasting a 93% profit margin and a 0.1% churn rate [3]. Backed by a strategic partnership with global tax giant Vertex Inc., Kintsugi is actively expanding its operations into emerging markets, specifically targeting India to capitalize on the growing demand for automated GST and e-invoicing solutions among e-commerce and SaaS businesses [3] [4]. 

## Product and Service
Kintsugi provides an end-to-end AI-native tax automation platform designed for modern recurring revenue and e-commerce models [1] [2]. The platform replaces manual tax agency work by automating nexus monitoring, jurisdictional registration, real-time tax calculation, and remittance [1] [4]. 

Key product differentiators include:
* **Rapid Onboarding:** Users can install the app and view their tax liability in just seven clicks and three minutes, without needing to speak to sales personnel [5] [3].
* **Seamless Integrations:** The platform offers no-code integrations with major revenue-generating systems, including Shopify, Stripe, Chargebee, and QuickBooks, alongside a powerful API/SDK for custom workflows [1] [3].
* **Global GST & VAT Support:** Kintsugi's infrastructure supports international tax compliance, making it highly adaptable for Indian startups navigating complex GST frameworks [1] [3].

## Founding Team
The company was founded by a technical leadership team that combines deep data engineering expertise with tax compliance background:
* **Pujun Bhatnagar (CEO & Co-Founder):** A former Senior Machine Learning Engineer at Facebook, Bhatnagar leveraged his technical background and his father's 37-year career in tax compliance to build Kintsugi's AI engine [5] [6].
* **Jeff Gibson (CTO & Co-Founder):** Brings over 20 years of software development experience, having previously spearheaded the Data Platforms team at Atlassian for six years [5] [6].

## Business Model
Kintsugi utilizes a product-led growth strategy focused on delivering immediate value. The platform allows businesses to monitor their sales tax exposure and calculate liabilities entirely for free [7] [3]. The company monetizes by charging transparent, month-to-month fees for automated tax filing and remittance, with no hidden implementation costs or contract lock-ins [7] [3].
