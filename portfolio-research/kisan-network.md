# Kisan Network – India’s Tech-Enabled Farm-to-Market Platform: How a $3 M Seed-Funded Startup Is Re-Shaping Agribusiness (2024-2026 Outlook)

## Executive Summary
Kisan Network emerged as a promising agritech player in India, aiming to digitize the traditional agricultural supply chain. Backed by Y Combinator and a $3 million seed round, the company built a platform connecting farmers directly to buyers [1]. However, as of 2026, significant information gaps exist regarding its current operational status, highlighted by the departure of its co-founder and CEO in 2022 [2]. 

## Company Overview & Value Proposition

### End-to-End Supply Chain Integration
Kisan Network operates as a tech-enabled supply chain startup focused on the Indian agriculture sector [3]. The company's core value proposition is providing end-to-end, on-ground supply chain management that moves produce directly from the farm to the buyer's doorstep [1]. By cutting out traditional middlemen, Kisan Network offers transparent pricing and ensures quality control [1]. To achieve this, the platform utilizes computer vision technology to remotely assess the physical characteristics and quality of crops before they are shipped [4].

## Founding Team & Leadership

### Silicon Valley Pedigree Meets Agronomy
The company was founded in 2015 by the father-son duo of Sanjay Agarwalla and Aditya Agarwalla [1] [4]. 
* **Aditya Agarwalla (Co-Founder & Former CEO):** Aditya dropped out of Princeton University in his junior year to build Kisan Network, becoming a Thiel Fellow and participating in Y Combinator's Winter 2016 batch [1]. 
* **Sanjay Agarwalla (Co-Founder):** Provided the foundational agronomic and traditional business expertise required to navigate India's complex farming landscape [1].

**Critical Leadership Update:** Public profiles indicate that Aditya Agarwalla stepped down as CEO of Kisan Network in 2022 to co-found a new venture, Mera Gaon, where he served until 2024 [2]. The current executive leadership of Kisan Network as of 2026 remains publicly undisclosed.
