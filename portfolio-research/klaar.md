# Klaar 2025-26: How an AI-Powered Performance-Management Startup Is Leveraging India's Tech Talent to Crack the U.S. HR-Tech Market

## Executive Summary

Klaar is a rapidly growing B2B SaaS HR technology platform founded in Bengaluru, India, that specializes in AI-powered performance management and talent development [1] [2]. By combining deep human resources expertise with cutting-edge artificial intelligence, Klaar aims to replace clunky, outdated performance review systems with continuous, data-driven insights [3]. Following an $800,000 Seed round in 2023, the company recently secured a $5 million Series A in September 2025 to fuel its expansion into the United States and scale its Indian engineering hub [4] [2]. Klaar's platform is already utilized by major enterprises and unicorns, positioning it as a formidable emerging player in the global HR tech landscape [2].

## Company Overview & Founding Team

Klaar Digital Solutions Private Limited was founded in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India, in 2021/2022 [1] [5] [2]. The company was built by HR leaders to solve operational pain points they experienced firsthand [6]. 

The founding team brings a strong mix of domain expertise and operational execution:
* **Sharthok Chakraborty (Co-Founder & CEO):** Brings nearly 10 years of experience in HR and HR Tech, having previously served as a Regional VP of HR for Asia & ANZ [7]. He noted that the platform was built to solve the frustrations of "constantly battling with clunky and outdated tools" [3].
* **Atri Roy (Co-Founder & COO):** An XLRI Jamshedpur alumnus with a background as an experienced Program Manager, driving the operational and technological execution of the platform [5] [8].

## Product & Technology Deep-Dive

Klaar provides a comprehensive suite of tools designed to foster a seamless culture of high performance, engagement, and continuous development [2]. The platform leverages AI to provide data-driven insights, reduce bias in evaluations, and help leaders align teams with measurable outcomes [5]. 

| Core Module | Description & Capabilities |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Goals & OKRs** | Defines, aligns, cascades, and tracks company and individual goals with real-time AI-powered predictive insights [9]. |
| **Performance Reviews** | Uses AI to facilitate unbiased, continuous performance reviews and talent calibrations [9]. |
| **Continuous Feedback** | Enables reviews and one-on-one check-ins to power timely, meaningful coaching conversations [3] [5]. |
| **360-Degree Surveys** | Automates and shortens administrative tasks, such as reducing 360 Nomination times from four weeks to just two hours [2]. |

The company claims that organizations using Klaar's performance management solution outperform their competition by 23% [6].

## Business Model & Market Position

Operating as a B2B SaaS platform, Klaar targets goal-driven industries such as technology, professional services, finance, and manufacturing [3] [2]. The company serves a wide range of clients, from startups to Fortune 500 enterprises, including Philips, ITC, Godrej, Razorpay, T-Mobile, Vidyard, Armstrong, and Zenwork [3] [2].

In the highly competitive performance management market, Klaar competes directly with established platforms such as Lattice, 15Five, CultureAmp, Leapsome, Betterworks, HiBob, Textio, and Workboard [9] [5]. Klaar differentiates itself by focusing heavily on AI-enhanced tools that provide real-time help and reduce the time required to customize objectives and track results [10].

## Recent News & Expansion Milestones

In September 2025, Klaar officially announced its launch in the United States alongside the closing of its Series A round [3]. This expansion comes at a critical time when HR leaders are facing increased pressure to foster engagement and retain talent in hybrid and distributed work environments [3]. 

To support this global push, Klaar announced plans to increase its engineering and artificial intelligence workforce in Bengaluru by up to 40 percent over the next 12 months [4]. This India hub will remain central to building and innovating Klaar's AI-powered solutions as the company scales its presence in North America [4] [5].
