# “Klaire Studio – Handmade Luxury Accessories Scaling India’s Slow-Fashion Wave”

## Executive Summary

Klaire Studio is an emerging India-based luxury accessory brand that caters to modern, forward-thinking women through its unique portfolio of handmade hair wraps, pony wraps, and jewelry [1] [2]. Operating at the intersection of traditional craftsmanship and contemporary design, the startup champions a "slow fashion" ethos by utilizing a made-to-order production model [3]. Founded by Sheetanshi Shah and headquartered in Ahmedabad, the bootstrapped company leverages a hybrid business model encompassing direct-to-consumer (D2C) e-commerce, B2B wholesale, and corporate gifting [4] [5]. By designing its pieces in Ahmedabad and handcrafting them in Jaipur, Klaire Studio effectively balances regional artisanal heritage with modern aesthetic appeal [6].

## Company Overview & Product Offerings

Klaire Studio positions itself as a brand for the unconventional woman, offering vibrant, architectural, and nature-inspired accessories [7]. The company's core product lines include:

* **Hair & Pony Wraps:** Signature items like the "Sitaare Hair Wrap" (priced at Rs. 2,399) and the "Lune Hair Wrap" feature premium materials and architectural details designed to elevate everyday hairstyles [8] [9].
* **Mismatched Earrings & Jewelry:** The brand actively promotes mismatched earrings as a revolutionary fashion trend, appealing to consumers who favor asymmetrical, avant-garde aesthetics [7].

The company strictly adheres to a slow-fashion philosophy. Every piece is made-to-order, requiring 3-5 business days to ship, ensuring that each item is crafted with intentionality and minimal waste [3]. 

## Founding Team & Ownership

Klaire Studio is led by its founder, Sheetanshi Shah, who is based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat [4] [10]. Shah's background in luxury retail and design curation heavily influences the brand's premium positioning and aesthetic direction [11]. The company operates as an independent, founder-led entity, maintaining close ties to local artisans in Rajasthan to execute its designs [6].

## Funding History & Financials

Currently, Klaire Studio operates as a bootstrapped venture with no publicly disclosed external funding rounds from venture capital or angel investors. The business is a registered entity in India, operating under the GST number 24HAKPS2884D1ZC out of Paldi, Ahmedabad [12]. This self-financed approach aligns with its controlled, made-to-order inventory model, which inherently reduces overhead and inventory risks [3].

## Business Model & Revenue Streams

Klaire Studio utilizes a diversified, multi-channel revenue strategy to capture different market segments:

| Revenue Channel | Target Audience | Operational Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Direct-to-Consumer (D2C)** | Individual retail shoppers | Sales driven through the official Shopify store and Instagram. Offers free shipping across India to incentivize conversions [3]. |
| **B2B Wholesale** | Boutique owners and luxury retailers | Dedicated wholesale program allowing physical stores to stock Klaire jewelry, expanding offline brand visibility [5]. |
| **Corporate Gifting** | Businesses and corporate clients | Provides customized packaging and personalized accessory curation for corporate loyalty and gifting programs [5]. |

This hybrid approach allows the brand to maintain high margins on D2C sales while securing volume through wholesale and corporate partnerships.

## Market Position & Recent News

Klaire Studio occupies the premium tier of the Indian accessory market. By marketing its products as "Luxe Accessory" items designed for "new-age women," the brand differentiates itself from mass-market fast fashion [1]. 

Recent brand activities heavily revolve around social media-driven product drops and localized community engagement. For instance, the brand teased exclusive product drops in September 2025 [13] and recently hosted a pop-up event in Ahmedabad to connect directly with its local customer base and showcase its founder's vision [4].

## Strategic Recommendations

To accelerate growth and solidify its market position, Klaire Studio should consider the following strategic actions:

1. **Formalize the B2B Partner Program:** Create a tiered wholesale structure with volume-based incentives to aggressively expand physical retail presence beyond Gujarat and Rajasthan.
2. **Leverage the "Slow Fashion" Narrative:** Prominently feature the artisans of Jaipur and the sustainable, made-to-order process in digital marketing campaigns to justify premium pricing and attract eco-conscious consumers.
3. **Introduce an "Express" Product Line:** While the 3-5 day made-to-order window aligns with slow fashion [3], offering a limited selection of pre-made, ready-to-ship bestsellers could capture impulse buyers who are deterred by longer wait times.
