# Mantys: AI-Powered Hospital Front-Office Automation in India and the GCC

## Executive Summary

Mantys is a Bengaluru-based startup that has successfully transitioned from a SaaS financial analytics platform into a specialized AI-driven healthcare automation provider. By targeting the administrative bottlenecks in hospital front offices, Mantys automates patient registration, insurance eligibility verification, and claims reconciliation [1] [2]. The company has achieved rapid early adoption, currently serving over 50 healthcare providers and processing more than 12 million patient visits annually [1]. With backing from prominent investors like Y Combinator and Peak XV Partners [3], Mantys is positioned to capture significant market share in the GCC and Indian healthcare sectors by reducing manual errors and accelerating revenue cycles for medical facilities.

## Company Overview & Founding Team

Mantys was founded in 2021/2022 and is headquartered in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India [3] [4]. The company was established by Kriti Arora, who currently serves as CEO, and Mudit Dangi, who is listed as a former co-founder [4]. Kriti Arora previously worked as a financial analyst at A91 Partners, where she identified a gap in financial planning tools for high-growth companies, leading to the initial iteration of Mantys [5]. The company operates as a privately held entity with a lean team of approximately 8 to 12 employees [3] [6]. 

## Product & Service Offering

Mantys initially launched as an ARR reporting and forecasting tool for SaaS companies during Y Combinator's Winter 2023 batch [6]. However, the company has since pivoted its core product to focus entirely on healthcare administration. The Mantys AI platform acts as an "operational heartbeat" for modern hospitals, offering the following core modules:

* **Eligibility Verification:** Instantly checks insurance coverage and auto-verifies scheduled appointments [1].
* **Patient Registration:** Automates pre-registration workflows and integrates seamlessly with existing EHR/PMS systems [1].
* **Claims Reconciliation:** Automatically matches claims, payments, and remittances to prevent revenue leakage [1].
* **Prior Authorization & Agentic Flows:** Utilizes proprietary verification models that continuously learn from claims patterns to handle edge cases [1].

The platform boasts a 10x increase in accuracy and saves healthcare providers over 100,000 hours daily across the GCC [1].

## Funding History

Mantys is a venture-backed startup that has raised a total of $500K in funding [3] [4]. The company completed its Seed funding round on March 6, 2023 [4]. The round was led by Y Combinator, with participation from prominent venture capital firms including Peak XV Partners (formerly Sequoia Capital India), Neon Fund, and DeVC [1] [3]. 

## Recent News & Strategic Outlook

Recent deployments highlight the tangible ROI of Mantys's platform. At Shifa Al Jazeera Medical Center, a multi-specialty group with five branches, the implementation of Mantys AI resulted in a 60% drop in eligibility errors and a 20% increase in patient throughput within just three months [2]. Similarly, at Aster Hospital in Dubai, Mantys reduced the time required for eligibility checks from 8 minutes to less than 2 minutes, resulting in cleaner claims and fewer rejections [2]. 

Moving forward, Mantys is well-positioned to expand its footprint across Tier-2 Indian hospitals and deepen its penetration in the GCC. However, to scale its sales engine and maintain its technological edge against well-funded competitors, the company will likely need to pursue a Series A funding round in the near future.
