# Marrfa — Disrupting Cross‑Border Dubai Real‑Estate Investment for Indian Capital (2026 Strategic Snapshot)

## Executive Summary
Marrfa is a fast-growing prop-tech startup that serves as a trusted companion for individuals seeking to purchase or invest in Dubai's dynamic real estate market [1]. Founded in 2023 by serial entrepreneur Jamil Ahmad, the company targets the massive $270 billion unstructured cross-border real estate market by democratizing access to high-yield investments [1] [2]. Leveraging an AI-powered advisory platform, Marrfa bridges the gap between global retail investors—particularly from India—and lucrative Dubai properties [3] [2]. Backed by global venture capital firm Foundamental, the company operates on a dual B2B and B2C business model and has demonstrated significant transaction momentum in early 2026, closing multi-million dirham deals across premium Dubai developments [1] [4] [2].

## Company Overview & Founding Team
Headquartered in Business Bay, Dubai, Marrfa was established in 2023 to simplify overseas property investments [1] [4]. The company is led by Founder and CEO Jamil Ahmad, a serial entrepreneur previously credited with co-founding Fashinza and scaling it to a $300 million valuation across four continents within three years [1] [2]. Ahmad utilizes his extensive experience in cross-border trade flows to innovate within the traditional real estate industry, aiming to make high-yield investments accessible to a broader audience [2]. The core team currently consists of 11 to 50 employees, including specialized property consultants and country directors [1] [4].

## Product & Service: AI-Driven Real Estate Advisory
Marrfa provides an end-to-end digital platform that allows users to research markets, evaluate properties, and handle paperwork seamlessly [3]. A core differentiator is its proprietary "Marrfa.AI" Customer Investment Advisor, an AI chatbot designed to help users find perfect properties to buy, sell, or rent [5]. 

The platform exclusively partners with licensed developers and top real estate agencies, ensuring all transactions comply with Dubai's Real Estate Regulatory Agency (RERA) to keep investments secure [3]. Marrfa's platform provides comprehensive information access with a single click, enabling investors to target properties that typically yield a 6% to 9% annual ROI in Dubai [3] [2].

## Funding History
Marrfa successfully closed its pre-seed funding round on March 27, 2024 [4] [2]. The undisclosed investment amount was led by Foundamental, a global VC firm based in Berlin, Germany [2]. The round also saw significant participation from Prashant Malik, General Partner at Tykhe Block Ventures and an early Facebook engineer, who invested in his individual capacity [2]. Investors highlighted Marrfa's unique approach to unifying developers, buyers, and financiers in rapidly growing, under-served markets [2].

## Market Position & Business Model
Marrfa operates on both B2C and B2B business models [1]. It positions itself as a gateway for international capital flowing into the UAE, specifically addressing the intricate challenges of the $270 billion unstructured cross-border real estate market [2]. By targeting first-time international buyers and serial investors alike, Marrfa capitalizes on Dubai's strong upward market trend driven by economic growth and population rise [3].
