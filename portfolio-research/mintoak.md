# Mintoak 2024-26: Scaling a Bank-First Merchant SaaS Platform through Profitable Growth, CBDC Play & Strategic Acquisitions

## Executive Summary

Mintoak has established itself as a highly profitable, bank-first merchant Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform in India's competitive fintech landscape. Unlike direct-to-merchant acquirers, Mintoak provides white-labeled solutions that empower traditional banks to digitize and monetize their SME customer base [1]. Between 2024 and early 2026, the company achieved significant milestones, including turning highly profitable, expanding into Central Bank Digital Currency (CBDC) solutions through the acquisition of Digiledge, and raising debt to acquire Dubai-based ICC Loyalty [2] [3] [4]. 

## 1. Company Overview: What Mintoak Does

### Product and Value Proposition
Mintoak operates a modular, cloud-native, and API-first merchant SaaS platform [5] [6]. The company provides a white-labeled merchant application that banks can offer to their Small and Medium Enterprise (SME) customers [1]. 

The platform's core modules include:
* **DigiOnboard:** Digitizes merchant risk assessment and generates Merchant/Terminal IDs [7].
* **SoundHub:** Provides low-latency messaging and instant soundbox activation [7].
* **Business360 & Cross-sell:** Enables real-time analytics and the cross-selling of pre-approved loans, insurance, and business credit cards [7].
* **Bill Payments & CBDC:** Facilitates utility payments and e-rupee acceptance [7] [8].

By partnering with Mintoak, banks can offer a comprehensive digital ecosystem without building the technology in-house, while merchants benefit from a single app for payments, analytics, and financial services [1] [6].

## 5. Recent News and Milestones (2024-2026)

Mintoak has executed an aggressive expansion and acquisition strategy over the past two years:

* **October 2024:** Partnered with Axis Bank to launch "Neo for Merchants," a payment acceptance and commerce enablement app [6].
* **January 2025:** Closed an INR 71 Crore secondary funding round led by Z3Partners [9].
* **March 2025:** Acquired Digiledge for approximately $3.5 million. This marked India's first major CBDC-related acquisition, enabling Mintoak's partner banks (HDFC, Axis, SBI) to offer e-rupee payment solutions [8] [4].
* **March 2026:** Board approved raising Rs 80 crore in debt from BlackSoil to acquire Dubai-based ICC Loyalty, a customer loyalty management platform, signaling a push into the Middle East and enhanced merchant retention tools [3].
