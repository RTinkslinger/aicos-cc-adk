# Modus Secure (Modus AI): India's Emerging AI-Driven Fraud-Detection Co-Pilot

## Executive Summary

Modus Secure, operating primarily under the brand Modus AI, is a Bengaluru-based early-stage startup founded in 2025 that provides an AI-powered fraud detection co-pilot for financial institutions [1] [2]. Backed by prominent investors including WTFund and Nikhil Kamath, and recently selected for the government-backed SAMRIDH program, the company is positioning itself as a critical infrastructure layer for risk and compliance teams at major Indian banks and fintechs [1] [3] [4]. With a recent $6.5M grant injection, Modus AI represents a high-potential, agile player in the rapidly growing cybersecurity and fraud prevention market [2].

## Company Overview

Incorporated as Modus AI Private Limited in 2025, the company is headquartered in Indiranagar, Bengaluru, Karnataka [1] [2]. Operating as a privately held entity, Modus AI currently maintains a lean team of 2-10 employees [1]. The company's core mission is to serve as an AI-driven fraud detection co-pilot, helping financial institutions piece together fraudsters' modus operandi faster and more accurately [2] [5].

## Product & Technology

Modus AI is building specialized AI infrastructure for risk and compliance teams [1]. The technology functions as a co-pilot that utilizes artificial intelligence to analyze patterns and behaviors, enabling the accurate detection of fraudulent activities [2]. 

Key technological pillars include:
* **Multi-Agent Orchestration:** Deploying AI agents that can reason, plan, and execute complex compliance reviews [1].
* **Graph Machine Learning (Graph ML):** Utilizing advanced graph analytics to map relational links and reveal coordinated fraud rings [1].
* **Comprehensive Coverage:** The platform handles merchant monitoring, compliance review, fraud detection, case resolution, document review, and due diligence [6].

## Founders & Leadership Team

The company is spearheaded by co-founders Somesh Lund and Sunit Gautam [1] [7]. The leadership and early core team bring significant pedigree from top-tier global and regional tech and finance giants. The team comprises former professionals from Goldman Sachs, KPMG, Apple Card, Uber, and Niyo, providing them with deep domain expertise in financial risk management and scalable technology [1]. 

## Funding History & Capital Structure

Modus AI has successfully secured early-stage capital to fuel its product development. 

* **Latest Round:** The company raised a Grant (prize money) round on February 13, 2025, securing approximately $6.5M ($6,500,576) [2].
* **Key Backers:** The startup is backed by WTFund (which publicly announced its support for the founders' mission to tackle rising financial fraud), Inuka Capital, DeVC, and prominent angel investor Nikhil Kamath [1] [3]. 
* **Government Support:** The company also benefits from backing by the Government of India through strategic startup initiatives [1].

## Recent News & Milestones

Modus AI has recently achieved significant validation milestones:
* **SAMRIDH Program Selection:** In early 2025, Modus AI was selected for the prestigious SAMRIDH program, a notable milestone that provides government-backed acceleration and credibility in the Indian tech ecosystem [1] [4].
* **Active Expansion:** The company is currently in a private beta phase with limited pilot partnerships and is actively hiring for engineering roles (specifically in multi-agent orchestration and Graph ML) at its HSR Layout office [1].

## Business Model

Operating as a B2B enterprise software provider, Modus AI targets India's largest fintechs, banks, and insurers [1] [3]. By offering a specialized AI co-pilot, the company aims to embed itself into the core compliance and risk workflows of these financial institutions, likely monetizing through enterprise SaaS licensing, usage-based API queries, or pilot partnership contracts [1].
