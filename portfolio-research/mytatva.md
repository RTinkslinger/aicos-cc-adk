# GoodFlip (formerly MyTatva): How India's First Metabolic-Health Digital-Therapeutics Platform Is Shaping Chronic-Care Tech

## Executive Summary
TatvaCare, operating primarily through its rebranded consumer platform GoodFlip (formerly MyTatva), is an Indian health-tech company focused on chronic care management and clinic digitization [1] [2]. Founded in 2020, the company offers a suite of AI-powered digital therapeutics and practice management tools [3] [2]. Despite scaling to over 3,000 users and securing major clinical empanelments in 2026, the company remains officially unfunded, presenting both strategic opportunities and valuation opacity for potential partners [4] [3] [5].

## 1. Company Overview – From MyTatva to GoodFlip
Originally launched as MyTatva in 2021, the platform was developed by Ahmedabad and Bangalore-based Digicare Health Solutions Pvt. Ltd. (TatvaCare) [4] [5]. The company recently rebranded its patient-facing application to GoodFlip, pivoting from a basic vitals-tracking app to a comprehensive digital therapeutics platform targeting metabolic conditions like PCOS, Fatty Liver, Diabetes, and Obesity [2] [5]. 

## 2. Product Portfolio & Value Proposition
TatvaCare operates a multi-tiered ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between patients, doctors, and researchers [2]. 

| Product Name | Target Audience | Core Capabilities |
|---|---|---|
| **GoodFlip (formerly MyTatva)** | Patients | Digital therapeutics for metabolic health, CGM integration, lifestyle coaching, habit tracking, and science-backed nutraceuticals [2] [5]. |
| **TatvaPractice** | Clinics & Hospitals | AI-powered, ABDM-certified Electronic Medical Record (EMR) platform for practice management [2]. |
| **Evalus AI** | Pharma & Biotech | Real-world evidence platform for running late-phase clinical studies with electronic data capture [2]. |

This interconnected suite allows TatvaCare to capture value across the entire healthcare continuum, from clinical workflows to at-home patient adherence [2].

## 3. Founding Team & Leadership
TatvaCare was founded in 2020, with early records indicating Gouri Gore and Gunjan Jain as founders [3]. The current executive leadership driving the company's expansion includes Manoj Balaji as Chief Executive Officer (CEO) and Javed Khan as Chief Technology Officer (CTO) [1] [2]. 

## 4. Funding & Financial Snapshot
Despite its operational scale and multiple office locations in Ahmedabad and Bangalore, TatvaCare and its GoodFlip subsidiary are currently listed as "unfunded" across major startup databases as of early 2026 [4] [3] [5]. 

**Information Gap Flag:** There is no publicly available data regarding the company's Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR), Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC), or external capital raised. This opacity makes independent valuation challenging.

## 5. Market Position & Competitive Landscape
In the Indian digital health market, GoodFlip competes with consumer fitness apps and digital pharmacy chronic-care bundles. However, GoodFlip differentiates itself through its deep clinical integration. By leveraging its ABDM-certified TatvaPractice EMR, the company creates a B2B2C funnel where doctors directly prescribe GoodFlip programs to patients [2]. 

## 6. Business Model, Traction & Key Metrics
GoodFlip operates on a subscription-based care program model (e.g., 6-month condition-specific programs), though exact pricing tiers are not publicly published [5]. 

| Metric Category | Disclosed Traction |
|---|---|
| **User Base** | 3,000+ users on the GoodFlip platform [5]. |
| **Clinical Outcomes** | Users report up to 15% reduction in SGPT/SGOT values in three months and significant weight loss (e.g., 5.5 kgs) [5]. |
| **Research Validation** | A retrospective study of 823 patients demonstrated the clinical utility of the GoodFlip Metabolic Score in assessing metabolic parameters [2]. |

## 7. Recent Milestones (2024-2026)
* **March 2024:** TatvaCare announced a strategic partnership to leverage Microsoft Azure OpenAI for transforming healthcare practices [6].
* **February 2026:** The company won "Best Engineering Team of the Year (HealthTech)" at Quantic's 10th Edition India DevOps Show for its seamless Continuous Glucose Monitor (CGM) integration [2].
* **Early 2026:** TatvaPractice was officially empanelled by the Research Society for the Study of Diabetes in India (RSSDI) as an approved Clinic Management System, a critical milestone given that less than 40% of Indian diabetes clinics currently use a CMS [2].

## 8. Regulatory & Compliance Landscape
TatvaCare is heavily aligned with India's digital health infrastructure. Its TatvaPractice platform is Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM) certified, ensuring interoperability with national health registries [2]. 

## 9. Risks, Gaps & Open Questions
While the clinical and technical traction is evident, several critical information gaps remain:
1. **Pricing Opacity:** GoodFlip does not publicly list the cost of its enterprise or consumer care programs [5].
2. **Financial Metrics:** The lack of disclosed funding, revenue, and unit economics prevents a full assessment of the company's financial runway [4] [3].

## 10. Strategic Recommendations
For potential investors or enterprise partners, TatvaCare represents a highly capable, bootstrapped asset in the Indian health-tech space. Interested parties should request confidential financial disclosures to assess unit economics. Furthermore, GoodFlip should leverage its recent RSSDI empanelment to aggressively expand its B2B clinic licensing, using doctors as the primary acquisition channel for its consumer therapeutics platform.
