# NuPort Robotics: Retrofit Autonomy for India's Trucking Market

## Executive Summary

NuPort Robotics is an autonomous driving company that provides a capital-light, retrofit Level-2 Advanced Driver Assistance System (ADAS) for heavy-duty commercial vehicles [1] [2]. Originally founded in Canada, the company has strategically expanded its operations into India, establishing a base in Pune to address the country's critical road safety challenges [1]. By upgrading existing fleets rather than requiring the purchase of new autonomous vehicles, NuPort offers immediate operational expenditure (OPEX) savings and productivity gains [3]. With recent expansions into 3PL e-commerce logistics and a pipeline of commercial agreements slated for 2026, NuPort is positioning itself as a pragmatic, scalable player in the global and Indian supply chain automation markets [4] [5].

## Founding Team & Organizational Footprint

NuPort Robotics was founded in 2019 by Raghavender Sahdev (Co-Founder & CEO) and Bao Xin Chen (Co-Founder & CTO) [6] [2]. Sahdev brings extensive experience as an AI entrepreneur, while Chen provides deep technical expertise in robotics, both having educational backgrounds from York University [5] [7]. While headquartered in Toronto, Ontario, with a lean team of 1-10 employees, the company has established a significant operational footprint in India through a strategic collaboration with the World Trade Centre (WTC) Pune [1] [2].

## Product & Technology

NuPort's core offering is a proprietary retrofit technology designed to turn existing conventional trucks into smarter, safer vehicles without disrupting ongoing operations [3]. 

### Core Hardware and Software Capabilities
The company's technology focuses on Level-2 autonomy, which includes:
* **Customized Sensor Hardware:** Compatible with all truck OEMs, providing 360-degree sensor coverage up to 500 meters [3].
* **Redundant Safety Systems:** Ensures high reliability in obstacle and collision avoidance [3] [8].
* **Fleet Assist Application:** A digital platform that streamlines dispatching, route optimization, and safety compliance by providing real-time vehicle and driver insights [2].

## Business Model & Revenue Streams

NuPort operates on a "Retrofit-as-a-Service" model. Instead of forcing fleet operators to purchase expensive new autonomous trucks, NuPort modernizes existing fleets [3]. This approach delivers immediate OPEX savings through lower fuel, labor, and maintenance costs [3]. 

Recently, NuPort has diversified its business model by integrating with CarryBee to offer a complete third-party logistics (3PL) ecosystem [4]. This partnership unifies fulfillment, warehousing, and last-mile delivery into a single platform, allowing NuPort to tap into the lucrative e-commerce logistics sector [4] [9].

## Market Position & India Strategy

In April 2022, NuPort officially entered the Indian market to deploy its technology at scale, targeting the country's massive heavy-duty commercial vehicle sector [1]. Partnering with WTC Pune, NuPort aims to improve road safety and assist Indian manufacturers of electric trucks and buses [1] [11]. By focusing on Level-2 ADAS rather than full Level-4 autonomy, NuPort bypasses severe regulatory hurdles, offering a pragmatic safety solution that aligns with India's push toward sustainable and safe electric mobility [11].

## Recent News & Strategic Moves

Heading into 2026, NuPort has maintained strong commercial momentum. CEO Raghavender Sahdev noted that 2025 was a highly productive year, setting the stage for the company to enter multiple commercial agreements in 2026 [5]. Furthermore, the strategic integration with CarryBee, announced recently, marks a significant expansion from pure trucking autonomy into end-to-end e-commerce logistics, providing brands with a unified fulfillment and delivery platform [4] [9].
