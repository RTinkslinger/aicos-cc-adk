## 1. Company Overview & Value Proposition

Oberoi Realty is a Mumbai-based real estate development company focused on premium developments across residential, office space, retail, hospitality, and social infrastructure segments [6]. The company's core value proposition lies in creating "destination developments" by integrating residential projects with premium office space, retail, and hospitality, which enhances the desirability and value of its residential units [6]. 

## 2. Founding Team & Leadership

The company is led by Chairman and Managing Director Vikas Oberoi, who drives the brand's vision and strategy [7]. The financial strategy is helmed by Saumil Daru, who has been associated with the company since 2002 and serves as the Director of Finance and Chief Financial Officer [8] [9]. The board also includes Bindu Oberoi as a Non-Independent, Non-Executive Director [7].

## 3. Funding History & Capital Structure

Oberoi Realty has a strong history of attracting institutional capital. The company executed its Initial Public Offering (IPO) in October 2010, raising up to Rs 1,500 crore through the issuance of 39,562,000 equity shares [10] [11]. The issue was managed by Kotak Mahindra Capital, Enam Securities, J.P. Morgan, and Morgan Stanley [6]. 

Recently, in October 2024, the company's board of directors approved plans to raise up to Rs 6,000 crore in one or more tranches to fund future expansion [1]. The company maintains a highly conservative balance sheet, with a gross debt-to-equity ratio of just 0.18x and a net debt-to-equity ratio of 0.09x as of FY24 [3].

## 4. Portfolio & Market Position

Oberoi Realty operates primarily in Mumbai, which remains India's largest residential market and saw an 11% increase in housing sales in 2024, driven heavily by the luxury segment [4] [12].

## 5. Recent Performance & Traction (FY24-FY25)

The company has demonstrated exceptional financial traction. For the third quarter of FY25 (ending December 2024), Oberoi Realty reported a 71.70% YoY increase in net profit to Rs 618.38 crore, with revenue from operations growing 34% YoY to Rs 1,411.08 crore [1]. 

For the full fiscal year FY25, provisional data indicates booking values jumped 31% YoY to Rs 5,266 crore, with 928 units sold (a 32% increase from FY24) [2]. Total carpet area sold in FY25 reached 12.8 lakh square feet [2].

## 6. Key News & Milestones (2024-2026)

* **February 2024:** Entered an agreement with Marriott International to develop luxury hospitality projects in the Mumbai Metropolitan Region [5].
* **May 2024:** Conferred 'ESG Champions of India 2024' by Dun & Bradstreet [3].
* **October 2024:** Launched Oberoi Garden City in Thane, achieving a gross booking value of Rs 1,348 crore for 5.65 lakh square feet within the first three days [1].
* **December 2024:** Signed a development agreement for luxury villas and a hotel on an 81.05-acre land parcel in Alibaug, Maharashtra [1].

## 7. Business Model & Revenue Streams

Oberoi Realty employs a diversified business model across its segments. It utilizes a sale model for residential projects, collecting approximately 20% as a down payment at booking, with the remainder linked to construction milestones [6]. For commercial and retail assets (like Oberoi Mall and Commerz), it uses a lease model to generate stable, recurring cash flows [6]. Hospitality projects operate under an operating agreement model where the hotel is owned by Oberoi and operated by a global chain (e.g., Marriott) [6].

## 8. Risks, Constraints & Information Gaps

**Risks:** The company faces significant geographic concentration risk, as its operations and land banks are almost entirely focused in the Mumbai Metropolitan Region and surrounding areas (Thane, Alibaug) [6] [1]. Furthermore, execution lag is visible in newer projects like Forestville, which has yet to reach its project completion threshold for revenue recognition [3]. 

**Information Gaps:** Publicly available data lacks a detailed debt maturity schedule, specific tenant concentration metrics for its retail and commercial portfolios, and granular unit-level pricing for the FY25 bookings surge. 

## 9. Investment Thesis & Recommendations

Oberoi Realty presents a compelling growth story anchored by Mumbai's booming luxury real estate market and a highly disciplined balance sheet. The company's ability to generate Rs 5,266 crore in FY25 bookings while maintaining a 0.18x gross debt-to-equity ratio provides massive optionality for future land acquisitions [3] [2]. 

**Recommendation:** Buy/Hold. Investors should capitalize on the strong cash flow generation and upcoming Rs 6,000 crore capital raise, which signals aggressive expansion [1]. However, close monitoring of project completion timelines (specifically Elysian and Sky City) is required to ensure revenue recognition targets are met.
