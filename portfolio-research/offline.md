# Offline: India's Ultra-Exclusive Founder Club

## Executive Summary

Offline is a highly curated, private membership network designed exclusively for India's top tech founders and CEOs. Founded in 2023 by former AngelList India head Utsav Somani, the platform serves as a "third place"—distinct from home and the office—where high-growth entrepreneurs can engage in "professional therapy" and unfiltered peer-to-peer problem solving [1] [2]. By enforcing strict eligibility criteria, a strict no-pitch policy, and high membership fees, Offline has positioned itself as the premier sanctuary for India's startup elite, prioritizing deep, confidential connections over mass networking [1] [3].

## Product and Service Offering

Offline’s core offering revolves around hyper-curated peer groups and exclusive experiences designed to combat founder isolation. 

### Core Experience: Monthly Pods
Members are placed into carefully selected "pods" of 6 to 10 peers, matched by company size, sector, and experience level, ensuring no direct competitors are in the same group [3]. These pods meet monthly for 2.5 to 3 hours in off-the-record sessions facilitated by executive coaches [1] [3]. The meetings are guided by "The Offline Playbook," developed with inputs from Georgia Dienst of the Mochary Method [1] [3]. 

### Events and Digital Community
Beyond the pods, Offline offers:
* **Signature Events:** Quarterly Michelin-star dinners, private art exhibitions, and music festivals [3].
* **Annual Retreats:** 2-3 day immersive experiences focused on technology, art, and leadership [3].
* **Digital Access:** A 24/7 digital community for immediate advice, a member directory, and a weekly newsletter called *The Offline Post* [3].

## Founding Team

Offline is led by a team with deep roots in the Indian startup and hospitality ecosystems:
* **Utsav Somani (Founder):** Previously oversaw AngelList's local operations in India for five years, building a massive network of over 150 startups as an investor [1].
* **Fabiola Monteiro (Narrative & Experience Lead):** Brings curation expertise from Paper Planes and Nat Geo Traveller India [4].
* **Sahil Talwar (Community and Partnerships Lead):** Formerly at EquityList [4].
* **Sharon Pereira & Aparna Pande:** Lead member success and experiences, drawing from backgrounds at Oberoi Hotels and Network 18 [4].

## Funding History

Offline launched with significant backing from the very demographic it aims to serve. In September 2023, the company secured a **$2 million seed round** [1] [2]. 

| Investor Category | Notable Backers | Strategic Value |
|-------------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **Unicorn Founders** | Co-founders of Groww, Razorpay, Polygon, CoinDCX, Shiprocket, Ultrahuman, BharatPe [1] [2] | Instant credibility and a built-in referral network for elite members. |
| **Venture Capital** | Peak XV Partners, Better Capital, DeVC, Huddle, Riverwalk Ventures [1] | Institutional backing and access to high-growth portfolio founders. |

This cap table allows Offline's investors to act as early customers and brand ambassadors within their own extensive networks [1].

## Business Model

Offline operates on a premium, paid-community model that targets profitability from Day 1 by avoiding high customer acquisition costs [2]. 

### Pricing and Eligibility
The financial barrier to entry includes an annual fee of approximately **$6,000 (500,000 INR)** and a one-time onboarding fee of **$600** [1]. 

To even apply, candidates must meet rigorous criteria:

| Eligibility Metric | Minimum Requirement |
|--------------------|---------------------|
| **Funding Raised** | $10 million [3] (Note: Early reports cited $5M [1]) |
| **Annual Revenue** | $5 million+ [3] |
| **Previous Exit** | $10 million [3] |
| **Corporate Role** | CXO at a startup with a $50M+ valuation [3] |

Meeting these metrics only grants an interview; final admission is vetted by a Membership Advisory Committee to ensure cultural fit [1]. The company intentionally limits scale, aiming for only a "couple hundred members in the first couple of years" [1].

## Market Position

Offline occupies a unique niche in the Indian market. While traditional multi-geography organizations like the Young Presidents’ Organization (YPO) and Entrepreneurs’ Organization (EO) have long existed for legacy business owners, Indian tech startup founders lacked a dedicated, modern equivalent [1]. 

Globally, Offline's model mirrors US-based networks like **Hampton** (founded by Sam Parr) and the women-centric network **Chief** [1]. Within its target regions, Offline currently operates chapters in Bangalore, Delhi, Mumbai, and Dubai, with plans to expand into Singapore [1] [3].

## Recent News

Since its high-profile launch and funding in late 2023, Offline has established itself as a premium hub for thought leadership. The club has hosted exclusive talks with prominent global tech figures, such as Gokul Rajaram (board member/investor at Google, Meta, Coinbase) [5]. Furthermore, founder Utsav Somani remains highly active in the broader ecosystem, recently co-leading a $1.85M seed round in Thane-based Grexa AI alongside other prominent investors [6]. By 2025, Offline is widely cited as the benchmark for premium, private founder communities in India [7] [8].
