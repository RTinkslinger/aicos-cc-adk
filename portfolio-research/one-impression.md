# One Impression: India's Full-Stack Influencer Marketplace

## Executive Summary
One Impression is a Gurgaon-based technology startup that has built a comprehensive, full-stack influencer marketing platform. Founded in 2018, the company bridges the gap between brands and content creators by automating the entire campaign lifecycle—from discovery and pricing to compliance and payouts [1] [2]. With access to over 7 million creators globally and backing from major investors like Krafton, One Impression has positioned itself as a critical infrastructure layer in India's rapidly expanding creator economy, serving over 500 top-tier brands including Unilever, Amazon, and Meta [1]. 

## Founding Team & Vision
One Impression (legally Anycast Technology Private Limited) was founded in July 2018 by brothers Apaksh Gupta and Jivesh Gupta [1] [2]. Apaksh Gupta, who serves as CEO, previously co-founded SummerLabel, bringing prior entrepreneurial experience to the venture [1]. Jivesh Gupta serves as the company's COO [1]. The founders' vision is to democratize the influencer marketing economy by replacing fragmented, manual agency processes with an "Amazon-like" platform that offers speed, scale, and reliability for both brands and creators [2] [3].

## Product Portfolio & Business Model
One Impression operates a two-sided marketplace that monetizes through brand fees and creator commissions, with plans to introduce premium subscription-based tools for brands [2]. The company's product suite includes:

* **Core Campaign Management Platform:** A full-stack solution that allows brands to discover creators, access standardized pricing, facilitate compliant contracts, ensure on-time delivery, and automate payouts [1] [4]. It supports real-time performance tracking and is currently enabled for Instagram and YouTube, with plans to integrate LinkedIn, Moj, and TikTok [2].
* **Amplify:** An AI-powered service designed to generate ad-ready short-form videos from human creators within 72 hours. The service features fixed, transparent pricing starting at ₹2,900, eliminating traditional back-and-forth negotiations [5] [6].

The startup has successfully raised $11 million across two primary funding rounds, demonstrating strong investor confidence in its growth trajectory [7]. The company stated an ambitious goal to reach $40 million in annual recurring revenue (ARR) by 2024 [7].

## Market Position & Competitive Landscape
One Impression operates in a booming sector; the Indian influencer marketing industry was estimated at ₹3,600 crore in 2024 and is projected to grow by 25% in 2025 [8] [9]. The platform is active in over 14 countries, including India, the USA, the UAE, and Indonesia [1]. It has successfully generated over 100,000 content pieces across more than 10 languages [2] [4]. 

The company competes with other Indian influencer marketplaces and data platforms such as Qoruz, Influencer.in, and Phyllo [10] [11] [7]. However, One Impression differentiates itself by offering end-to-end workflow automation rather than just creator discovery [2]. A notable success is its work with Galderma (Cetaphil), where the platform helped generate over 700 content pieces, reaching 100 million unique viewers and driving a 46% spike in organic search [12].

## Recent News & Strategic Outlook
Following its $10 million Series A, One Impression announced aggressive expansion plans. The company is utilizing the fresh capital to build its marketing team and scale its sales force to 50-70 people [2] [7]. Strategically, One Impression is targeting international expansion, starting with Southeast Asia and the UAE [3] [7]. To accelerate this global footprint, CEO Apaksh Gupta noted the company is actively looking to make acquisitions in Dubai and other markets, preferring to buy smaller companies to enter new regions faster rather than building from scratch [2] [7].
