## 8. Strategic Recommendations & Information Gaps
To transition from a boutique agency to a scalable enterprise, OnlineInfatuation should:
1. **Pivot from Discounting to Value:** Heavy discounting (BOGO offers) risks commoditizing their services [1]. The agency should publish data-backed case studies with clear ROI metrics to justify premium pricing.
2. **Geographic Expansion:** Establish a presence in Tier-1 tech hubs (Bengaluru, Hyderabad) to capture higher-ticket SaaS and IT clients.
3. **Financial Transparency:** For future strategic partnerships or M&A, the company must bridge its current information gaps by standardizing and reporting its revenue mix, CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost), and LTV (Lifetime Value).
