## 7. Risks & Red Flags

**1. Trademark & IP Vulnerability (Critical)**
* **"Slice" Conflict:** PepsiCo owns the "Slice" brand for mango drinks in India [5]. Launching a fruit-based brand named "Orange Slice" invites immediate litigation from a multinational incumbent.
* **Genericness:** "Orange Slice" is descriptive. Trademarking it for Class 29 (Dried Fruits) or Class 30 (Confectionery) is legally difficult.
* **Existing Marks:** The mark "O R A N G E" is already registered in Class 30 by Sanjeev Bhandari (since 2002) [14].

**2. Search Engine Optimization (SEO) Nightmare**
* Any search for "Orange Slice" is dominated by the US AI startup (funding news) or generic recipes. A new consumer brand would face exorbitant costs to rank for its own name.

**3. Low Traction Signals**
