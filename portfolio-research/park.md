# Park+ — India’s Fast‑Growing Auto‑Tech Super‑App: Funding, Foothold & Future Play‑books (2024‑2026)

## Executive Summary
Park+ has rapidly evolved from a corporate parking solution into India’s premier "super-app" for car owners. Backed by approximately $55 million in venture capital, the platform serves around 6 million registered car owners and generates ₹131 crore in annual revenue [1] [2] [3]. By bundling parking discovery, FASTag management, insurance, and AI-driven access control, Park+ has built a robust data ecosystem. This report outlines the company's growth levers, competitive moat, and strategic trajectory in the Indian auto-tech market.

## Company Overview: The Auto-Tech Super-App
Park+ operates as a comprehensive mobile platform designed to solve the daily friction points of car ownership in India. 

### Core Products and Value Proposition
The company's product suite includes parking discovery and booking, FASTag purchases and recharges, e-challan tracking and payments, car insurance renewals, valet services, and EV charging discovery [4] [2]. For residential and commercial real estate, Park+ provides automated access control systems using boom barriers and ANPR (Automatic Number Plate Recognition) technology [1] [5]. Its value proposition lies in digitizing the entire car-ownership lifecycle, transitioning users toward cashless payments and AI-optimized mobility [6] [3].

## Founding Team and Leadership
The leadership team brings deep expertise in the Indian fintech and consumer internet sectors:
* **Amit Lakhotia (Founder & CEO):** A seasoned executive who previously served as Vice President at Paytm Payments and held roles at MakeMyTrip and Tokopedia [2] [7].
* **Hitesh Gupta (Co-Founder & CTO):** Elevated to co-founder in 2023, Gupta previously worked as the head of engineering for payments at Paytm [8] [9].
* **Siva Chetan (Co-Founder):** Recognized as a co-founder involved in the company's early establishment [10].

## Market Position and Competitive Landscape
The Indian auto-tech and parking management market is highly fragmented. Park+ differentiates itself by offering an integrated ecosystem rather than a single-point solution.

| Competitor | Core Focus | Differentiation vs. Park+ |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Park+** | Auto-tech super-app | Combines FASTag, parking, insurance, and society access control [4] [2]. |
| **GetMyParking** | Smart parking SaaS | Strictly focused on interoperable parking technology [15]. |
| **MyGate / NoBrokerHood** | Society management | Focuses on visitor logs and community payments, lacking deep vehicle-centric fintech integrations [16]. |

*Takeaway:* Park+'s moat is its multi-service ecosystem. By partnering with existing players (like malls, fuel retailers, and even MyGate) rather than purely disrupting them, Park+ embeds itself deeply into the urban mobility infrastructure [11].

## Recent News and Milestones (2024-2026)
Park+ has achieved significant operational milestones recently, heavily leveraging AI and strategic partnerships:
* **Maha Kumbh 2025 Deployment:** Park+ served as the official parking partner for the Maha Kumbh Mela, deploying an AI-powered parking system across 30+ zones. The system handled over 5 lakh vehicles, reduced wait times by 91%, and enabled FASTag payments for seamless entry [6] [17] [1].
* **Strategic Partnerships:** The company partnered with Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL) to offer fuel discounts, positioning itself as a major online fuel distributor [6] [1]. It also expanded its review platform, hosting over 5 lakh user-generated car reviews [1].

## Business Model and Traction
Park+ monetizes through a mix of B2B SaaS fees (access control systems), transaction fees (FASTag, parking), and commissions (insurance, car reselling partnerships with Tata AIG, Cars24, and Spinny) [1]. 

### Key Performance Metrics
* **Revenue:** Posted ₹131 crore in revenue for FY24 [1].
* **User Base:** Hosts a community of approximately 6 million registered car owners [11] [2].
* **Infrastructure:** Its access control business spans over 15,000 locations, processing more than 2 crore daily barrier movements [1].

### Information Gaps and Risks
While top-line revenue and user counts are public, there is a lack of granular financial data. **Information Gaps:** Specific metrics such as Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) from SaaS, Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC), Lifetime Value (LTV), churn rates, and exact profitability margins are not publicly disclosed. Furthermore, the identities of the current CFO or COO are not widely publicized. **Risks:** The company faces potential reputation hazards, as evidenced by isolated user complaints regarding the transparency of its "Free Petrol" reward systems [18]. Conducting an independent audit of loyalty programs is recommended to safeguard brand trust.
