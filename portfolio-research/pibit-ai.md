# Pibit AI 2025-2026 Deep-Dive: AI-Powered Underwriting Platform Scaling from Silicon Valley to India

## Executive Summary

Pibit.AI is an insurtech startup that leverages agentic artificial intelligence to automate and centralize commercial insurance underwriting. Operating with a dual presence in San Francisco and Bengaluru, the company addresses the industry's talent shortage and manual processing bottlenecks. 
* **Rapid Efficiency Gains:** The platform delivers up to 85% faster underwriting cycles and a 32% increase in gross written premium (GWP) per underwriter [1] [2].
* **Recent Capital Injection:** Pibit.AI secured a $7 million Series A in November 2025 led by Stellaris Venture Partners, bringing total funding to approximately $7.5 million [2] [3].
* **Strategic Positioning:** By framing AI as an augmentation tool rather than a replacement for underwriters, Pibit.AI successfully navigates the industry's talent crisis narrative [4] [5].
* **Information Gaps:** While performance metrics are heavily publicized, the company's actual Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR), exact pricing model, and profitability remain undisclosed [2] [3].

## Company Snapshot - Product & Value Proposition

Founded in 2020, Pibit.AI provides the CURE™ (Centralized Underwriting Risk Environment) platform, designed for commercial insurance carriers and Managing General Agents (MGAs) [1]. The platform converts raw submissions into decision-ready outcomes by automating intake, triage, and data enrichment from documents and external sources [1]. 

The CURE platform is modular, featuring specific components such as ClearCURE for triage, DocumentCURE for document intelligence, ResearchCURE for data enrichment, RiskCURE for portfolio evaluation, and WorkflowCURE for orchestration [5]. By unifying these workflows, Pibit.AI allows underwriters to focus on complex risk assessment rather than manual data entry [5] [3].
