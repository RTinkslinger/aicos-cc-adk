# Potpie AI – Building the Enterprise-Ready Context Layer for AI-Driven Software Development

## Executive Summary
Potpie AI is a foundational context layer that enables AI agents to operate safely and effectively within large, complex enterprise codebases [1]. While headquartered in the San Francisco Bay Area, the company has deep Indian roots, driven by Indian-origin founders and a growing engineering hub in Bengaluru [2] [3]. By converting entire codebases into structured knowledge graphs, Potpie solves the critical "context gap" that prevents large enterprises from deploying AI agents at scale [4] [1]. With early traction among Fortune 500 companies and a recent $2.2 million pre-seed round, Potpie is positioned as a critical infrastructure layer for AI-first engineering [4] [1].

## Product & Service Overview
Potpie provides a spec-driven development platform that builds custom AI agents deeply aware of an organization's entire engineering ecosystem [2] [5]. 
* **Knowledge Graph Architecture:** The platform converts source code, tickets, logs, documentation, and code reviews into a Neo4j-based knowledge graph [1]. This allows AI agents to reason across entire systems rather than just individual files [1].
* **Enterprise Integrations:** Potpie integrates seamlessly with existing developer stacks, including GitHub, Slack, Notion, Datadog, Snowflake, and Jira [5].
* **Open Source Core:** The company maintains a strong open-source presence, with its core projects surpassing 5,100 stars on GitHub [1] [5].

## Founding Team & Talent Footprint
Potpie was founded in 2023 by Aditi Kothari (CEO) and Dhiren Mathur (CTO) [4] [1]. Both founders bring significant experience from scaling complex systems: Kothari previously worked as a product manager at the Indian fintech unicorn Razorpay, while Mathur spent four years building scalable microservices at Cisco Webex [1]. 

While the company is headquartered in California, it maintains a strong operational and talent footprint in India. Recent job listings indicate active hiring for backend engineers, site reliability engineers, and developer relations roles based in Bengaluru, offering competitive salaries ranging from ₹20L to ₹35L [3].

## Market Position & Competitive Landscape
Potpie operates in the rapidly expanding AI agent platform market, which research suggests will be worth almost $24 billion by 2029, growing at over 40% annually [4]. While competitors like OpenAI and Anthropic focus heavily on code generation speed, Potpie differentiates itself by focusing on *context* and system-level understanding for codebases exceeding one million lines of code [4] [1]. 

## Customer Adoption & Case Studies
The company has successfully onboarded eight large enterprises across regulated sectors such as healthcare and fintech [4]. The ROI for these early adopters has been significant:
* One enterprise customer with a codebase exceeding 40 million lines reduced root cause analysis for production issues from nearly a week to roughly 30 minutes [1].
* Another customer maintaining decades-old hardware-integrated systems compressed multi-sprint test generation into a fraction of the original timeline [1].
