# ProsParity: Fueling India's EV-Financing Gap with a Full-Stack Digital Platform

## Executive Summary
ProsParity is a Mumbai-based fintech startup founded in 2023 that operates a full-stack digital financing platform dedicated to India's rapidly growing electric mobility sector [1]. By acting as a bridge between capital providers and the EV ecosystem, ProsParity enables the seamless flow of credit from lenders to individuals, fleet operators, dealerships, and Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs) [2] [1]. Having secured $2 million in early-stage funding and generating initial revenue traction of ₹1.04Cr by early 2025, the company is strategically positioned to address the massive credit gap in India's transition to sustainable transportation [1]. 

## Product and Business Model
ProsParity functions as an end-to-end electric mobility financing platform designed to aggregate financing demand on a single unified interface [3]. The core business model revolves around easing access to financing relationships for EV dealers and OEMs, thereby enabling them to offer seamless credit options to their end-customers [3]. 

For lenders, ProsParity's proprietary origination approach focuses on compliance, scalability, and agility, allowing traditional financial institutions to safely channelize their capital into the emerging EV asset class [3]. By digitizing the credit flow and enhancing risk management, the platform reduces the friction typically associated with alternative asset lending [4].

## Funding History
In May 2024, ProsParity successfully raised $2 million in a pre-seed/seed funding round [1] [3]. The round attracted significant backing from global institutional investors, including BEENEXT, Sparrow Capital, All In Capital, DeVC, and Huddle Ventures [3]. Furthermore, the round saw participation from prominent angel investors such as Dr. Ashish Gupta (co-founder of Junglee), Raj Dugar, Dhyanesh Shah, Abhishek Goyal (co-founder of Tracxn), and Nitin Kaushal [3]. 

The capital was earmarked for building the platform's proof of concept, enhancing technology, and expanding origination networks, with an initial geographic focus on central India [3]. Despite the institutional investment, the founders maintain a strong controlling stake of 68.19%, while funds hold 22.71% and the ESOP pool accounts for 8.00% [1].

## Recent News and Partnerships
A critical component of ProsParity's growth strategy is building a comprehensive partnership network across the EV supply chain [3]. In March 2025, it was announced that ProsParity entered into a strategic partnership with Manba Finance [1]. According to Manba Finance, this alliance with ProsParity has been instrumental in helping them "digitise credit flow and enhance risk management" to expand their vehicle financing and EV adoption initiatives [4]. This integration validates ProsParity's technology stack and demonstrates its ability to act as a critical infrastructure layer for established non-banking financial companies (NBFCs) looking to penetrate the EV market.
