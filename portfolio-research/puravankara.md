# Puravankara Limited 2024-26 Playbook: Growth Engines, Market Position & Risks for India's Largest Residential Builder

## Executive Summary
* **Pre-sales acceleration fuels revenue surge:** Pre-sales jumped 90% YoY to ₹5,914 Cr in FY24, driving a 61% YoY rise in operating revenue to ₹2,260 Cr [1].
* **Average realization is rising despite volume slowdown:** Realisation per sq ft rose 12% YoY to ₹9,500 psf in Q3 FY26, while sales-area growth slowed to 4% YoY [2].
* **Acquisition-driven GDV pipeline:** The company added over 12.76 msft of potential developable area in 9M FY26 with an estimated Gross Development Value (GDV) of approximately ₹13,900 Cr [2].

## 1. Company Overview & Value Proposition
Founded in 1975 and headquartered in Bengaluru, Puravankara Limited is one of India's leading publicly-held real estate developers [3] [4]. The company designs, develops, constructs, and markets residential and commercial properties [5]. Puravankara operates through three distinct residential brands catering to different market segments: Puravankara Limited (premium), Provident Housing Limited (mid-income), and Purva Land (plotted development) [2]. Additionally, its wholly-owned subsidiary, Starworth Infrastructure and Construction Limited (SICL), focuses on technology-enabled construction solutions [2].

## 2. Founding Team & Leadership DNA
The leadership team combines decades of real estate experience with strategic expansion capabilities:
* **Ravi Puravankara:** Founder and Executive Chairman, established the company in 1975 [6] [4].
* **Ashish Puravankara:** Managing Director and Whole Time Director, driving the company's modern expansion and premium brand pipeline [6] [4].
* **Nani R Choksey:** Vice-Chairman [4].

* **Neeraj Kumar Gautam:** Chief Financial Officer [6].

## 3. Funding History & Capital Structure

## 5. Recent Milestones (2024-2026)
* **FY24 Record Performance:** Achieved highest-ever sales value of ₹5,914 Cr (up 90% YoY) and customer collections of ₹3,609 Cr [1].
* **Q3 FY26 Growth:** Reported pre-sales of ₹1,414 Cr (17% YoY growth) and collections of ₹1,140 Cr (22% YoY growth) [2].
* **Strategic Acquisitions (9M FY26):** Acquired a 53.5-acre land parcel in Anekal Taluk, Bengaluru, with a GDV potential of ~₹4,800 crores. Total acquisitions in 9M FY26 added 12.76 msft of developable area with an estimated GDV of ₹13,900 Cr [2].

## 6. Business Model, Revenue Streams & Traction
Puravankara generates revenue primarily through the sale of residential and commercial units, supplemented by land-share arrangements. In FY24, the Puravankara brand contributed 47% of sales, Provident 35%, and Purva Land 19% [1]. The company maintains strong cash flow visibility, with balance receivables from sold units covering approximately 71% of the balance cost to complete inventory open for sale [1].
