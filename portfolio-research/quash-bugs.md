# Quash Bugs: AI-Powered Mobile QA Startup Research Report

## Executive Summary

Quash (operating legally as Brisk Labs Private Limited) is a Bengaluru-based artificial intelligence startup that is transforming mobile application testing through autonomous Quality Assurance (QA) agents [1] [2]. Founded in 2023, the company replaces traditional, brittle testing scripts with an intent-driven, plain-language AI platform that adapts to user interface changes automatically [3]. Having secured $635K in pre-seed funding led by Arali Ventures, Quash is positioned to capitalize on the growing demand for scriptless testing solutions, claiming to reduce manual testing effort by 85% while significantly increasing test coverage [4] [2]. 

## Company Overview & Founding Team

Headquartered in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India, Quash was founded in 2023 by a technical and product-focused founding trio [1] [5]. The leadership team consists of:
* **Ayush Shrivastava (CEO & Founder):** Leads the company's strategic vision and technical direction [6] [2].
* **Ameer Hamza (Co-Founder, Product):** Drives product development and user experience [6] [2].
* **Prakhar Shakya (Co-Founder, Growth):** Manages growth, marketing, and community engagement [6] [2].

The company operates with a lean team of 1-10 employees and is deeply focused on building a developer-centric culture [5] [7]. 

## Product and Service Offerings

Quash provides an AI-driven platform that automates mobile app testing by generating and executing test cases without the need for traditional coding scripts [5] [3]. 

The core offerings include:
* **Quash Automate:** An intent-driven mobile testing tool where users write tests in plain language. The AI agent handles tapping, scrolling, and navigation, utilizing built-in "self-healing" capabilities to adapt when the app's UI changes [5] [3]. It supports real devices, cloud devices, and local emulators [3].
* **Quash Report / Quash MAX:** An open-source in-app bug reporting tool that captures bugs during QA with session replays, logs, and AI-driven fix suggestions [5] [7].

The company claims its platform delivers an **87% increase in test coverage**, catches **4X more edge cases**, enables **25X faster test suite creation**, and results in **85% less manual testing effort** [4].

## Business Model

Quash operates a B2B SaaS business model targeting mobile engineering and QA teams. While specific pricing tiers are not publicly detailed, the company utilizes a product-led growth strategy anchored by its open-source offerings [7]. By releasing Quash MAX on GitHub and fostering a community of mobile developers called "Appstronauts" (used by over 11 companies), Quash drives top-of-funnel awareness [7] [8]. The commercial monetization relies on enterprise adoption of its autonomous AI agents for end-to-end testing pipelines [7].
