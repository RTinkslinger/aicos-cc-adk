# RapidClaims — AI-Powered Revenue-Cycle Intelligence poised to Disrupt Healthcare Billing

## Executive Summary

RapidClaims is an AI-driven revenue cycle management (RCM) platform that automates medical coding and claim submissions to help healthcare providers reduce administrative costs and accelerate cash flow [1] [2]. Founded in 2023, the company recently secured an $11 million funding round led by Accel, valuing the startup at $30 million [3] [4]. By leveraging proprietary few-shot learning technology, RapidClaims achieves over 95% coding accuracy and reduces accounts receivable (A/R) timelines from 20 days to just 5 days [5]. With a usage-based business model and a founding team blending deep clinical and technical expertise, RapidClaims is well-positioned to capture a significant share of the multi-billion dollar healthcare billing market.

## Company Snapshot

### RapidClaims at a Glance
RapidClaims operates as a Series A startup focused on solving mid-revenue cycle inefficiencies for hospitals and physician groups [6] [5]. While targeting the massive US healthcare market, the company maintains strong ties to the Indian startup ecosystem through its investor base and talent pool [4] [7]. The company currently generates an annualized recurring revenue (ARR) of under $5 million but has demonstrated a sixfold growth rate over the past year [3] [4].

## Product & Technology

### AI-Powered Claims Engine
RapidClaims utilizes artificial intelligence to prevent billing mistakes that lead to payment delays [9]. The platform integrates with over 15 electronic health record (EHR) systems and covers more than 25 medical specialties [4] [9]. 

| Product Module | Core Function | Performance Metric |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **RapidCode** | Autonomous medical coding | 98% accuracy in specialties like radiology [4] |
| **RapidCDI** | Clinical documentation improvement | Scans 50+ patient documents for risk-adjusted coding [4] |
| **RapidScrub** | Pre-submission claim scrubbing | Reduces code-based denials by up to 70% [4] [8] |

*Takeaway: RapidClaims differentiates itself through "few-shot learning," requiring only 100-500 patient records to onboard a new specialty, compared to the industry standard of 50,000+ records, allowing for implementation in just hours [5].*

## Business Model & Pricing

### Per-Chart Pay-As-You-Go
RapidClaims employs a usage-based pricing model where healthcare providers are charged for each chart reviewed [8]. This per-patient fee structure lowers the barrier to entry for hospitals and allows RapidClaims to scale its revenue directly in line with a provider's patient volume [8]. This model has proven highly effective, driving a 6x increase in ARR within a 12-month period [4].

## Market Position & Landscape

### Solving the $250B Inefficiency
The US healthcare system loses approximately $250 billion annually due to inefficient revenue cycle management [4] [8]. Industry data indicates that 40% of claim denials stem from coding errors, while another 35% are rejected due to late submissions [8]. Traditional RCM tools are often bogged down by outdated software and long integration cycles [5]. RapidClaims positions itself as a faster, more accurate alternative that can cut hospital administrative costs by up to 70% [4] [9].
