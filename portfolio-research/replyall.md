# ReplyAll (formerly PRBT): The Rise of Bengaluru's "All-Day" Apparel Brand & Its $750K Seed Playbook

## Executive Summary

ReplyAll is a Bengaluru-based direct-to-consumer (D2C) apparel startup founded in 2025 that specializes in "all-day clothing" designed for urban professionals and commuters [1] [2]. Operating in the retail apparel and fashion sector, the company has quickly gained traction by engineering proprietary performance fabrics that blend comfort, style, and functionality [1] [2]. Backed by a recent $750K seed funding round led by Sparrow Capital and Antler India, ReplyAll is positioning itself as a premium, utility-driven menswear brand tailored for the modern Indian demographic [3] [4]. 

## Founding Team & Origins

ReplyAll was founded in 2025 by Rushabh Sanghavi, who serves as the primary public face and driving force behind the brand [1] [5]. The company maintains a lean core team of 2-10 employees headquartered in Bengaluru, Karnataka [1]. Key team members listed alongside Sanghavi include Nishchal Khurana, Nehal Misra, and Aditya Iyer [1]. The brand was built on the philosophy that "comfort is table stakes, function is non-negotiable, and style is a side effect," targeting individuals who live life in motion without wanting to change outfits between work, errands, and social events [1] [2].

## Product & Service: The "All-Day" Wardrobe

ReplyAll's product catalog includes topwear, bottomwear, and outerwear, featuring specific items like the "Field Day Overshirt," "All In Joggers," "Stepwise Tee," and "No Sweat Shorts" [2]. The core differentiator is their proprietary "Fabric Library," which engineers textiles to handle city environments, heat, and humidity [2]. 

| Fabric Technology | Key Characteristics | Primary Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **WeatherWise** | Warm, breathable, weatherproof without bulk | Outerwear / Jackets [2] |
| **UrbanWeave** | Plain-woven nylon, structured, cool touch | Utility wear [2] |
| **FlexKnit** | Warp-knit performance, 4-way stretch, shape retention | Active/Commuter wear [2] |
| **ZephyrMax** | Structured cotton exterior, Coolmax® interior | Heat management [2] |
| **TransitWeave** | Light, structured, quick-drying | Travel and motion [2] |
| **TemperForm** | Engineered to drop skin temperature by 3°C | High-heat environments [2] |

*These proprietary fabrics allow ReplyAll to command premium pricing, with items like the Field Day Overshirt retailing at ₹5,999 and All In Joggers at ₹3,999 [2].*

## Business Model & Market Position

ReplyAll operates on a Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) e-commerce business model, selling exclusively through its website (replyall.in) [1] [2]. The brand utilizes digital marketing, offering a "WELCOME10" discount code for first-time buyers and building an email subscriber base for exclusive drops [2]. 

In the competitive Indian apparel market, ReplyAll positions itself in the premium-utility niche. Rather than competing on deep discounts, it competes on fabric technology and lifestyle integration, specifically targeting the "modern Indian man" who requires versatile clothing for a continuum of daily activities [2] [4]. 

## Recent News & Milestones

The most significant recent milestone for ReplyAll was the announcement of its ₹6.8 Crore ($750K) seed funding round in late 2025 [6] [3]. This funding event generated notable media coverage across startup and venture capital networks, highlighting the brand's rapid ascent since its founding earlier that same year [3] [4]. The company has also successfully launched its "Drop 01: Your New Uniform Awaits" collection, signaling its transition from product development to active commercial scaling [2].
