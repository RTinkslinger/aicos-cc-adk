# Revenoid AI – How an Indian‑Founded, US‑Based Startup Is Re‑Defining Enterprise Sales Intelligence

## Executive Summary
Revenoid (formerly Evabot) is an AI-powered sales intelligence and execution platform founded in 2024 [1]. While headquartered in the United States (San Francisco/Carrollton, CA), the company is driven by a founding team with deep Indian roots and enterprise experience [2] [1]. Revenoid positions itself as a "System of Intelligence" for go-to-market teams, utilizing AI agents to automate account research, identify strategic pain points, and execute personalized outreach [3] [4]. Backed by $3M in seed funding, the company is targeting enterprise sales teams by promising a 25% increase in pipeline generation without adding headcount [4] [1].

## Product & Technology
Revenoid's core offering centers on "agentic sales execution," moving beyond simple data aggregation to full-cycle automation [5]. 

* **Pipeline Generation Engine:** The platform deploys AI agents that research accounts and write personalized messages 24/7, claiming to deliver 25% more pipeline without requiring additional headcount [4].
* **The 3P Framework:** Launched in October 2025, this proprietary framework translates buying signals into strategic outreach based on "Pain, Persona, and Proof" [6].
* **Security and Compliance:** To appeal to enterprise buyers, Revenoid maintains SOC 2 Type 2 certification and GDPR compliance [3].

## Founding Team & Advisory Board
Revenoid's leadership combines Indian engineering talent with global enterprise sales expertise:
* **Rabi Gupta (CEO & Co-founder):** Previously built and sold iCouchApp (which amassed over 1.5M active users) [2].
* **Satwick Saxena (CMO & Co-founder):** Former AVP of Digital Media at Star India (now Disney) [2].
* **Ashish Gupta (CTO & Co-founder):** Brings deep technical experience, having previously founded iCouchApp [7] [2].
* **Akshay Gupta (COO):** Operational leader with past experience at iCouchApp and Wipro [2].

The team is supported by advisors like Jim Dicso (Enterprise sales lead) and Rob Salmon (former President of NetApp) [7].

## Recent News & Milestones
* **October 2025:** Revenoid officially introduced its "3P Framework" to help sales teams translate raw data signals into strategic buyer moves [6].
* **Mid-2025:** CEO Rabi Gupta announced the launch of the industry's first "agentic sales execution" platform, describing it as "lovable for enterprise sales teams" [5].
* **July 2025:** Closed a $750K pre-seed funding tranche to accelerate product development [8].

## Business Model & Pricing
Revenoid operates on a B2B SaaS model targeting mid-market and enterprise companies (11-50 employees internally, but selling to larger orgs) [8]. The platform integrates directly into existing CRM and marketing automation workflows, providing data cleaning, enrichment, and lead qualification [9]. 

## Risks, Challenges & Mitigation
While the AI capabilities are strong, user reviews on platforms like G2 highlight potential friction points. Users appreciate the multi-source research (financial statements, news, social media) but have noted complexities in the user experience [9]. To mitigate churn, Revenoid must prioritize UX/UI improvements and ensure its AI agents seamlessly integrate into existing enterprise workflows without requiring steep learning curves.

## Strategic Recommendations & Next Steps
1. **Leverage Indian Roots for APAC Expansion:** Utilize the founders' strong networks in India (e.g., former Star India/Wipro connections) to target the booming Indian SaaS and enterprise market.
2. **Validate ROI Claims:** Publish audited case studies proving the "25% more pipeline" claim to accelerate enterprise procurement cycles [4].
3. **Prepare for Series A:** With $3M in seed capital deployed, Revenoid should target a Series A raise by late 2026 to outpace competitors like Tactic.
