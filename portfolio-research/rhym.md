# Rhym: Company Profile & Strategic Research Report

## Executive Summary
Rhym (legally incorporated as Orionwave Private Limited) is a Bengaluru-based seed-stage startup that provides cloud-based gamified marketing solutions for e-commerce businesses and digital brands [1]. Founded by a team with extensive consumer tech experience, the company enables marketers to create interactive content—such as games, quizzes, and product recommendations—without requiring coding expertise [1] [2]. Having secured $600K in a Seed funding round in January 2024, Rhym is positioning itself as a cost-effective, high-engagement alternative to traditional digital advertising formats [1]. With a recent push towards GDPR compliance and a growing presence on platforms like the Shopify App Store and AppSumo, Rhym is actively targeting the SMB and mid-market D2C sectors to drive lead generation and sales conversions [3] [2].

## Product and Service
Rhym operates as a digital platform that transforms brand engagement through interactive, gamified content [4]. The platform features a drag-and-drop editor that allows users to build gamified campaigns, email popups, discount wheels, and quizzes in minutes [1] [3]. 

Key product capabilities include:
* **Game Templates:** Access to various ready-made templates, including high-performing formats like "Claw Machine," "Spin-the-Wheel," and "Memory Match" [2].
* **Integrations:** Native integrations with major marketing and analytics tools, including Mailchimp, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and Klaviyo [3] [2].
* **Deployment:** Content can be published via website plugins, Shopify apps, and social media ads [1] [3].
* **Customization:** Features include custom brand kits, CNAME support for branded URLs, and real-time user analytics [3] [2].

## Founding Team
Rhym was founded by a trio of experienced consumer tech professionals:
* **Ekta Singh (CEO & Co-Founder):** Leads the company's strategic vision and operations [1].
* **Gaurav Wadhwani (Co-Founder):** Brings expertise in building consumer and AI-led products, having previously led the product and AI division at Khatabook, scaling it to 8M monthly active users [1] [2].
* **Gaurav Toora (Co-Founder):** Instrumental in the product development and technical execution of the platform [1].

The broader team consists of approximately 10 members, including developers, designers, and marketing personnel based in Karnataka, India [2].

## Market Position
In the highly fragmented interactive advertising market, Rhym ranks 21st out of 131 active competitors tracked by Tracxn [1]. Its top competitors include more heavily funded entities like KERV, Moviebook, and DeepMarkit [1]. Rhym differentiates itself by targeting small-to-medium e-commerce brands that lack the budget for custom agency-developed interactive content, offering a low-barrier entry point through platforms like Shopify [3] [2].

## Recent News
Rhym has rolled out several significant updates to enhance its platform's appeal to a global audience:
* **GDPR Compliance (June 2025):** The platform became fully GDPR-compliant, allowing users to define custom data storage regions (such as Mumbai, India) to ensure transparency and trust for EU-targeted campaigns [2].
* **AppSumo Launch (February 2026):** Rhym launched an exclusive lifetime deal on AppSumo to rapidly acquire users and generate marketplace visibility [2].
* **Feature Expansions (Late 2024 - 2025):** Introduced new game templates, account-level CNAME capabilities, and team member edit access to facilitate agency collaboration [2].

## Business Model
Rhym operates on a SaaS (Software as a Service) subscription model, primarily billing users based on monthly engagement volume. 

| Tier | Pricing | Monthly Engagements | Key Features |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Free | $0 / month | 50 | Real-time analytics |
| PRO | $49 / month | 1,500 | Integrations (Mailchimp, Pixel) |
| PRO PLUS | $109 / month | 3,000 | Brand Kit, Music |
| PREMIUM | $249 / month | 7,500 | Full feature access |

*Note: Pricing is based on the Shopify App Store listings [3].*

Additionally, Rhym has utilized lifetime license deals via AppSumo (ranging from 5,000 to 200,000 monthly clicks) to inject short-term cash flow and rapidly expand its user base [2].
