## 1. Company Overview & Value Proposition
Founded in 2020, ROSHI provides a real-time loan bidding and matching platform that connects borrowers with licensed moneylenders and banks [1] [4]. The platform's core value proposition centers on transparency, simplicity, and AI-driven personalization. By utilizing an automated application module, borrowers can access a personalized dashboard, receive pre-approved loan offers in real-time, and chat directly with loan managers [1] [2]. ROSHI strictly lists banks regulated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) and licensed moneylenders regulated by Singapore's Ministry of Law [5].

## 2. Founding Team
ROSHI is a founder-led business driven by Amir Nada (Founder and CEO) and Said Betmurzaev [5] [6]. The two founders met during the Antler startup accelerator in Ho Chi Minh City [6]. Drawing from personal experiences with hidden fees and complex loan terms, they established ROSHI to foster financial inclusion and bring clarity to the borrowing process in Southeast Asia [6].
