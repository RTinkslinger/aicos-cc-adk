# ScreenRoot—India’s Mid-Market UI/UX Powerhouse and Its Growth Playbook (2024-26)

## Executive Summary
ScreenRoot is a mature, Goa-based UI/UX design agency with over 16 years of experience delivering enterprise-grade digital experiences [1]. Operating as an unlisted public company, the firm has carved out a highly credible niche in India's regulated sectors, particularly Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance (BFSI) [1] [2]. While the company boasts award-winning traction and a strong portfolio of enterprise clients, its bootstrapped nature and opaque revenue metrics present both unique operational stability and potential scaling limitations [3]. 

## Core Offerings & Value Proposition
ScreenRoot positions itself as a research-driven human-screen interaction design firm [1]. The company bridges the gap between technical feasibility and user needs by partnering with enterprises to tackle complex, ambiguous projects [4]. 

| Service Category | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **UI/UX Design** | Crafting interactive, intuitive designs for digital interfaces [5]. |
| **Enterprise App Design** | Transforming complex B2B applications into user-friendly experiences [5]. |
| **UX Audits & Testing** | Fixing critical usability issues and gaining insights into user behavior [5]. |
| **Research & Innovation** | Conducting targeted UX research, design workshops, and product innovation [5]. |
| **Next-Gen Devices** | Creating seamless experiences for emerging technologies and wearables [5]. |

*Key Takeaway:* ScreenRoot offers a full-stack design consultancy model, moving beyond basic wireframing to provide strategic, end-to-end digital transformation for complex enterprise platforms.

## Founding Team & Governance
ScreenRoot operates under the umbrella of Prototyze, a venture incubator that has spawned multiple digital-economy companies [6]. The leadership team blends deep design expertise with serial entrepreneurship.

## Market Position & Differentiation
ScreenRoot competes in the rapidly growing Indian UI/UX market against both large IT consultancies and boutique design studios. The company differentiates itself through a deep specialization in heavily regulated industries, including BFSI, Healthcare, Pharmaceuticals, and IT [1]. By successfully navigating the compliance and security requirements inherent in banking platforms, ScreenRoot commands a premium market position.

## Recent News and Milestones (2024-2026)
The 2024-2026 period has been defined by significant industry recognition, validating ScreenRoot's dominance in the financial sector:
* **Great Indian BFSI Awards 2024:** Named the "Best UI UX Design Agency" [9].
* **India Banking Summit & Awards 2024:** Awarded "UI UX Digital Agency of the Year" [1].
* **Product Recognition:** The "iris by YES BANK" app, designed by ScreenRoot, was featured in Google Play’s Best of 2023 [1].
* **Major Case Studies:** Successfully executed high-profile website redesigns for Karnataka Bank and Suryoday Bank ("A Fresh Look for the Bank of Smiles") [1] [10].

## Business Model and Traction
ScreenRoot operates on a B2B services model, generating revenue through project-based fees, multi-year retainers, and design workshops [1] [11]. 

| Client Category | Notable Customers |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Banking & Wealth** | Karnataka Bank, Suryoday Bank, YES BANK, Tata Capital, Edelweiss, Nuvama Wealth [1]. |
| **Healthcare & Corporate** | Medanta, Yum Brands, DIGO Communication Services [1]. |

*Key Takeaway:* The company has achieved strong traction with enterprise clients who praise their "partnership approach" over a traditional vendor relationship [1].
