# Sheru: Orchestrating India's Distributed Energy Future through V2G and Cloud Storage

## Executive Summary
Sheru (legal name: Seygnux Solutions Pvt Ltd) is a Delhi-based climate-tech startup transitioning India's electric vehicle (EV) ecosystem from simple transportation into a distributed grid storage network [1] [2]. By leveraging its proprietary cloud energy storage platform, Sheru allows utilities to access idle EV battery capacity on-demand, bypassing the massive capital expenditure (CAPEX) required for traditional stationary storage [3] [4]. Operating in a market that requires 74GW of energy storage by 2032 [5], Sheru differentiates itself from asset-heavy battery swapping competitors by focusing on the software and digital infrastructure layer of energy management [6] [7].

## Company Overview & Core Value Proposition
Sheru operates as a provider of cloud-based energy storage solutions, building the backend for new energy networks [1] [2]. The company's core value proposition is creating a global network of distributed batteries that minimizes transmission and distribution (T&D) losses and allows grid operators to scale virtually and pay per use [8] [4]. 

### The "Energy-as-a-Service" Product Ecosystem
Sheru's technology stack bridges the gap between e-mobility and utility power management:

| Product | Core Function | Target Audience |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **NetBat** | A bidirectional energy flow platform facilitating Vehicle-to-Grid (V2G) interactions to stabilize the grid during peak demand [7] [9]. | Utilities (DISCOMs) & Independent Power Producers [6] [7] |
| **ScaleBat** | An AI-driven "Battery Copilot" offering comprehensive tracking and optimization of battery lifecycle management [10]. | EV OEMs & Fleet Operators [10] |
| **EnergyBox** | A physical battery charging dock designed for detachable e-two wheeler batteries [2]. | Retailers & Swapping Operators [2] |

*Takeaway: Sheru's multi-layered approach allows it to capture value across hardware charging, software lifecycle management, and utility-scale grid balancing.*

## Market Position & Competitive Landscape
The Indian EV battery swapping and storage market is highly competitive, featuring heavily funded players like SUN Mobility, Battery Smart, Bounce Infinity, and RACE Energy [20] [21]. 

| Feature | Sheru | Traditional Competitors (e.g., Battery Smart, SUN Mobility) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Focus** | Software/Grid Integration (V2G) & Cloud Storage [4] [7] | Physical Battery Swapping Infrastructure [20] |
| **Asset Intensity** | Low (SaaS/Marketplace) [22] | High (Station Infrastructure & Battery Inventory) [23] |
| **Grid Interaction** | Bidirectional (V2G) [7] | Unidirectional (Grid-to-Vehicle Charging) [3] |

*Takeaway: Sheru differentiates itself by acting as the digital layer that connects mobility assets to the power grid, rather than competing purely on physical swapping station density.*

## Recent News and Milestones (2024-2026)
* **May 2023 (Foundation):** Partnered with BSES Rajdhani Power Limited (BRPL) to launch India's first vehicle-to-grid station, allowing EV batteries to support the grid during peak consumption [3].
* **May 2024:** Selected as one of 12 ventures for the prestigious Unreasonable Impact Asia Pacific programme, a strategic partnership between Unreasonable Group and Barclays [24].
* **July 2024:** Highlighted by investors for its ScaleBat platform, which integrates with ERP systems and IoT devices to act as an AI-driven "Battery Copilot" [10].
* **February 2025:** Recognized in industry reports for its NetBat platform's role in facilitating bidirectional energy flow, enhancing grid stability amidst India's rapid EV revolution [7].

## Business Model and Traction
Sheru operates a B2B SaaS and marketplace revenue model. It charges for its cloud-enabled platform that allows renewable power producers and utilities to access energy storage on-demand [22] [4].
