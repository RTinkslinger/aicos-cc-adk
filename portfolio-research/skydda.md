# Skydda Insurance Web Aggregator: Company Research Report

## Executive Summary
Skydda Insurance Web Aggregator Private Limited is a Mumbai-based insurtech startup incorporated in early 2020 [1] [2]. Operating under the official regulatory framework of the Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI), the company provides a digital marketplace for consumers to compare and purchase various insurance policies [3] [4]. By leveraging its official web aggregator license, Skydda aims to capture a segment of India's rapidly growing digital insurance market, positioning itself as a transparent intermediary between insurance carriers and policyholders. 

## Product and Service Offering
Skydda operates primarily through its digital portal, www.skydda.in [4]. As an IRDAI-approved insurance web aggregator, the company's core service is providing a third-party platform where consumers can browse, evaluate, and purchase insurance policies from multiple different carriers operating in the Indian market [3]. 

The platform functions as a digital broker, offering comparison services that help users determine the right pricing strategies and coverage options for their specific needs [3]. By aggregating data from various general and life insurance providers, Skydda simplifies the often complex process of policy selection, allowing users to compare premiums, claim settlement ratios, and coverage benefits side-by-side before making a purchasing decision.

## Founding Team and Leadership
Skydda Insurance Web Aggregator was incorporated as a private limited company with its registered office in Andheri East, Mumbai [5] [1]. The company's strategic direction and operational growth are spearheaded by its board of directors, which includes Priya Shah Shishir, Jumana Aspan Patel, and Rohit Arun Mondkar [2]. 

Rohit Mondkar plays a critical role in the organization, serving not only as a Director but also as the officially designated Principal Officer for the aggregator [2] [4]. Mondkar brings significant industry expertise to the startup, possessing over 25 years of experience in spearheading operations within the Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance (BFSI) sector [6]. 

## Funding History

Currently, there are no public disclosures regarding institutional venture capital or private equity funding rounds. The company appears to operate on a bootstrapped or closely held capital structure, funded primarily by its founders and internal revenues since its incorporation in 2020.

## Market Position
Skydda operates in a highly lucrative but intensely competitive Indian insurance market. The general insurance sector in India has seen robust growth, reaching a Gross Direct Premium Income (GDPI) of INR 2.73 Trillion in 2023, representing a 13.3% growth rate [3]. 

As an authorized web aggregator holding the certificate code IRDAI/INT/WBA/61/2021 [4], Skydda competes directly with heavily funded industry giants.

## Business Model
Skydda's business model is strictly governed by IRDAI regulations for web aggregators [3]. The company does not underwrite insurance risk or settle claims itself; rather, it acts as a mediating party between insurance companies and potential clients [3]. 

Revenue is generated primarily through commissions and lead-generation fees paid by the insurance providers for every policy sold or lead successfully converted through the Skydda portal. This model allows the company to maintain a relatively lean operational structure, focusing its capital on digital customer acquisition, platform maintenance, and regulatory compliance rather than capital-intensive risk underwriting.

## Recent News
Since its official licensing, Skydda has maintained its active status on the IRDAI's list of approved web aggregators [4]. The broader regulatory environment in which Skydda operates has seen recent developments, including IRDAI's push for wider product choices, simplified document submission for claims, and mandatory Know Your Customer (KYC) requirements for new health, motor, and travel insurance policies [3]. Skydda's ongoing operational focus remains on aligning its digital platform with these evolving compliance mandates to ensure seamless service delivery. *(Note: The Indian aggregator Skydda is distinct from Skydda.ai, a separate cybersecurity startup, and the Swedish PPE distributor of the same name).*
