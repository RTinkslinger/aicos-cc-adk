# Spintly — Wireless Mesh Access Control Disrupting India’s Fast‑Growing Smart‑Building Market

## Executive Summary
Spintly is rapidly transforming the physical security landscape by replacing hardware-heavy, wired access control systems with a mobile-first, cloud-based wireless mesh architecture. 
* **Rapid Growth:** The Indian access control market is projected to double from $588.8 million in 2024 to $1.22 billion by 2030 [1].
* **Cost Disruption:** Spintly's patented Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) mesh technology reduces installation costs by up to 80% compared to traditional wired systems [2].
* **Strong Backing:** The company recently secured an $8 million Series A led by Accel, bringing total funding to $13.9 million [3] [2].

## 1. Company Snapshot & Value Proposition
Founded in 2020 and headquartered in Goa, Spintly develops wireless access control and smart building solutions [2]. The company's product portfolio includes Bluetooth and NFC-enabled access readers (such as KREO and ZEON QR), biometric devices, and cloud-based software modules for attendance, visitor management, and video surveillance [2] [4]. 

Spintly’s core value proposition lies in its patented wireless mesh architecture, which eliminates extensive wiring, thereby reducing labor and project costs by up to 80% [2]. Crucially, the system is retrofittable; it supports legacy Wiegand and OSDP protocols, allowing customers to upgrade existing infrastructure without a complete "rip and replace" [2] [4].

## 2. Founding Team
Spintly is led by co-founders Rohin Parkar (CEO) and Malcolm D’Souza (CTO) [2] [5]. Parkar brings over 18 years of experience in wireless technology and product development, having previously built and scaled products at major tech firms including Motorola, Nokia, and Broadcom [2] [6].

## 6. Business Model, Traction, and Information Gaps
Spintly operates on a hybrid revenue model. It generates upfront revenue through the sale of hardware (access readers, controllers, smart locks) and earns recurring SaaS revenue through its cloud platform and remote management tools [7]. The company also offers white-labeling solutions for partners [4].

**Traction:** India remains Spintly’s largest market with over 500 customers. The company has also expanded internationally, securing close to 100 customers in the US and 10 to 15 customers in the Middle East [2]. 

**Information Gaps:** While customer counts and funding totals are public, there is a lack of disclosed financial metrics. Exact Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR), profit margins, and churn rates are currently unavailable in public domains [5].
