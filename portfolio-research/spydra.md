# Spydra — India’s Low‑Code Enterprise Blockchain Playbook for Asset Tokenisation

## Executive Summary
Spydra Technologies is a Bangalore-based enterprise blockchain startup that provides a low-code "Decentralization-as-a-Service" (DaaS) platform for real-world asset tokenization [1] [2]. Founded in 2022, the company enables businesses to digitize assets like equities, bonds, gold, and renewable energy on both private and public blockchain networks [3] [4] [5]. Backed by a $3 million pre-seed round and a recent strategic Series A investment from Hitachi Payment Services, Spydra is positioned to capitalize on India's growing demand for Web3, Central Bank Digital Currency (CBDC), and compliant digital asset infrastructure [2] [5]. For enterprises, Spydra drastically reduces the time-to-market for blockchain integration by solving critical bottlenecks in custody, compliance, and connectivity [6].

## Product and Service Portfolio
Spydra offers a comprehensive suite of high-availability APIs and developer tools designed to make blockchain adoption seamless for enterprises [6] [5]. The platform supports both permissioned networks (like Hyperledger Fabric) for sensitive corporate data and public chains for broader market access [1] [7]. 

| Core Offering | Key Capabilities | Target Use Case |
|---|---|---|
| **Private Network for Enterprises** | Customized governance, high privacy, lower transaction costs [1]. | Supply chain tracking, internal financial operations [1] [8]. |
| **Public Chain Tokenization** | High transparency, global participation, trustless verification [1]. | Fractional ownership of bonds, equities, and commodities [3]. |
| **Developer Tooling** | Fabric Debugger, GraphQL, Listeners, Smart Contract deployment [2]. | Rapid API integration and workflow automation for IT teams [2]. |

*Takeaway:* By offering a hybrid approach (public and private) alongside robust developer tools, Spydra allows traditional businesses to deploy blockchain solutions in weeks rather than months, saving clients like Expertia AI 3-4 months in go-to-market time [1].

## Founding Team

## Market Position
Spydra targets the intersection of enterprise IT and decentralized finance. While many tokenization projects fail due to hidden bottlenecks in custody, compliance, and connectivity, Spydra positions itself as a solution that embeds these requirements directly into its workflows [6]. The company has successfully onboarded clients across diverse sectors, including Livspace, Space Matrix, EzCred, Pitstop, and Rupify [1]. Operating primarily out of Bangalore with a secondary location in Austin, Texas, Spydra is well-positioned to serve both the Indian domestic market and global enterprises [6] [13].

## Recent News
Spydra's recent trajectory highlights its integration into mainstream financial systems:
* **Hitachi Partnership (Feb 2025):** Hitachi Payment Services invested in Spydra to co-develop solutions in Web 3.0, CBDCs, and cross-border payments. This partnership aims to leverage Spydra's blockchain capabilities to enhance security, reduce fraud, and drive financial inclusion in India's payment ecosystem [7] [5].
* **Asset Class Expansion (Jan 2026):** Spydra has actively published insights and case studies on the maturation of tokenized gold and renewable energy assets, noting that institutional investors are increasingly attracted to the transparency and ESG alignment these digital assets provide [6] [4].

## Business Model
Spydra operates on a B2B "Decentralization-as-a-Service" model [6]. By providing low-code APIs and plug-and-play infrastructure, the company monetizes through enterprise software licensing, API usage fees, and implementation support [1] [2]. This model allows Spydra to generate recurring revenue while embedding itself deeply into the operational workflows of its clients, creating high switching costs and long-term customer value.
