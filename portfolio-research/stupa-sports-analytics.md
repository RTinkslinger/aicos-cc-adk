# Stupa Sports Analytics: Scaling AI-Powered Racquet-Sport Analytics in India and Beyond

## Executive Summary
Founded in 2020, Stupa Sports Analytics is a pioneering Indian sports-technology startup that provides AI-driven data analytics, broadcasting, and event management solutions tailored for racquet sports. Following a significant Rs 280 million Pre-Series A funding round in May 2024, the company is aggressively pursuing global expansion and product enhancement. By securing strategic partnerships with major federations like Table Tennis Australia and Ultimate Table Tennis, Stupa is cementing its position as a critical infrastructure provider for modern racquet sports, transforming how athletes, coaches, and fans engage with the game.

## Company Overview: Foundations & Vision
Stupa Sports Analytics Private Limited was founded in 2020 and is headquartered in Gurgaon, Haryana, India [1] [2]. The company was established by Megha Gambhir, who serves as CEO, and her husband Deepak Malik, who serves as COO and Chief Technical Advisor [3] [1] [2]. Malik brings deep domain expertise to the venture as a former national coach of the Indian table tennis team [2]. The company's core mission is to build a category-defining, full-stack platform that combines SaaS, analytics, media, and AI to digitize the sporting experience [3].

## Product & Technology Stack
Stupa offers a comprehensive suite of digital tools designed specifically for racquet sports, currently covering Table Tennis, Badminton, Tennis, Pickleball, and Padel [3]. The platform leverages artificial intelligence and computer vision to deliver high-end broadcast and performance analytics.

| Core Product Module | Key Capabilities & Features |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Instant Decision Review** | Detects and reviews line calls and fault serves in under 15 seconds [3]. |
| **AI-based Media Highlights** | Automatically creates highlight packages and reels for social media engagement [3]. |
| **Real-time AI Stats** | Enhances live broadcasts with real-time statistics for in-play and post-match coverage [3]. |
| **Immersive Graphics** | Provides VR/AR graphics for TV broadcasts to elevate storytelling [3]. |
| **Event Operations** | End-to-end logistics, venue management, and participant coordination [3]. |

*The integration of these modules allows Stupa to serve as a one-stop technological partner for tournament organizers, replacing fragmented legacy systems.*

## Business Model & Revenue Streams
Stupa operates a hybrid B2B and B2B2C business model. Its primary revenue streams currently stem from B2B services offered to sports federations, clubs, and broadcasters, including sports production, streaming, event management, and media rights management [2]. Looking ahead, the company plans to initiate another fundraising round in 2025 to build a dedicated B2B2C segment catering directly to players, coaches, and fans, while also implementing advanced data and media rights monetization strategies [2].

## Partnerships & Market Reach
Strategic partnerships are the primary growth engine for Stupa's market penetration. The company boasts impressive operational metrics, claiming over 1,500 clubs/teams, 500+ events organized, and 10,500+ hours of streaming [3]. 
Key partnerships include:
* **Table Tennis Australia (TTA):** In August 2025, TTA announced Stupa as its partner to launch an advanced competition management platform to streamline event operations [3] [5].
* **Ultimate Table Tennis (UTT):** Stupa provides cutting-edge analytics for the Indian UTT league, turning matches into actionable insights [6] [7].
* **WTT Star Contender:** Stupa powered the WTT Star Contender events, including the Chennai 2024 tournament, focusing on grassroots analytics and live broadcast enhancements [8] [7].

## Competitive Landscape & Market Position
In the Indian sports-tech ecosystem, Stupa is positioned alongside other emerging startups like KIBI Sports and Footrax, which are also deploying AI in sports [9]. However, Stupa differentiates itself by maintaining a hyper-focus on racquet sports rather than field sports like cricket or football. By expanding its portfolio to include rapidly growing sports like pickleball and padel [3] [2], Stupa is capturing niche markets before larger, generalized sports-tech firms can establish dominance.

## Strategic Recommendations
1. **Accelerate Talent Acquisition:** Utilize the Rs 280 million funding to aggressively hire senior AI/ML engineers to maintain a competitive edge in computer vision capabilities [2].
2. **Capitalize on Emerging Sports:** Fast-track the deployment of analytics modules for Pickleball and Padel, leveraging their explosive global growth to acquire new club-level subscriptions [3] [2].
3. **Execute B2B2C Rollout:** Prioritize the development of the planned player and fan portal to diversify revenue away from purely federation-dependent contracts, creating a sticky, recurring subscription base [2].
