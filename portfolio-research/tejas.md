# Tejas (formerly Patcorn): From Raipur-Based Dry-Mix to a Vertically-Integrated Building-Materials Challenger

## Executive Summary
Tejas, formerly known as Patcorn Drymix Private Limited, is an emerging Indian startup operating in the vertically integrated building materials sector. Founded in 2023 and based in Raipur, Chhattisgarh, the company targets the top 5% of the Indian market with premium specialty construction chemicals [1] [2]. By partnering with the established Kamdhenu Group to launch "Kamdhenu Adhesive," Tejas is transitioning from a traditional dry-mix manufacturer to a full-stack retail brand that eliminates conventional dealer networks [1]. 

## Company Overview & Founding Team
Originally incorporated in November 2022 as Patcorn Drymix Private Limited, the company has rebranded its consumer-facing identity to Tejas [1] [2]. The startup is driven by a highly technical and strategic founding team. Saijal Rekhani, an IIT Bombay 2023 graduate and former Kearney consultant, brings material science expertise to the table [3]. Sailesh Rekhani, an ex-Y Combinator founder, leverages deep industry networks and strategic insights gained from interactions with major industrial players [4]. Nikhil Agrawal is also noted as a co-founder [1].

## Product & Service Portfolio
Tejas focuses on the specialty construction chemicals segment, specifically tile and stone adhesives, with plans to expand into waterproofing, plywood resins, and sealants [1]. Its flagship offering is **Kamdhenu Adhesive**, launched in partnership with the Kamdhenu Group [1]. 

**Core Adhesive SKUs**

| SKU | Type | Packaging | Key Features |
|-----|------|-----------|--------------|
| K50 | Cement-based, polymer-modified | 20 kg bag | Reliable bonding for ceramic, vitrified, and natural stone tiles [5] |
| K80 | Type 2 Premium Adhesive | 20 kg bag | High-performance grade [6] |

The products utilize imported German and Korean polymer technology, ensuring high lot consistency and superior bonding strength compared to standard market alternatives [7].

## Market Position & Business Model
Tejas is positioning itself in the $3.5 billion Indian specialty construction chemicals market [1]. 

**Business Model Highlights:**
* **Vertical Integration:** The company operates with an IP-led materials R&D wing to create cutting-edge formulations [1].
* **Direct-to-Market:** Tejas aims to eliminate traditional dealers and distributors by going "full stack retail," offering private labels from Day 0 [1].
* **Strategic Partnerships:** By co-branding with Kamdhenu Group, Tejas instantly gains market trust and leverages an established brand name in the Indian construction sector [1] [7].

## Funding History & Financial Health
Tejas is currently in its Seed stage [1]. While the company was initially bootstrapped during its incorporation as Patcorn Drymix, recent data indicates that it has secured backing from **AC Ventures**, which is listed as its sole disclosed institutional investor [8]. Exact funding amounts and valuations remain undisclosed in public filings.

## Recent News & Milestones
Between 2024 and early 2026, Tejas has focused on formalizing its corporate structure and expanding its product footprint:
* **Brand Evolution:** Successfully transitioned its market identity from Patcorn to Tejas, while maintaining Patcorn Drymix Pvt. Ltd. as the legal manufacturing entity [1] [2].
* **Regulatory Compliance:** In May 2025, the company's Board of Directors approved a comprehensive Privacy Policy aligning with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, signaling a maturation in its corporate governance [9].
* **Market Expansion:** Actively listed its Kamdhenu Adhesive products (K50 and K80) on major B2B platforms like IndiaMART to capture digital procurement demand [6] [5].
