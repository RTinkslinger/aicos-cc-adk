# Townhall (India) – The Deep-Hybrid Event Platform Redefining the Post-COVID Event Landscape

## Executive Summary
Townhall is a fast-growing, India-based event technology startup that provides a "deep-hybrid" platform for managing in-person, virtual, and hybrid events. Rebranded from Townscript in September 2024, the company has pivoted from a traditional ticketing solution to a comprehensive enterprise event suite. Backed by strategic investor BookMyShow and prominent angel investors, Townhall differentiates itself through native hybrid capabilities and AI-driven engagement tools, though its exact funding amounts remain undisclosed. 

## Company Overview and Product Value Proposition
Founded in 2021 and based in Pune and Hyderabad, India, Townhall operates as a comprehensive hybrid event management platform [1] [2]. The company officially rebranded from Townscript to Townhall in September 2024 to reflect its evolution into a unified platform that bridges the gap between in-person and virtual audiences [3]. 

Townhall’s core value proposition is its "truly hybrid-first" architecture. Unlike competitors that patch virtual streaming onto physical ticketing tools, Townhall allows organizers to manage both audiences on a single backend [4] [2]. Key features include:
* **End-to-End Ticketing:** Early bird pricing, QR code check-ins, and multi-tiered ticketing [4] [5].
* **Interactive Virtual Venues:** Real-time networking, virtual booths, live Q&A, and polls [4] [6].
* **AI-Powered Engagement:** Generative AI matchmaking for networking and an "AI Co-host" for efficient session hosting [4] [5].

## Founding Team and Leadership
Townhall is led by a team with deep experience in the Indian event-tech and SaaS ecosystems:
* **Venkateswara (Venky) Reddy K:** Co-founder and CEO, who leads the company's strategic vision and oversaw the 2024 rebranding effort [1] [6] [3].
* **Sachin Sharma:** Co-founder and Advisor. Sharma previously founded Townscript in 2012, building it into a major ticketing platform before its evolution into Townhall [6] [7].
* **Parth Dalmia:** Co-founder and Growth Head, responsible for driving enterprise adoption and market expansion [6].

## Funding History and Ownership
Townhall boasts strong backing from high-profile Indian investors, though specific financial details are closely guarded. 
* **Lead Investors:** The company is backed by BookMyShow as a strategic investor, alongside marquee angel investors Kunal Shah and Haresh Chawla [2] [8]. BookMyShow previously acquired a 75% stake in Townscript in 2017, laying the groundwork for this strategic relationship [9] [10].

## Business Model and Traction
Townhall employs a SaaS subscription model combined with transaction fees. 
* **Pricing Structure:** The "Standard" plan starts at $300 per month, which includes up to 6 streaming hours and standard support. All paid tickets incur a 2% ticketing fee (excluding payment gateway charges) [5].
* **Enterprise Upsell:** Custom pricing tiers offer unlimited attendees, priority support (2-hour response time), and advanced AI features [5].
* **Traction:** As of March 2026, the company reported an estimated annual revenue of ₹10.2Cr [1]. The platform is trusted by major brands including Salesforce and CNBC [4] [2]. 

## Recent News and Milestones (2024-2026)
* **September 2024:** Officially rebranded from Townscript to Townhall, launching an enhanced virtual and hybrid event platform [3].
* **Early 2025:** Hosted an internal 36-hour "AI Hackathon" to drive rapid prototyping and integrate new generative AI solutions into the platform [2].
* **Team Expansion:** Continued to scale its engineering and product teams in Pune, hiring senior talent from companies like Quickheal [2].
