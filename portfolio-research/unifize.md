# Unifize 2024-2026: AI-Native Quality Management Platform Scaling on $1.7M Revenue

## Executive Summary
Unifize is an AI-native, collaborative quality management platform that unifies Quality Management Systems (QMS), Product Lifecycle Management (PLM), and Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES) into a single no-code interface [1] [2]. Operating with a highly capital-efficient model, the company reached $1.7 million in revenue in 2024 with a lean team of 31 employees [3]. While headquartered in the US, Unifize maintains a critical technical center in Bangalore, India, leveraging local engineering talent to build its globally deployed platform [2].

## 1. Company Overview and Value Proposition
Founded in 2018, Unifize targets ISO and FDA-compliant companies in life sciences, medical devices, cosmetics, and advanced manufacturing [4] [2]. Traditional enterprise software separates data capture from user collaboration, leading to inefficiencies [5]. Unifize solves this by embedding chat and AI directly into workflows, creating a "collaboration-first" environment [5] [1]. Customers report significant ROI, including a 10x increase in speed to compliance, 4x faster time-to-market, and a 70% reduction in meetings [6] [7]. 

## 2. Founding Team
The leadership team brings decades of experience in regulated industries and software development:
* **Ben Merton (Co-founder & CEO):** Brings over 15 years of global leadership in regulated industries (industrial automation, life sciences, aerospace). He has led operations across the U.S., India, China, and Korea [2].
* **Lakshman Thatai (Co-founder):** Offers over 20 years of experience leading operational excellence in food processing, manufacturing, and medical devices [2].
* **Avinash Sultanpur (Co-founder & CTO):** Leads the technical architecture and engineering teams [1].

## 4. Market Position and Differentiation
Unifize competes in the broader QMS and PLM software market against legacy giants like MasterControl and Veeva, as well as integration tools like Kong and Boomi [1] [6]. 
* **Differentiation:** Unlike rigid legacy systems, Unifize offers a "blank canvas" approach with drag-and-drop simplicity, allowing users to configure workflows without IT tickets [12] [6]. Its AI-native architecture and WhatsApp-like collaborative interface provide a distinct moat [1].
