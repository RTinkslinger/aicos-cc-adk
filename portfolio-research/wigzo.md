# Wigzo Tech – Indian MarTech Trailblazer Turned Shiprocket-Backed Growth Engine (2022-2026)

## Executive Summary

Wigzo Technologies is an Indian Customer Data Platform (CDP) and marketing automation suite that has successfully transitioned from an independent startup to a core component of Shiprocket's e-commerce logistics ecosystem. Acquired in a majority stake deal in 2022, Wigzo leverages artificial intelligence to drive personalization for direct-to-consumer (D2C) brands. Despite raising only a modest $1.6M in early funding, the company achieved significant traction, reporting $11.1M in revenue and serving approximately 600 customers by 2024. The strategic integration with Shiprocket positions Wigzo uniquely at the intersection of marketing technology and fulfillment in India's booming D2C market.

## 1. Company Overview – What Wigzo Does

### 1.1 Product Suite & Value Proposition
Wigzo operates as an enterprise marketing automation suite and Customer Data Platform (CDP) designed specifically for e-commerce and D2C brands [1] [2]. The platform allows marketers to personalize communication across multiple channels—including email, push notifications, Facebook, and Google ads—from a single integrated dashboard [2]. By consolidating retail data into a single warehouse, Wigzo enables brands to run multi-channel campaigns and calculate ROI efficiently [3]. The platform offers seamless five-minute integrations with major e-commerce engines like Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, and Zapier [3].

### 1.2 Technology & AI Differentiators
Unlike traditional rule-based marketing tools, Wigzo differentiates itself as a machine-intelligence-driven personalization platform [2]. It utilizes big data, AI, and machine learning algorithms to analyze customer affinity and purchasing behavior in real-time, enabling highly tailored customer experiences that boost conversion rates [1].

## 2. Founding Team & Leadership

Wigzo was founded in 2013/2014 by a core team of technology and marketing professionals [2] [4]:
* **Umair Mohammed (Founder & CEO):** Spearheaded product and sales, bringing 10 years of experience from large internet companies in Singapore and India [4].
* **Himanshu Kaushik (Co-Founder & COO):** Brings experience in aligning technology with business strategy [5] [6].
* **Mohammad Atyab (Ex-CTO):** A key technical architect who later exited the company to found RaySuite AI [7] [8].
* **Jeremiah Cathey (Co-Founder & CRO):** Leads revenue operations [5].

## 3. Funding History & Capital Structure

Prior to its acquisition, Wigzo was highly capital-efficient, raising approximately $1.6M across early rounds [4]. ## 5. Business Model, Traction & Financials

Wigzo operates on a B2B SaaS revenue model. By 2024, the company generated $11.1M in revenue [4]. 

* **Customer Base:** The platform serves approximately 600 customers [4]. Early enterprise adopters included brands like Awok, Mr Button, and W [2]. Data indicates that at least 98 of its current customers generate $100k or more in revenue [16].
* **Team Size:** The company maintains a lean team of 31 employees, including 7 engineers and 10 marketing personnel [4].

## 6. Recent News & Milestones (2024-2026)

* **Shiprocket IPO (Dec 2025):** Parent company Shiprocket filed for an IPO to raise Rs 2,342.3 crore, reporting consolidated operating revenue of Rs 942.7 crore for H1 FY26 [17]. This public market entry solidifies the financial backing of Wigzo's parent ecosystem.
* **Founder Transitions (Nov 2025):** Former Wigzo CTO Mohammad Atyab returned to the startup scene, launching a new AI venture, RaySuite AI, marking the continued influence of Wigzo alumni in the Indian tech ecosystem [7].
