# ZuAI (Professor Curious) – India's Generative-AI EdTech Playbook: Funding, Product, and Market-Fit in 2026

## Executive Summary
ZuAI, legally registered as Zu Technologies Private Limited and recently rebranded as "Professor Curious," is a Bengaluru-based generative AI EdTech startup [1] [2]. Founded in 2021, the company provides a 24/7 AI-powered personal tutor designed primarily for AP and IB students [2] [3]. Having raised approximately $1.72 million across multiple seed rounds from prominent investors like Prime Venture Partners and 100X.VC, ZuAI has established a strong foothold in the competitive Indian EdTech landscape [4] [3]. With recent viral traction in the US market and backing from upGrad's AI incubator, ZuAI represents a compelling case study of an Indian startup successfully exporting AI-driven personalized learning [5] [6].

## Product & Service: The "Professor Curious" AI Tutor
ZuAI's flagship product, Professor Curious, functions as a real-time AI professor that explains answers step-by-step in a clear voice [7]. The platform is specifically tailored as a study platform for AP and IB students, offering personalized study plans, practice questions, and instant doubt-clearing [3]. 

A core differentiator is its interactive "@" function, which allows students to talk to the AI and instantly generate tailored outputs, including analyses, videos, diagrams, or images [8]. The system also generates custom practice sets based on individual weaknesses and provides AI-generated study materials aligned with specific exam formats [3].

## Founding Team: From FinTech to EdTech
ZuAI was founded in 2021 by Anubhav Mishra and Arpit Jain [3]. The duo brings a unique dual-industry background, having previously built ZuPay, a premier payment and investing application designed for teenagers [9]. Their experience in building consumer-facing products for Gen Z has heavily influenced ZuAI's interactive and highly engaging user interface. The founders currently operate with a lean team of 11-50 employees out of their Bengaluru headquarters [2].

## Business Model & Financials
ZuAI operates on a freemium subscription model. Users can download the app and initiate a "start free trial," after which they are prompted with a subscription page to continue accessing premium AI tutoring features [7]. Financially, the company is still in its early monetization phase, reporting an annual revenue of ₹25.1 Lakhs as of March 31, 2025 [3].
